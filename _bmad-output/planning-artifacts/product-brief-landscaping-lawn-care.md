# Product Brief: Landscaping & Lawn Care Business OS

## Executive Summary

The US landscaping services market reached $184-189 billion in 2025 with 726,000+ businesses, yet the software serving this industry forces operators into an impossible choice: use Yardbook's free tier (no dispatching, no 2FA, no iOS app) or pay $120-598/month for Jobber, Service Autopilot, or LMN. The average landscaping business has just 2 employees, 70%+ have only 1-4 workers, and 59% earn under $500K/year. These operators spend 10+ hours per week on admin, use 7-9 fragmented software tools, and cite cost (50%) and learning curve (43%) as top adoption barriers.

Meanwhile, the competitive landscape is consolidating under private equity — ServiceTitan IPO'd at $8.9B, LMN/SingleOps/Greenius merged into Granum, WorkWave acquired RealGreen — driving price increases, support quality decline, and feature stagnation. Service Autopilot users openly call it "garbage after the buyout." Jobber lacks lead CRM and reporting. No affordable platform combines satellite-based property measurement, route optimization, chemical tracking, and seasonal scheduling.

This product — **MowPro** — is the all-in-one lawn care Business OS purpose-built for solo operators and small crews (1-5 people). It integrates CRM, scheduling, dispatching, invoicing, route optimization, satellite property measurement for instant quoting, chemical application tracking, and seasonal workflow management — all for a $79-99 one-time lifetime deal, less than a single month of Jobber Connect.

The go-to-market strategy launches via AppSumo at $79 (capped at 300-500 seats), targeting the tight-knit lawn care community through LawnSite.com (dominant industry forum), r/lawncare (500K+ members), Facebook groups, and trade shows. Route optimization alone saves 2+ hours per crew per day — the killer ROI story that sells itself in forum threads. Transition to $29-39/month SaaS follows at month 6.

**Opportunity score: 97/105 — BUILD.**

## Core Vision

### Problem Statement

Solo lawn care operators and small crews (1-5 people) face a punishing three-way trade-off when choosing business management software:

1. **Free tools that lack critical features**: Yardbook offers a free tier but has no dispatching, no 2FA security, no iOS app, weak mobile experience (scored 2/5), GPS data that syncs only every 4 hours, and email-only support. LawnPro Free has glitches, hidden 2% credit card fees, nonfunctional buttons, and terrible customer service. These tools work for tracking a few customers but collapse when an operator needs to optimize routes, dispatch crews, or comply with chemical application regulations.

2. **Professional tools they can't afford**: Jobber costs $39-199/month plus $29/user. Service Autopilot runs $49-199/month with $97-247 signup fees. LMN charges $297-598/month. ServiceTitan starts at $145/user/month. For a solo operator earning $54,000-$115,000/year with 10-14% margins, spending $1,200-7,200/year on software is a non-starter — especially when these tools are designed for general field service, not lawn-care-specific workflows.

3. **Spreadsheets and cobbled-together tools**: Most small operators use 7-9 different software tools — QuickBooks for accounting, Google Calendar for scheduling, Google Maps for routing, paper forms for chemical tracking, text messages for customer communication. This fragmentation wastes 10+ hours per week on admin and creates blind spots where jobs, leads, and compliance records fall through cracks.

With 80% of landscaping companies struggling to fill positions and 72% citing labor as their biggest growth barrier, the industry desperately needs software that makes existing crews more productive. But 83% of landscaping professionals haven't adopted AI tools, and 50% cite cost as the top obstacle. The market is screaming for an affordable, lawn-care-specific, all-in-one solution.

### Problem Impact

- **Time**: Solo operators spend 10+ hours per week on administrative tasks — scheduling, invoicing, routing, quoting. That's an entire lost workday every week that could be spent servicing customers or winning new business.
- **Revenue leakage**: Without route optimization, crews drive inefficient routes that waste 2+ hours per day. With optimized routing, businesses can service 15-20% more properties with the same team and reduce routes by up to 35%.
- **Quoting delays**: Without satellite measurement tools, every quote requires an on-site visit. A solo operator spending 30 minutes driving to and measuring a property for a $50 mowing bid is losing money on the quote itself.
- **Compliance risk**: Chemical application tracking requires EPA registration numbers, applicator certifications, active ingredients, dilution rates, and application methods — stored for up to 7 years in many states. Manual tracking on paper creates audit liability and consumes up to 6 hours per technician per week.
- **Growth ceiling**: Operators who want to grow from solo to 2-3 crews hit a wall — their manual systems break, but $200+/month software feels like a leap of faith. This artificial ceiling suppresses business growth across the industry.
- **Labor crisis amplification**: With 76% of landscape firms having open positions and average labor costs at $20-25/hour, every minute of crew inefficiency has an outsized cost impact. Software that saves 2 hours/crew/day is the equivalent of hiring a part-time worker.

### Proposed Solution

A mobile-first, lawn-care-specific Business OS with six core modules:

1. **Customer CRM & Service History** — Lawn-care-specific customer profiles with property details, service history, seasonal preferences, and communication log. Lead tracking from inquiry to close. Customer portal for self-service booking and payment.

2. **Smart Scheduling & Dispatching** — Drag-and-drop calendar with seasonal templates (spring cleanup → weekly mowing → fall aeration → winter snow removal). Crew dispatching with real-time status. Bulk rescheduling for rain days. Recurring service automation.

3. **Route Optimization** — AI-powered route planning that reduces drive time by up to 35%. Dynamic rerouting for cancellations and new requests. GPS crew tracking. Visual route maps. Save 2+ hours per crew per day.

4. **Invoicing & Online Payments** — One-tap job completion → automatic invoice generation. Online payment portal for customers. QuickBooks integration. Payment tracking and reminders. Batch invoicing for recurring services.

5. **Satellite Property Measurement** — Draw property boundaries on satellite imagery to calculate square footage instantly. Generate accurate quotes without site visits. Save measurement data to customer profile. Quote in 30 seconds vs. 30 minutes for an on-site visit.

6. **Chemical Application Tracking** — Log applications with EPA registration numbers, applicator certifications, chemical names, active ingredients, dilution rates, quantities, and methods. State compliance report generation. 7-year record retention. Audit-ready documentation.

**Pricing principle**: One price, forever. $79-99 lifetime deal at launch. All features included. No per-user charges, no monthly fees, no hidden costs. Less than a single month of Jobber Connect.

**UX principle**: Built for people who mow lawns, not people who sit at desks. Mobile-first, offline-capable, designed for use in a truck between jobs. Core actions in 3 taps or fewer. Onboarding in 15 minutes.

### Key Differentiators

1. **Satellite property measurement for instant quoting** — No competitor at this price point offers integrated satellite measurement. Standalone tools like SatQuote and Deep Lawn exist but cost extra and don't connect to CRM/invoicing. MowPro's integrated measurement eliminates on-site visits for quoting, letting operators bid jobs in 30 seconds from their truck.

2. **Route optimization included, not premium** — Most affordable tools either don't include route optimization or charge extra for it. MowPro includes it at the base price, delivering the single most impactful ROI: 2+ hours saved per crew per day, 35% shorter routes, 15-20% more properties serviced.

3. **Lawn-care-specific, not generic field service** — Seasonal scheduling templates (spring → mowing → fall → snow), chemical tracking compliance, property measurement — features that generic platforms like Jobber and ServiceTitan don't understand. Built by and for lawn care, not adapted from plumbing/HVAC software.

4. **$79-99 lifetime deal vs. $1,200-7,200/year** — One-time cost less than a single month of LMN. No monthly subscription, no per-user fees, no mid-contract price increases. Directly targets the 50% who cite cost as their top adoption barrier.

5. **Chemical application compliance built in** — GorillaDesk and Spraye handle chemical tracking but are separate subscriptions. RealGreen does it but at premium pricing. MowPro integrates compliance tracking into the same platform where scheduling, routing, and invoicing happen — one system, not four.

6. **PE-proof simplicity** — While every major competitor is getting acquired, merged, and price-hiked under private equity, MowPro is bootstrapped with a lifetime deal model. No investors pushing for 25% annual price increases. No post-acquisition quality decline. The software stays simple and affordable because that's the entire business model.

Lost a $2,400 seasonal contract because he took two days to get back to the customer with a quote.

**Persona 3: "Doug the Part-Timer"**

- **Profile**: 58 years old, retired firefighter who mows lawns 3 days a week for extra income and to stay active. 18 residential customers in his neighborhood and surrounding area. Revenue: $28K/year, supplementing his pension. Not tech-savvy — his daughter set up his smartphone.

### Secondary Users

**Crew Members** — Receive daily route assignments and job details on their phones. Mark jobs as complete, add notes or photos. Their adoption of the mobile app determines whether the crew leader can track progress in real time. Must be dead-simple — these users won't tolerate a learning curve.

**Residential Customers** — Receive invoices, pay online, submit service requests, and view their service history through a customer-facing portal. Their experience with invoicing and payments directly impacts the operator's cash flow and professional image.

**Seasonal Subcontractors** — During peak seasons or for specialized services (aeration, tree removal, snow plowing), operators hire subcontractors who need job assignment visibility and simple completion tracking without full system access.

## MVP Scope

### Core Features

**1. Customer CRM & Service History** (Critical — Day 1)
- Customer profiles with property details, service preferences, and notes
- Service history log per property
- Lead tracking (inquiry → quote → customer)
- Customer contact directory with search
- Basic customer portal for invoice viewing and payment

**2. Scheduling & Dispatching** (Critical — Day 1)
- Drag-and-drop weekly/bi-weekly/monthly scheduling
- Recurring service templates (mow every Tuesday, fertilize quarterly)
- Crew assignment and dispatching
- Rain day bulk rescheduling
- Seasonal service transitions (spring → mowing → fall → snow)
- Calendar view with day/week/month toggles

**3. Route Optimization** (Critical — Day 1)
- Automatic daily route optimization using OSRM + OpenStreetMap (minimizes API costs)
- Drag-to-reorder with automatic recalculation
- Visual route map with turn-by-turn overview
- Dynamic rerouting for cancellations or additions
- GPS crew tracking on active routes
- Route efficiency metrics (distance, time, stops)

**4. Invoicing & Payments** (Critical — Day 1)
- One-tap job completion → automatic invoice generation
- Online payment portal for customers (credit card, ACH)
- Payment status tracking dashboard
- Automatic payment reminders (email/SMS)
- Batch invoicing for recurring services
- QuickBooks Online integration

**5. Satellite Property Measurement** (Critical — Day 1)
- Satellite imagery overlay (Google Maps/Mapbox)
- Polygon drawing tool to trace property boundaries, lawn areas, beds
- Automatic square footage calculation
- Save measurements to customer profile
- Generate quotes from measurements with configurable price-per-sqft rates
- Before/after comparison for re-quoting

**6. Mobile-First Responsive Design** (Critical — Day 1)
- Progressive web app optimized for mobile (iOS + Android browsers)
- Offline mode for areas with poor cell coverage
- Push notifications for new quote requests, payments, schedule changes
- Core workflows in 3 taps or fewer
- Photo capture for job completion documentation

- **Snow removal module** — Add in Phase 2 (Month 4-5) with seasonal scheduling templates
