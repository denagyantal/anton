# Product Brief: StudioSimple — Gym & Fitness Studio Management

---

## Executive Summary

**StudioSimple** is a dead-simple gym and boutique fitness studio management platform built for independent operators under 300 members. It replaces the bloated, commission-extracting incumbents (Mindbody, Glofox, PushPress) with a flat $79/month subscription that includes everything: member management, class scheduling, mobile booking app, Stripe pass-through billing, and free Mindbody data migration — no add-ons, no marketplace commissions, no long-term contracts.

The market is experiencing a once-in-a-decade disruption window. Mindbody's EGYM merger is driving migration anxiety at scale. Glofox's documented billing fraud post-cancellation ($1,300 charged after cancellation, disputes ignored) has left thousands of studio owners actively angry and vocal. PushPress' hidden processing fees (4.19% on free tier vs 2.75% on paid) are creating buyer's remorse. No current player offers the combination of flat transparent pricing + native mobile member app + zero marketplace commission + 1-hour setup.

StudioSimple targets the ~57,000 independent US gym operators, starting with the 3,970 CrossFit boxes — the tightest, highest-WTP, most word-of-mouth-driven segment in fitness. The go-to-market is community-first (CrossFit affiliate FB groups, r/crossfit, r/gymowners), followed by a Lifetime Deal launch on AppSumo as the first-ever gym management software in that category.

**Positioning:** "The gym management software that treats you like an owner, not a transaction."

**Business model:** $79/month flat (up to 300 members), LTD tiers at $69/$149/$249, Stripe pass-through processing at standard 2.9% + $0.30 with zero markup.

**12-month target:** 200–500 paying customers → $190K–$475K ARR. LTD launch: 500–2,000 buyers → $75K–$298K one-time.

## Core Vision

### Problem Statement

Independent gym and boutique fitness studio owners — CrossFit box operators, yoga studios, pilates instructors, martial arts schools — are trapped between two bad options:

1. **Overpriced, overcomplicated incumbents** — Mindbody starts at $159/month but balloons to $500–700+/month once a branded app, automation, and advanced reporting are enabled. Add the 20% marketplace discovery commission (capped at $30 per transaction) on every new client found through their app, and a gym running intro offers loses 23.5% of revenue on those sales. Onboarding takes 8 weeks. Data is held hostage for $499 per location. Glofox (now ABC Fitness) bills after cancellation — documented cases of $1,300 charged on stored cards months after cancellation with disputes ignored.

2. **Spreadsheets, Venmo, and WhatsApp** — the free "alternative" used by owners who gave up on dedicated software because it was too expensive or too complex to onboard during the 6 AM class.

Neither option is right for the 57,000 independent US gym operators, the majority of whom have 50–200 members, $150K–$600K annual revenue, and a software budget of $100–$300/month.

### Problem Impact

- A CrossFit box running 150 members at $150/month ($270K ARR) spending $267–$500/month on a fragmented software stack (PushPress Core + Grow + Train + branded app) spends $3,200–$6,000/year on tools that still don't integrate cleanly.
- A yoga studio paying Mindbody Starter ($159/month) can't afford the branded app ($599/month add-on), so members book through a browser — a friction point competitors exploit directly.
- A 60-member gym on Mindbody's basic plan can't export their member data without paying $499 — the software effectively holds their business hostage.
- PushPress' free tier charges 4.19% + $0.30 per transaction; at $30,000/month in member billing, that's $432/month in excess fees — more than the $159/month Pro plan they thought they were avoiding.
- The emotional toll: gym owners routinely describe software decisions as their single largest source of business stress, above staffing and member retention.

### Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

| Platform | Core Weakness | Real Monthly Cost |
|---|---|---|
| **Mindbody** | 20% marketplace commission + 8-week onboarding + $499 data hostage | $500–700+ (functional setup) |
| **PushPress** | Hidden 4.19% processing on free tier; fragmented (Core + Grow + Train are 3 apps) | $559 for full stack |
| **Gymdesk** | No native mobile member app; limited scheduling for complex class types | $75–200 (best value but incomplete) |
| **WellnessLiving** | AI-only support since Aug 2025; slow payment processing | $89–250 |
| **Walla** | $199–679 + branded app ($149/mo add-on); demo-only purchase process | $350–830+ |
| **Vagaro** | Built for salons/spas, not gyms; limited class management | $30–85 |
| **Wodify** | Delayed payouts; mobile app crashes; expensive ($179+/mo) | $179+ |

**The white space:** No platform currently combines flat transparent pricing under $100/month + native mobile member booking app + zero marketplace commissions + month-to-month terms + 1-hour setup. Gymdesk comes closest on pricing but lacks the mobile app — the single most-cited feature members expect and the single most common reason Gymdesk customers cite for not fully switching from Mindbody.

### Proposed Solution

StudioSimple is a fully-integrated gym management platform built from the ground up for independent operators, not stripped-down enterprise software.

**The core product:**
- **Member management** — profiles, membership types, attendance history, payment history
- **Class scheduling** — unlimited class types, recurring schedules, capacity limits, waitlists
- **Online booking** — members book classes through a mobile app (iOS + Android, white-label optional) and web
- **Billing** — Stripe pass-through (2.9% + $0.30, zero markup), automated recurring payments, failed payment retry, automated dunning emails
- **Check-in** — QR code or PIN at the door; no hardware required beyond a tablet
- **Free Mindbody migration** — import members, classes, and payment history; attacks the $499 data hostage barrier directly

**The pricing:**
- $79/month flat, all features included, up to 300 members
- No per-member fees, no marketplace commissions, no add-on modules, no setup fees
- Month-to-month, cancel anytime in 2 clicks
- LTD: $69 (50 members), $149 (150 members), $249 (300 members)

### Key Differentiators

1. **Flat pricing, all-in** — $79/month includes everything including the mobile app. PushPress charges $97/month extra for a branded app. WellnessLiving charges separately for SMS. Mindbody charges for advanced reporting. StudioSimple has one price.

2. **Zero commissions, forever** — No marketplace. No discovery fee. No percentage taken from new member sales. Your clients are yours.

3. **1-hour setup, not 8 weeks** — A step-by-step wizard designed for a gym owner who's also coaching a 6 AM class. Most setups complete during lunch.

4. **Free Mindbody data migration** — We import your member list, class schedule, and payment history. No $499 ransom fee. No "data transfer nightmare" (documented GymInsight term). This is our primary sales closer.

5. **Mobile member app included** — iOS and Android, white-label optional. Members book classes, view their schedule, and check attendance history from the app. Not a $97/month add-on.

6. **Month-to-month, no contracts** — Cancel in 2 clicks. No 12-month lock-in. No 3-month early termination penalty. This directly addresses the #1 trust barrier post-Mindbody/Glofox.

7. **CrossFit-ready (Phase 2)** — WOD delivery and member leaderboard for the CrossFit segment (must-have for that ICP; not required for yoga/pilates).

## Target Users

### Primary Users

#### Persona 1: Marcus — CrossFit Box Owner (Highest Priority ICP)

**Background:** Marcus, 38, owns a CrossFit affiliate in suburban Atlanta with 165 members. He's been coaching CrossFit for 10 years and opened his box 5 years ago. He manages the business solo — coaching 4 classes/day, handling member inquiries, and managing billing — with one part-time front desk helper on weekends.

**Current software:** PushPress Core ($79/mo) + PushPress Grow ($329/mo, basically GoHighLevel) + no branded app because he decided $97/mo extra was too much. Members book through a browser. He hates it. "It looks like my gym is from 2015 online."

**Pain:** He's paying $408/month for a fragmented experience. PushPress' reporting is broken — he can't easily see which members are at churn risk. He discovered that PushPress' free tier (that he was on before upgrading) was eating ~$300/month in excess processing fees. He joined a CrossFit affiliate owner Facebook group and saw 12 people complaining about the same thing.

**What success looks like:** One login. Members book from an app that looks like his gym. He gets paid automatically. He knows which members haven't been in for 2 weeks. He set it up on a Sunday afternoon before the Monday morning WOD.

**Switching trigger:** A peer in his CrossFit affiliate regional group posts: "Switched to StudioSimple, took me 90 minutes to set up, members are stoked on the app, saving $300/month." Marcus DMs them directly.

**WTP:** $79–$149/month flat. Would consider $249 LTD immediately if a peer vouches for it.

**Must-haves at launch:** Class scheduling, Stripe billing, mobile member app (booking + schedule). WOD delivery is important but could wait 60 days.

### Secondary Users

**Members (end users of the booking app)**
Members are not purchasers, but they're the reason gym owners switch or stay. The mobile app's UX for members determines word-of-mouth referrals and owner retention. Key actions: book a class, view schedule, receive booking confirmations and reminders, check in via QR code.

**Part-time staff / instructors**
Gym owners with staff need to give instructors limited access to view the class roster, mark attendance, and send announcements. Full admin access is not appropriate for hourly staff.

**Prospective gym owner evaluators (buyers)**
Before purchase, the gym owner evaluates StudioSimple in a free trial mode. The trial UX must demonstrate core value within 30 minutes: "I added 5 members, created this week's class schedule, and set up auto-billing" — all without a sales call or documentation.

## Success Metrics

### User Success Metrics

**Setup success rate**
- Target: 80% of trial users complete core setup (add members + create class schedule + connect Stripe) within 2 hours
- Measurement: Funnel tracking in onboarding wizard
- Rationale: "1-hour setup" is our core promise; if owners can't validate it in trial, they don't convert

**Trial-to-paid conversion**
- Target: 30% of trial accounts convert to paid within 30 days
- Measurement: Stripe subscription events
- Rationale: Industry benchmark is 15–25%; our strong positioning and community-driven acquisition should yield above-average conversion

**Member booking adoption**
- Target: 70% of gym members at active accounts use the mobile app to book within 60 days of studio going live
- Measurement: App session analytics (bookings initiated via app vs. web)
- Rationale: App adoption is the single strongest signal that the gym owner is "all-in" and will not churn

**Monthly active usage**
- Target: 90% of paying accounts log in and perform a management action (view roster, process payment, create class) at least 3× per week
- Measurement: Session analytics
- Rationale: Gym management is a daily-use case; low weekly usage predicts churn within 60 days

**Net Promoter Score**
- Target: NPS ≥ 55 (exceptional for SaaS; CrossFit community segment expected to be highest)
- Measurement: In-app NPS survey at day 60
- Rationale: Our growth is word-of-mouth first; NPS directly predicts referral velocity

**Churn rate**
- Target: <3% monthly churn (vs. ~8–12% industry average for small business SaaS)
- Measurement: Stripe churned subscriptions / total active subscriptions
- Rationale: Fitness market is trend-sensitive; stickiness comes from data dependency (member records) and the pain of migration

## MVP Scope

### Core Features (Launch — Month 1)

**1. Member Management**
- Create member profiles (name, contact, photo, membership type, start date, status)
- Membership types (recurring monthly, punch card, trial, paused)
- Attendance history per member
- Member notes and internal tags
- Bulk member import (CSV) + Mindbody-specific import tool (structured importer, not just CSV)
- Member portal: members can update contact info, view their schedule and attendance

**2. Class Scheduling**
- Create class types (e.g., "CrossFit WOD", "Open Gym", "Yoga Flow") with duration, capacity, location
- Recurring class schedules (weekly repeat) with override capability for holidays/substitutes
- Add/remove instructors per class
- Class roster view for instructors
- Waitlist: auto-promote from waitlist when a spot opens, send notification to member

**3. Online Booking (Member-Facing)**
- Members book and cancel classes via mobile app (iOS + Android) and web
- Booking window configurable (e.g., "open 7 days before class, closes 1 hour before")
- Automated booking confirmation and reminder emails/push notifications
- Late cancel / no-show tracking with configurable penalty rules

**4. Billing (Stripe Pass-Through)**
- Connect gym's own Stripe account (no middleman markup)
- Automated recurring billing on the 1st of each month (configurable date)
- One-time charges (class packs, drop-ins, retail)
- Failed payment retry logic (3 attempts, then notify owner)
- Automated dunning email sequence to member
- Revenue dashboard: MRR, upcoming charges, outstanding balances
- Stripe fees visible and transparent; zero StudioSimple markup

**5. Check-In**
- QR code check-in (member scans from app or printed card)
- PIN check-in (4-digit code displayed in member app)
- Manual check-in by staff via admin app
- Real-time class roster with check-in status visible to instructors

**6. Owner Admin App**
- Mobile admin view (iOS + Android) for gym owners and designated staff
- View today's classes, roster, check-ins
- Add a member, process a charge, view attendance — all from mobile
- Push notifications for failed payments and waitlist activity

**7. Mindbody Migration Tool**
- Guided import of member list (from Mindbody export CSV)
- Class schedule import
- Payment method re-authorization workflow (Stripe requires re-auth; guided email sequence to members)
- Migration checklist with status tracking: "Your migration is 80% complete"
- In-app migration support chat (real human, not AI)

**8. Basic Analytics**
- Active member count, new members this month, churned members this month
- Average class attendance rate
- MRR trend (last 6 months)
- At-risk members: members not attended in 14+ days (list view)
- Revenue by membership type

### MVP Success Criteria (Go/No-Go for Phase 2)

The MVP is validated and ready to scale when all of the following are true:

1. **25 paying customers** — at least 15 on monthly ($79/mo) and 10 on LTD, from at least 3 different gym types (CrossFit, yoga, general gym)
2. **NPS ≥ 50** — surveyed at day 60 from first 25 customers
3. **<3% monthly churn** — sustained over 90 days with ≥20 active accounts
4. **3 organic community posts** — unprompted mentions in CrossFit affiliate FB groups or r/gymowners recommending StudioSimple (not planted by founder)
5. **Mindbody migration NPS ≥ 60** — specifically for customers who migrated from Mindbody; this differentiator must actually work
6. **Average setup time ≤ 90 minutes** — measured from account creation to first automated billing test run

If all 6 criteria are met: proceed to AppSumo launch preparation and CrossFit WOD delivery feature.
