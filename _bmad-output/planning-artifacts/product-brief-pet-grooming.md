## Core Vision

### Problem Statement

Independent pet groomers and small grooming salons (1–3 chairs) operate with a daily administrative burden that costs them 15–30 minutes per dog, compounds into no-shows, and creates preventable revenue loss — because no booking software understands how pet grooming actually works.

The structural problem is a tool gap at the $0–$40/mo price point for grooming-specific workflows:

- **Generic horizontal tools** (Square Appointments, Vagaro, Acuity) don't support pet profiles, breed-specific appointment durations, vaccination tracking, or grooming history. Every groomer using these must maintain parallel paper systems.
- **Enterprise multi-service platforms** (MoeGo $49–$239/mo, Gingr $105–$180/mo, DaySmart $29–$249/mo) bundle boarding, daycare, and training features that a grooming-only business will never use — and charge accordingly.
- **Breed-aware scheduling doesn't exist anywhere.** A Bernese Mountain Dog full groom takes 3+ hours; a short-coated Beagle takes 45 minutes. Generic tools book them in identical slots. This causes daily schedule blowouts, frustrated clients, and lost same-day bookings.

Mobile van groomers face an additional layer: route optimization, offline operation in low-signal residential areas, and bulk rescheduling when a van breaks down — none of which MoeGo's Basic/Growth tiers support without paying $99–$159/mo.

The result: most independent groomers cobble together 3–5 tools at once, paying for fragmented solutions while losing revenue to no-shows, scheduling errors, and unbillable admin time.

### Problem Impact

**Quantified impact on a solo groomer doing 15 appointments/week:**

| Problem | Frequency | Revenue Impact |
|---------|-----------|---------------|
| No-shows (10% rate) | 1.5 per week | $75–$175 per lost slot |
| Scheduling blowout from breed mismatch | 2–3 per week | 30–60 min overtime/late client |
| Manual "ready for pickup" texting | 15x per week | 15–20 min/day admin |
| Paper card lookup before each groom | 15x per week | 5–10 min/day admin |
| Missed calls while actively grooming | 3–5 per week | 1–2 lost booking opportunities |

A conservative estimate: independent groomers lose $500–$1,500/month to no-shows and admin friction that purpose-built software eliminates. At $25/mo, GroomBook's ROI is immediate and measurable.

**Emotional impact:** Groomers entered the profession for the craft — they love dogs, not admin. The daily friction of broken tools turns what should be a satisfying trade into burnout. "I just want to groom" is the psychographic that defines the primary user.

### Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

| Solution | Why It Fails Groomers |
|----------|----------------------|
| **Square / Acuity / Vagaro** | No pet profiles, no breed awareness, no grooming history. Groomers maintain parallel paper cards. No grooming-specific notification workflows. |
| **MoeGo (Growth $99/mo)** | SMS caps (900/month) exhausted in 2 weeks for active shops. Texts come from MoeGo's number, not the business owner's. Financial reporting described as "impossible to navigate." History of surprise price increases after data migration. |
| **Gingr ($105–$180/mo)** | PE-acquired (Togetherwork); support response time collapsed from 20 min to 3 days post-acquisition. Payment processing captive with 2-day payouts. SMS delivery unreliable. Built for multi-service facilities — overkill for grooming-only. |
| **DaySmart Pet ($29–$249/mo)** | Interface consistently described as "dated." SMS is add-on except at $249/mo Platinum tier. Support degraded to AI-first email; phone effectively gone. |
| **GrooMore / Groomer.io** | Mobile-first but bare-bones; limited support; no AppSumo presence; unclear long-term viability. |

**The compound failure:** The most common groomer software journey is: start on Square (free, limited) → graduate to DaySmart (affordable, dated) → try MoeGo (feature-rich, expensive) → feel trapped when price increases arrive after data migration. Each transition costs a groomer a week of disruption and data re-entry.

### Proposed Solution

GroomBook is a grooming-only booking and client management platform designed for the workflow reality of independent groomers. It replaces 3–5 disconnected tools with one system that understands how grooming businesses actually work.

**The core product:**

1. **Breed-aware smart scheduler** — Admin configures breed × service type → appointment duration. The booking engine auto-suggests correct time slots. A Goldendoodle full groom gets 2.5–3 hours; a Chihuahua bath gets 45 minutes. No more schedule blowouts.

2. **Unlimited SMS, always included** — Booking confirmations, 24-hour reminders, and "your pet is ready for pickup" notifications are included at every price tier. No per-message caps, no SMS add-on fees. Texts come from the business's own dedicated number.

3. **Per-visit pet profiles** — Every appointment records the trim style, coat notes, temperament observations, and vaccination expiry. The groomer sees the complete history before every groom without searching paper cards.

4. **No-show protection built in** — Deposit collection at booking time, automated cancellation policy enforcement (charge card on file when client cancels within the policy window), and auto-fill from smart waitlist. No-shows drop from ~10% to <4%.

5. **Online self-booking** — Mobile-friendly client portal that respects breed-aware time slots, prevents double-booking, and confirms via SMS instantly.

**For mobile van operators (Mobile tier, $49/mo):**
- Route optimization — cluster appointments by geography for 6–8 dogs/day instead of 4–5
- Offline mode — access pet profiles and mark appointments complete without cell signal
- Bulk reschedule — mass client communication when van breaks down or weather cancels

**Positioning in one sentence:** "The only booking tool built for groomers, priced for groomers."

### Key Differentiators

1. **Breed-aware scheduling intelligence** — The only tool that auto-allocates appointment time based on breed, size, and service type. No competitor offers this at any price point.

2. **Unlimited SMS at every tier** — Competes directly against MoeGo's 900 SMS/month Growth cap and DaySmart's SMS add-on. This is the #1 cited differentiator in Teddy's positive reviews and a proven market signal.

3. **Price designed for solos** — $25/mo for 1-person operations vs. MoeGo's $49/mo Basic (salon) or $79/mo Basic (mobile). 50–65% cheaper with grooming-specific features that MoeGo's Basic tier lacks.

4. **No-show enforcement as a core workflow** — Not an afterthought or add-on. Deposit collection and auto-charge on cancellation are built into the booking flow from day one. The #1 most-requested missing feature across all competitor reviews.

5. **Grooming-only simplicity** — No boarding modules, no daycare scheduling, no training logs. The UI is optimized for a 15-minute setup by someone who has never used software to run their business.

6. **First grooming-specific LTD on AppSumo** — Category-defining positioning on the platform; zero competition; $69 price point in the proven $59–$89 sweet spot.

## MVP Scope

### Core Features

The MVP must deliver 5 things flawlessly before shipping anything else. These are the features groomers will abandon the product for if absent, and will stay forever if present:

**1. Breed-Aware Smart Scheduling**
- Admin configures: Breed (or size category) × Service type → Duration in minutes
- 350+ pre-populated breed library; admin can add custom breeds
- Booking engine auto-blocks correct duration per appointment
- System prevents back-to-back bookings that exceed available chair time
- Admin can override any duration for a specific appointment
- Mobile-friendly configuration UI (setup in <5 minutes)

**2. Unlimited SMS Communication (3 Templates, Auto-Triggered)**
- Booking confirmation: sent immediately on appointment creation (online or admin-booked)
- 24-hour reminder: sent automatically the afternoon before each appointment
- "Ready for pickup" notification: one-tap send from the groomer's appointment screen during checkout
- All texts come from a dedicated business phone number (Twilio-backed)
- Two-way SMS: clients can reply; replies appear in conversation thread
- No per-message caps at any tier

**3. Pet Profiles with Per-Visit Grooming History**
- Profile fields: pet name, breed, weight, coat type, temperament flags, vaccination expiry dates, owner info
- Per-visit notes: trim style (with optional photo), coat condition, service duration actual vs. scheduled, groomer notes, any incidents
- Quick-view history: most recent 3 visits visible before appointment begins
- Vaccination expiry alert: flag upcoming expirations 30 days in advance on appointment screen

**4. Online Client Self-Booking**
- Public booking link (shareable in Instagram bio, website, Google Business)
- Client selects: pet, service, groomer (if multi-groomer), preferred date
- System shows available slots respecting breed-aware durations
- Deposit collection at booking (configurable: $0–$50 or % of service)
- Client receives confirmation SMS immediately
- No login required for first booking (email-based returning client lookup)

**5. No-Show Protection and Deposit Enforcement**
- Configurable cancellation window (e.g., 24 hours before appointment)
- Card-on-file captured at booking via Stripe
- Auto-charge triggered when client cancels inside policy window
- Manual override available for compassionate exceptions
- Waitlist: clients can join a date's waitlist; auto-notified via SMS if slot opens
- No-show charge: admin triggers full-service charge from appointment screen with one tap

**UI/UX requirements for all MVP features:**
- Mobile-first design (groomers work on iPhones/Android all day)
- Dark mode optional (grooming environments vary)
- Setup wizard completes essential configuration in <15 minutes
- All core functions accessible within 2 taps from home screen
