  - https://agentzap.ai/blog/best-answering-service-for-jobber-ai-vs-live-vs-hybrid-2026
  - https://www.myaifrontdesk.com/blogs/discover-the-best-ai-agents-for-home-services-in-2026
- **Description**: HVAC, plumbing, roofing, and electrical companies miss ~27% of inbound calls — an average of $45K–$120K/year in lost revenue per company. AI voice agents now handle inbound calls 24/7, qualify leads, book appointments, and sync with existing FSM software. Avoca (YC 2024 → Kleiner Perkins Series A → Meritech/General Catalyst Series B) just raised $125M at a $1B valuation with 800+ customers and is on track to book $1 billion in jobs this year. Competitors include Sameday, LeadTruffle, AgentZap, and AgentVoice.
- **Target Industries**: HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical, pest control, pool service
- **Market Timing**: Growing fast — Avoca proves the category is fundable and scalable. Still early for small shops.
- **Example Products**:
  - Avoca — https://www.avoca.ai/ ($1B valuation, 800+ customers)
  - Sameday — https://www.gosameday.com/
  - AgentZap — https://agentzap.ai/ ($109/mo for Jobber integration)
  - LeadTruffle — https://www.leadtruffle.co/
- **White Space**: Avoca targets mid-market contractors ($5M+ revenue). There's a clear gap for a simpler, cheaper AI answering product for the **1–5 technician shop** at $49–$99/month. Also: multi-language AI for Spanish-speaking owner-operators in the trades. No one is targeting that specifically. Vertical specialization beyond HVAC (e.g., pool service, septic, chimney) is wide open.
- **Speed to Market**: 3–5 weeks for an MVP using existing voice AI APIs (Vapi, Retell AI, Bland AI) with a Jobber/Housecall Pro webhook integration.
- **LTD Fit**: Moderate — home service business owners do buy LTDs, but they need ongoing call handling (recurring makes more sense). A one-time setup fee + low MRR hybrid could work.
- **Content Strategy**: "HVAC answering service", "plumber answering service AI", "missed calls HVAC business", "AI receptionist for contractors"
