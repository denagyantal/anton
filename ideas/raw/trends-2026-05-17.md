## AI Voice Dispatchers for Trades (HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical)

- **Category**: Vertical AI
- **Signal Strength**: Strong
- **Sources**:
  - https://lithiumseo.com/the-ai-dispatcher-automating-appointment-scheduling-for-plumbers-electricians-and-hvac-pros/
  - https://useomniai.com/ai-field-service-management-home-services-2026/
  - https://serviceagent.ai/blogs/hvac-scheduling-software/
  - https://www.workiz.com/blog/tips-tricks/ai-and-field-service-transforming-hvac-plumbing-and-electrical-work-what-you-need-to-know/
  - https://fieldcamp.ai/features/ai-job-scheduling/
- **Description**: The average HVAC contractor misses 27% of inbound calls (35%+ during peak season). AI voice agents now pick up every call in under 2 seconds, qualify jobs, and book them directly into field service management (FSM) systems — no human dispatcher needed. Tools like ServiceAgent handle 24/7 inbound for HVAC/plumbing/electrical. Gartner predicts conversational AI will reduce contact center labor costs by $80B in 2026, with trades as a key beneficiary. When a job runs long or a truck breaks down, AI auto-reshuffles the day instead of forcing a manual rebuild.
- **Target Industries**: HVAC contractors, plumbers, electricians, home services
- **Market Timing**: Growing
- **Example Products**:
  - ServiceAgent (AI voice for HVAC) — https://serviceagent.ai/
  - FieldCamp AI scheduling — https://fieldcamp.ai/
  - OmniAI field service — https://useomniai.com/
  - Workiz (AI-assisted FSM) — https://www.workiz.com/
- **White Space**: Standalone AI dispatcher that integrates with ANY existing FSM (Jobber, Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan, etc.) rather than being locked to a single platform. Niche-specific voice AI tuned to contractor vocabulary (job types, parts, licensing). Bilingual (Spanish-English) for trades markets with large Latino workforce.
- **Speed to Market**: Moderate (4–8 weeks for MVP with existing voice AI APIs like Vapi/ElevenLabs + Twilio)
- **LTD Fit**: Moderate — trades owners prefer monthly pricing tied to call volume, but a capped lifetime deal for small shops ($500–1,000 calls/mo) is viable
- **Content Strategy**: "HVAC answering service", "plumber call answering", "field service AI dispatcher", "how to stop missing HVAC calls", "AI receptionist for contractors"
