## FieldFlow — $5/mo Jobber Alternative for Solo Tradespeople

- **Source**: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47294092
- **Additional Links**: https://fieldflow-nine.vercel.app/auth
- **Platform**: HN
- **Type**: Show HN
- **Engagement**: Low upvotes (1), 0 comments — very early stage
- **Revenue Data**: Not disclosed; pricing set at $5/month
- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5
- **Target Industry**: Carpentry, general contracting, solo tradespeople
- **Core Value Prop**: Jobber costs $40–200+/month and is designed for teams. FieldFlow strips it down to a $5/month solo-contractor tool — scheduling, jobs, invoicing.
- **Gap/Opportunity**: Jobber and ServiceTitan target 5–50 employee field service companies. Millions of solo tradespeople (plumbers, electricians, carpenters) have no affordable, simple tool. They still use paper, WhatsApp, or nothing.
- **Our Angle**: Build the "simple Jobber for one-person trades shops" — job tracking, customer contact, invoice generation, basic scheduling. LTD-friendly, no team features bloat.
- **LTD Potential**: 5/5

## Vertical CRM for HVAC / Plumbing / Electrical Contractors

- **Source**: https://entrepreneurloop.com/bootstrapped-saas-niches-solo-founders/ + IH research signal
- **Additional Links**: https://www.indiehackers.com/post/should-i-just-create-a-boring-b2b-saas-b6181991c0
- **Platform**: Indie Hackers (discussion + research synthesis)
- **Type**: Market research / discussion signal
- **Engagement**: High engagement on IH "boring B2B SaaS" threads
- **Revenue Data**: Comparable tools: Jobber $40–200/month; ServiceTitan $125–300/month; market gap at $29–79/month
- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5
- **Target Industry**: HVAC, plumbing, electrical — solo operators and 2–5 person shops
- **Core Value Prop**: Generic CRMs (HubSpot, Pipedrive) lack trade-specific features: equipment model tracking, seasonal maintenance reminders, warranty log, permit tracking, refrigerant certifications. A vertical CRM that "speaks HVAC."
- **Gap/Opportunity**: The solo/small HVAC shop (1–3 techs) can't justify Jobber or ServiceTitan pricing and complexity. They need customer history with equipment details, reminder sequences for annual AC tune-ups, and simple job quoting — nothing more.
- **Our Angle**: "The CRM built for one-truck HVAC operators." Equipment serial numbers in customer records, seasonal reminder campaigns, flat $39/month. High retention, low churn in this market.
- **LTD Potential**: 5/5
