## 5. Pest Control Compliance App

- **Additional Links**: https://entrepreneurloop.com/bootstrapped-saas-niches-solo-founders/

- **Engagement**: N/A
- **Revenue Data**: PestPac enterprise: $150+/user/month; basic tools: $30-$70/user/month. SMB gap under $50/month for 2-5 tech operations.
- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5
- **Target Industry**: Small pest control companies (2-10 technicians)
- **Core Value Prop**: Digital equipment inspection + chemical log + compliance records. 67% of small pest control businesses (under 5 techs) still use paper. OSHA and state ag department regulations require meticulous records. No simple tool under $30/month exists for this tier.
