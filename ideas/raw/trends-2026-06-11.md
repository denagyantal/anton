## AI-Native Field Service Management for Small Trades

- **Category**: Vertical AI / Trades Going Digital
- **Signal Strength**: Strong
- **Sources**:
  - https://serviceagent.ai/blogs/hvac-scheduling-software/ — ServiceAgent AI launched April 2025, 5.0/5 on Product Hunt, AI handles emergency intake and dispatches without human intervention
  - https://fieldworkhq.com/2025/12/26/field-service-management-trends-in-2026/ — 12 FSM trends for 2026, AI scheduling is #1
  - https://fieldcamp.ai/blog/field-service-trends/ — AI scheduling, mobile-first, no-code automation as top 3 ROI drivers for small businesses
  - https://programminginsider.com/how-field-service-management-software-boosts-profitability-in-2026/
- **Description**: AI is being bolted onto FSM platforms to handle inbound calls, emergency dispatch, and seasonal scheduling without adding staff. ServiceAgent AI (free model) specifically targets HVAC and plumbing with a vertical GPT that handles real-time call triage. The gap: most incumbents (ServiceTitan at $685M ARR, Jobber at $29/mo) are broad horizontal tools; AI-first, trade-specific tools are just emerging.
- **Target Industries**: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, general contractors, appliance repair
- **Market Timing**: Early-Growing — AI integration just accelerating
- **Example Products**:
  - ServiceTitan — https://www.servicetitan.com/ ($685M ARR, 25% YoY growth, incumbent)
  - Jobber — https://getjobber.com/ ($29/mo entry, broad FSM)
  - Housecall Pro — https://www.housecallpro.com/ (residential-focused)
  - ServiceAgent AI — https://serviceagent.ai/ (AI-first, free tier, PH launch April 2025)
- **White Space**: AI call agent + dispatch specifically for HVAC/plumbing solo operators (1-3 techs) who can't afford ServiceTitan pricing. Hyper-niche: "AI dispatcher for 1-truck HVAC companies" at $49-99/mo. Nobody is owning this low end with AI.
- **Speed to Market**: 3-4 weeks for MVP (AI call routing + basic job scheduling + mobile dispatch)
- **LTD Fit**: Yes — HVAC owners understand the ROI of fewer missed calls. $99-299 LTD is compelling.
- **Content Strategy**: "HVAC scheduling software", "HVAC answering service", "after hours HVAC dispatch", "plumbing job management app"
