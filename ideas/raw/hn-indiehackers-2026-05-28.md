## Market Signals & Trends

### Key Themes from HN & IH (May 2026)

1. **Vertical SaaS moats are widening**: The consensus across IH investors and founders is that deep vertical SaaS (HVAC, dental, restoration, AV, funeral) builds moats horizontal players can't cross without breaking their UX. Multiple senior investors explicitly calling this out as the dominant opportunity of 2026.

2. **Price gap for solo operators**: The "Goldilocks gap" between $0 (spreadsheets) and $150/mo (Jobber/ServiceTitan entry) is wide open for trades. Validated by SoloBid and CleanRoute concept discussions; SBA data says 89% of tradespeople lack adequate software.

3. **AI in boring industries is early**: Tracker AI (vet LLM, Dec 2025) and the funeral industry AI HN thread signal that boring industries are starting to see AI but have no clear winners. Early mover with industry-specific AI features (voice estimating, AI SOAP notes, AI obituary drafts) can dominate.

4. **Distribution is now the moat**: With vibe coding and AI reducing build cost to near zero, the IH community consensus (Noosa Labs, multiple posts) is that distribution — not product — is the durable moat. Trade Facebook groups, industry associations, and niche YouTube channels are where the boring business customer lives.

5. **LTD / AppSumo still works for boring niches**: Tradies, small facility operators, and local service businesses respond extremely well to lifetime deals. Word-of-mouth in tight professional communities makes a single AppSumo launch compound rapidly.
