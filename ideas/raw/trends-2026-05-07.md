## AI Voice Agents for Trades (HVAC, Plumbing, Roofing, Electrical)
- **Category**: Vertical AI
- **Signal Strength**: Strong
- **Sources**:
  - https://fortune.com/2026/04/27/avoca-ai-agents-missed-calls-hvac-plumbing-roofing-kleiner-perkins-chen-shrivastava-braswell/
  - https://www.avoca.ai/
  - https://getbreezyapp.com/
  - https://tradespilot.com/
  - https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/05/05/3287734/0/en/ServiceTrade-Launches-Stella-AI-Agents-That-Turn-Operational-Bottlenecks-Into-Revenue.html
  - https://solvea.cx/blog/best-ai-receptionist-for-hvac
- **Description**: AI voice/text agents that handle inbound calls, qualify jobs, schedule appointments, follow up on estimates, and manage dispatch — all without human involvement. The trigger: home service businesses miss 30-40% of calls (often worth $10K–$40K jobs). Avoca hit $1B valuation in April 2026 with 800 customers and $125M raised across YC, Kleiner Perkins, Meritech. ServiceTrade launched Stella (AI agents for operational bottlenecks) on May 5, 2026. Only ~12% of contractors have embedded AI into workflows despite 70%+ seeing it as relevant — early innings.
- **Target Industries**: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, general contractors
- **Market Timing**: Growing
- **Example Products**:
  - Avoca AI (https://www.avoca.ai/) — $1B valuation, 800+ customers
  - Breezy (https://getbreezyapp.com/) — AI-first, purpose-built for HVAC/plumbing/electrical
  - Trades Pilot AI (https://tradespilot.com/) — voice AI + SMS + FSM integrations
  - Stella by ServiceTrade (https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/05/05/3287734/) — launched May 2026
- **White Space**: Narrow AI focused on a single trade (e.g., roofing-only or plumbing-only), lower price point than Avoca ($1K+/mo) targeting 1-5 person shops; AI estimating follow-up sequences specific to HVAC maintenance agreements vs. install jobs; Spanish-language AI receptionist for Hispanic-owned trade businesses
- **Speed to Market**: 4-8 weeks for MVP (wrap existing LLM/voice APIs, integrate with Jobber/ServiceTitan webhooks)
- **LTD Fit**: Moderate — service businesses are recurring-revenue oriented, but a one-time "set it and forget it" AI receptionist could sell well at $199-$499 LTD
- **Content Strategy**: "HVAC answering service", "plumber missed calls software", "AI receptionist for contractors", "how to stop missing calls plumbing business"
