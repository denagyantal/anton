## Skimmer — Pool Service Management Platform

- **Engagement**: 35,000+ pool pros; Inc. 5000 honoree 2025
- **Revenue Data**: $5.7M ARR (2025); bootstrapped 8 years before $84M total raised (Series C $74M Oct 2024)

## WorkHero — AI Back Office for Small HVAC Contractors

- **Type**: Funded startup — clear market gap signal

- **Revenue Data**: Pre-scale (founded 2024). 85% of HVAC companies under 20 employees; owners spend 20+ hrs/week on admin.

## Key Insight

PE consolidation creates a two-tier opportunity: the consolidated top 40-60% is captured by enterprise software, while the independent bottom 40-60% lacks affordable tools. The chimney sweep and septic examples prove that simple CRUD apps with industry-specific routing or compliance features can generate $50K+ MRR with zero marketing spend, built solo in under 3 months.
