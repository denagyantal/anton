- **Market Timing**: Growing fast — 2026 is the inflection year
- **Example Products**:

  - WickedFile: https://www.wickedfile.com/ — AI reconciliation layer for repair shops

Independent shops (60–70% of the market) are losing customers to dealership chains because they lack the tech. No cheap, standalone AI advisor tool exists yet.
- **Speed to Market**: 3–4 weeks for MVP with LLM + OBD code lookup API
- **LTD Fit**: Moderate — shops worry about recurring costs, would respond well to LTD
