## AI Voice Agents for Home Services / Trades

- **Signal Strength**: Strong

  - https://serviceagent.ai/
  - https://myquoteiq.com/best-ai-tools-for-home-service-businesses/
- **Description**: AI agents that answer phones 24/7, qualify leads, book jobs, and follow up on estimates are becoming standard for HVAC, plumbing, roofing, and electrical contractors. Avoca ($125M raised, $1B valuation, 800+ customers) and Cactus ($7M seed, YC-backed) lead the space. The core pain: small home service shops miss 30-40% of inbound calls because technicians are in the field, and every missed call is a lost booking. AI voice agents fully handle this end-to-end. Market timing is exceptionally strong — only ~12% of contractors have embedded AI into workflows despite 70%+ seeing it as relevant.
- **Target Industries**: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, pest control, cleaning services, landscaping
- **Market Timing**: Growing (early movers funded, but massive TAM remaining)
- **Example Products**:
  - Avoca — https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/avoca-raises-125m-at-1b-valuation-to-power-americas-services-economy-with-ai-302753962.html (800+ customers, $1B valuation)
  - Cactus — https://techfundingnews.com/cactus-raises-7m-ai-automation-home-services/ ($7M seed)
  - ServiceAgent — https://serviceagent.ai/
  - Ringly.io — https://www.ringly.io/blog/ai-phone-answering-service
- **White Space**: Avoca is priced for mid-size businesses. Whitespace: ultra-affordable AI voice agent for 1-3 tech solo/micro-operators (sub-$100/month). Also: AI agents specialized by trade (e.g., HVAC-specific with parts/pricing knowledge). Niche: pest control, pool service, cleaning franchises not yet covered.
- **Speed to Market**: 3-4 weeks for MVP voice agent using existing LLM + telephony APIs (Twilio/Vapi); integration with Jobber/HouseCall Pro differentiates
- **LTD Fit**: Moderate — contractors prefer monthly SaaS; an LTD at $299 for small shops is plausible
- **Content Strategy**: "AI answering service for HVAC companies", "how to never miss a plumbing call", "AI scheduling for contractors", "best AI phone answering for small business"
