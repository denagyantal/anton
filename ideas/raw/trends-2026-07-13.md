## AI Voice Agents for Trades Dispatch

- **Category**: Vertical AI
- **Signal Strength**: Strong
- **Sources**:
  - https://www.saasmag.com/vertical-ai-agents-eating-horizontal-saas/
  - https://www.servicetitan.com/guides/2026-ai-in-the-trades
  - https://dreamerai.io/article-ai-automation-home-services.html
  - https://dispatchmvp.ai/dispatch-software-small-fleets
- **Description**: AI voice agents that handle inbound calls, scheduling, dispatching, and follow-ups for home service trades are exploding. Avoca (voice AI for HVAC, plumbing, field service) hit a $1 billion valuation on $125M in funding in April 2026 alone. ServiceTitan has rolled out AI call-handling and scheduling. Only 12% of trades contractors have embedded AI today — among early adopters, 62% report measurable efficiency gains, many saving 3+ hours per week.
- **Target Industries**: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, pest control, landscaping
- **Market Timing**: Growing fast — Avoca's unicorn valuation in April 2026 signals peak VC interest, but SMB penetration is still <15%
- **Example Products**:
  - Avoca (https://www.avoca.ai) — $1B valuation, April 2026, voice AI for home service trades
  - ServiceTitan AI (https://www.servicetitan.com/blog/hvac-ai) — incumbent adding AI layer
  - DispatchMVP (https://dispatchmvp.ai) — small-fleet AI voice dispatch, $49/month
- **White Space**: Avoca targets mid-market/enterprise trades. Tiny shops (1-5 techs) can't afford $500+/month ServiceTitan or Avoca enterprise pricing. A $49-99/month AI call-answering + scheduling bot for the solo HVAC tech / 3-person plumbing shop is wide open. No-code "answer my calls when I'm on a job" tool for micro-trades.
- **Speed to Market**: 2-4 weeks for MVP (LLM + Twilio + simple scheduling backend)
- **LTD Fit**: Moderate — trades owners buy tools per-job or per-month, not lifetime. A $299-499 LTD for a call-answering bot could work, but recurring at $49-79/month may convert better.
- **Content Strategy**: "HVAC answering service software", "missed call follow-up tool for plumbers", "AI dispatcher for small HVAC company"

- **Speed to Market**: 1-2 weeks MVP (file upload + date tracking + Twilio SMS alerts)
- **LTD Fit**: Strong — this is exactly the kind of simple utility with no obvious free alternative that LTD buyers on AppSumo love. $59-99 LTD is very viable.
