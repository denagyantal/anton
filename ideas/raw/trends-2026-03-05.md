The 2026 market has consolidated around three critical capabilities: AI-powered dispatch optimization, mobile-first field execution, and unified job-to-cash automation. Workever, Skedulo, and Simpro lead in mobile-first design, but most platforms still treat mobile as a "companion" to the desktop dashboard rather than the primary interface.
- **Target Industries**: Any industry with workers in the field — HVAC techs, plumbers, electricians, inspectors, property maintenance crews, delivery drivers, healthcare home visits
- **Market Timing**: Growing — mobile-first is the stated goal of every platform, but true "mobile-only" tools (no desktop required at all) are rare
- **Example Products**:
  - [Workever](https://workever.com/) — designed for mobile-first field teams
  - [Skedulo](https://www.skedulo.com/) — mobile-first for healthcare, energy, field services
  - [Simpro](https://www.simprogroup.com/) — trade contractor platform with strong mobile
  - [Housecall Pro](https://www.housecallpro.com/) — one-tap invoicing, GPS tracking, 40% admin time reduction
  - [ThermoGRID](https://thermogrid.com/) — HVAC-specific mobile-first
- **White Space**: A truly "phone-only" field service tool — no desktop dashboard at all. The owner manages everything from their phone: receives a lead via text, converts to a job with one tap, assigns to a tech (or themselves), tech gets directions + job details, takes completion photos, customer signs on screen, invoice sent automatically, payment collected via tap-to-pay. The entire business runs from two phones (owner + tech). Extreme simplicity for the tradesperson who has never logged into a "dashboard" in their life. Also: offline-first design — many job sites have poor cell reception, and most current apps break without connectivity.
- **Speed to Market**: Yes — a mobile-only job management app with SMS integration and Stripe payments is a 3-4 week MVP.
- **LTD Fit**: Strong — field workers are always on their phones and would try a simple, affordable tool. $99-$149 LTD for a phone-only job management tool with basic features.
