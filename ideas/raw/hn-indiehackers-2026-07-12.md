## ZenMaid — Scheduling SaaS for Maid/Cleaning Services

- **Source**: https://www.indiehackers.com/post/tech/from-a-cleaning-side-hustle-to-a-3m-yr-saas-for-cleaning-services-suhsqkDZB1zIwRmXxrFm
- **Additional Links**: https://get.zenmaid.com/
- **Platform**: Indie Hackers
- **Type**: Revenue milestone / Case study
- **Engagement**: 28 upvotes, 10 bookmarks, multiple comments (Feb 2025)
- **Revenue Data**: $3M/yr ($250K/month MRR), bootstrapped, 11 years old
- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5
- **Target Industry**: Residential cleaning / maid services
- **Core Value Prop**: Scheduling, automated client reminders, team management, and invoicing built specifically for small maid service businesses — replacing spreadsheets and generic CRMs
- **Gap/Opportunity**: ZenMaid is well-established but focused only on cleaning companies. Adjacent verticals (window cleaning, carpet cleaning, power washing, junk removal) remain fragmented and underserved. ZenMaid also shows the validation: cleaning service owners will pay recurring SaaS fees. Modern UX refresh + AI dispatch suggestions are missing.
- **Our Angle**: White-label or niche-out variant for a single adjacent vertical (e.g., window cleaning or carpet cleaning companies) with lighter onboarding, AI-powered customer reactivation texts, and a built-in referral program. Or build a cheaper LTD-compatible alternative targeting 1–5 person cleaning crews who can't afford $49+/mo.
- **LTD Potential**: 4/5

## Rentman — Operations Platform for AV/Event Rental Companies

- **Source**: https://www.indiehackers.com/post/tech/building-a-15m-arr-saas-from-a-gap-he-found-at-his-brick-and-mortar-HFriCBQLHukAmdXVEj1q

- **Platform**: Indie Hackers
- **Type**: Revenue milestone / Case study (April 2026)
- **Engagement**: Featured case study
- **Revenue Data**: $15M–$20M ARR ($1.25M+/month), bootstrapped for 8 years before taking outside capital, 70+ countries
- **Boring Business Score**: 4/5
- **Target Industry**: AV rental, event production, staging, broadcast, film equipment rental
- **Core Value Prop**: All-in-one operations platform for rental/production companies: equipment tracking, crew scheduling, quoting, logistics, invoicing. Replaces spreadsheets and clunky on-prem tools.
- **Gap/Opportunity**: Rentman proves the "boring rental operations" vertical can scale to $20M ARR. These niches are even less digitized than AV.
- **Our Angle**: Build a lightweight Rentman clone aimed at one adjacent underserved vertical — party/tent rental companies (strong seasonal patterns, simple inventory, price-sensitive, currently using pen and paper or Excel). Offer quoting + deposit collection + equipment availability in one. LTD launch on AppSumo to validate quickly.

## AI Voice Receptionist for HVAC / Plumbing / Trade Contractors

- **Source**: https://www.indiehackers.com/post/building-a-profitable-ai-voice-saas-agency-300-800-mrr-per-client-frAbgO1yQMfHOFFtY3gE
- **Additional Links**: https://newo.ai/hvac-plumbing-ai-receptionist/ ; https://www.leadtruffle.co/blog/best-ai-answering-services-contractors-2026/ ; https://www.getjobber.com/features/ai-receptionist/
- **Platform**: Indie Hackers
- **Type**: Revenue model / playbook post (Jan 2026)
- **Engagement**: Active discussion thread
- **Revenue Data**: Unit economics: $1–2K setup fee + $300–800 MRR per client, ~80% margin. Market: HVAC contractors miss 27–74% of inbound calls; each missed emergency call = $500–$900 lost revenue
- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5
- **Target Industry**: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, pest control, roofing contractors
- **Core Value Prop**: AI phone receptionist that answers inbound calls 24/7, qualifies leads, books appointments directly into the contractor's calendar — preventing revenue loss from missed calls during peak/after-hours
- **Gap/Opportunity**: Jobber launched an AI receptionist but it's locked to Jobber users. Rosie AI is standalone but generic. No one owns the HVAC-specific vertical with deep integration to HVAC dispatch software (ServiceTitan, FieldEdge, Jobber). A white-label AI receptionist built for HVAC with pre-trained scripts for common HVAC scenarios (emergency AC failure, seasonal tune-up, warranty calls) would command premium pricing.
- **Our Angle**: White-label AI voice agent with HVAC-specific call scripts, emergency escalation logic, and integrations to ServiceTitan/Jobber. Price at $299–499/month flat rate (simpler than per-call pricing). Target owner-operators of 1–5 tech HVAC shops via Facebook groups and trade associations. LTD not ideal here — MRR model fits better, but a one-time "setup + 1 year" bundle could work on AppSumo.
- **LTD Potential**: 2/5

## HVAC-Specific Vertical SaaS (Underserved Small Shop Segment)

- **Platform**: External research / market analysis
- **Type**: Market gap analysis
- **Engagement**: N/A
- **Revenue Data**: ServiceTitan starts at $245/tech/month with $5K–$50K setup fees — far too expensive for a 1–5 tech shop. Jobber is more affordable but generic. 89% of tradespeople lack adequate digital tools (SBA Q3 2025 report).
- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5
- **Target Industry**: Small HVAC companies (1–8 technicians)
- **Core Value Prop**: Scheduling, dispatch, invoicing, and equipment maintenance tracking purpose-built for micro HVAC businesses — without the $50K implementation fee of ServiceTitan or the generic feel of Jobber

- **Our Angle**: "ServiceTitan for small HVAC shops" — flat rate $99/month for up to 5 technicians, with HVAC-specific features: refrigerant inventory, maintenance agreement management, equipment history per customer address. Target via ACCA (Air Conditioning Contractors of America) forums, HVAC Reddit, and Facebook groups. LTD at $299 for 1-year access.

- **Boring Business Score**: 4/5

- **Core Value Prop**: Social media compliance archiving, content review queue, and automated policy-checking for small financial advisory firms that can't afford enterprise Smarsh/Proofpoint solutions
