- **Signal Strength**: Strong
- **Sources**:
  - https://profitabilitypartners.io/home-services-private-equity-acquirers/
  - https://ctacquisitions.com/why-private-equity-is-buying-home-services-companies/

- **Description**: PE firms are aggressively rolling up home service businesses (HVAC, plumbing, roofing, pest control) — 30+ active platforms, with Apollo putting ~$2B into Apex Service Partners at ~$10B valuation in May 2026. - **Target Industries**: HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical, pest control, pool service, garage doors
- **Market Timing**: Growing — 30+ active PE platforms now, market is mid-cycle in the consolidation wave
- **Example Products**:
  - ServiceTitan — https://www.servicetitan.com/ (11,800+ contractors, $59B in annualized GTV)
  - Housecall Pro — https://www.housecallpro.com/ (45,000+ contractors)
  - Jobber — https://getjobber.com/ ($700M+ valuation, 250K service businesses)
- **White Space**: "PE-readiness audit" tool for small shop owners (reports on software gaps, financial metrics that PE firms look for), multi-platform analytics dashboard for PE platforms managing mixed CRM ecosystems, benchmarking SaaS for home service operators to compare their KPIs against industry averages
- **Speed to Market**: 3–4 weeks for a benchmarking/audit report tool (read-only API connections to Jobber/HCP/ServiceTitan + automated PDF scorecard)
- **LTD Fit**: High — owners about to be acquired are highly motivated, one-time tool that creates a report they can hand to buyers; $299–$499 LTD very plausible
- **Content Strategy**: "how to sell your HVAC business to private equity", "PE-ready home service business", "ServiceTitan vs Jobber vs Housecall Pro for acquisition", "home services roll-up software"

The compliance crunch is real — small operators running on spreadsheets can't keep up. Existing solutions (RiskWatch, FileFlo, Contractor Foreman) are clunky or enterprise-priced.
- **Target Industries**: General contractors, subcontractors (electrical, plumbing, HVAC, drywall, roofing), construction management firms
- **Market Timing**: Growing — regulatory pressure increasing through 2026 makes this increasingly non-optional
- **Example Products**:
  - FileFlo — https://getfileflo.com/ (subcontractor compliance docs)
  - RiskWatch — https://www.riskwatch.com/ (enterprise compliance)
  - Contractor Foreman — https://contractorforeman.com/
- **White Space**: Simple COI + certification tracker for small GCs (5–50 subs) with automated email/SMS reminders, site access lock tied to expired certs, and one-click audit-ready PDF reports. Sub-$100/month, mobile-first, no enterprise contract. The sweet spot nobody is serving well.
- **Speed to Market**: 2–3 weeks for core (contact DB + document upload + expiry tracking + email reminders). MVP viable.
- **LTD Fit**: High — COI compliance is an annual pain point, one-time payment to solve it forever is very attractive to small GC owners; $149–$299 LTD plausible
- **Content Strategy**: "COI tracking software for contractors", "subcontractor compliance management", "certificate of insurance tracker", "OSHA compliance for general contractors"
