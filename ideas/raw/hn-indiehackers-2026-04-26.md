Market via r/therapists, private practice Facebook groups, NASW chapters, Psychology Today therapist directory.

## Key Themes & Market Intelligence

### Winning Playbook: Attack Incumbents That Raised Prices
1. Find a dominant incumbent that raised prices significantly (SimplePractice +69%, Typeform price hike, Jobber too expensive for solos)
2. Build a focused, cheaper alternative for the most frustrated segment (private-pay therapists, solo contractors)
3. Social listen for disgruntled users and DM them
4. Launch with LTD at $199-299 to fund early development
5. Proven by Youform ($18k MRR, $35k LTD launch) — apply to boring industries

### Strongest Boring Industry Signals Right Now
1. **Solo therapist EHR** — SimplePractice +69%, 225k clinicians, 1/3 go private-pay = ~75k frustrated users actively searching for alternatives
2. **Auto repair shop management (US market)** — Motara proved the model in Australia, US market 10x larger and underserved
3. **HVAC-specific field service** — 89% lack digital tools, ServiceTitan too expensive for small shops, niche-specific features justify $99-149/month
4. **Solo contractor field service** — FieldFlow and FieldLedgr both launched in Q1 2026 showing active validation, opportunity to niche down to one trade

### Distribution Channels for Boring Industries
- Trade associations (ACCA for HVAC, ASE/NAPA for auto repair, NASW for therapists)
- Industry Facebook groups (huge engagement in trades groups)
- Local chambers of commerce / BNI networks
- YouTube content for the specific trade (rank for "HVAC software" searches)
- AppSumo LTD for immediate revenue validation
