# Boring Business Software Trends — Research Report
**Date:** 2026-04-11
**Agent:** Trend Spotter
**Focus:** Emerging vertical SaaS for trades, local services, healthcare practices, logistics, property management

---

## Executive Summary

The "boring business software" space is experiencing a significant capital and startup wave in 2025–2026. Driven by AI commoditization, legacy system fragility, and regulatory pressure, non-glamorous industries are finally getting the software attention they deserve. The signal is strong across multiple verticals: HVAC/trades, dental, veterinary, property management, field services, elder care, childcare, auto repair, and construction. Y Combinator, Bessemer, TCV, and Prudence Capital are all actively backing companies in these sectors.

Key macro signal: The U.S. construction and home services market is worth ~$2.1–2.2 trillion, yet specialty trades still operate largely on paper, phone calls, and PDFs. Construction firms invest less than 1% of revenue in R&D/IT vs. 3.5–4.5% in advanced manufacturing — this "digital debt" is now being rapidly unwound.

## Trend 1: AI-Native Quote & Estimating Tools for HVAC/Trades Suppliers

**Signal Strength: STRONG**

### What's Happening
Commercial HVAC, electrical, and plumbing suppliers are getting AI-powered quoting tools that read blueprints using computer vision to auto-generate bills of materials and quotes. The incumbent workflow — a human estimator manually counting equipment on blueprint PDFs — is being automated away.

### Key Evidence
- **Rebar** (withrebar.ai) raised **$14M Series A** (March 2026) led by Prudence Capital, with Zero Infinity Partners, Founder Collective, Villain Capital, and Optimist Ventures participating
  - Doubles ARR in the first 6 weeks of 2026
  - 40 clients, 7 of which are also investors
  - Founded October 2024 by ex-HVAC estimator Evan Brown
  - Reduces quote generation time by 60–70%, boosts win rates by 2–3x
  - Uses proprietary computer vision models on construction blueprints
- **CountBricks** (countbricks.com) — AI construction estimating, users report 8+ hours/week saved and $95k+ additional annual revenue
- **Semble AI** — AI agents that design building systems (fire, HVAC, plumbing, security)
- AI takeoff systems now achieve 85–90% accuracy vs. manual, reducing a half-day task to minutes
- **Togal.AI** — automates plan reading and takeoff; ~$1,999/year, accessible to mid-size contractors
- **Downtobid** — AI bidding/scope analysis for subcontractors

### Industries Affected
HVAC, electrical, plumbing, commercial construction, MEP (mechanical/electrical/plumbing) suppliers

### White Space
- AI quoting for residential HVAC (vs. commercial-focused Rebar)
- Integration layer between quote-gen AI and existing FSM platforms (ServiceTitan, etc.)
- AI for specialty subcontractor niches: fire suppression, insulation, roofing
