## ZenMaid — Maid / Cleaning Service Management
- **Source**: https://indiebites.com/114
- **Additional Links**: https://www.highsignal.io/4m-a-year-from-maid-cleaning-software/
- **Platform**: Indie Hackers / IndieBites podcast
- **Type**: Revenue milestone / Founder interview
- **Revenue Data**: $4M ARR (~$333k MRR); 3,000+ customers; bootstrapped since 2013; 30 employees
- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5
- **Target Industry**: Residential cleaning / maid services

## Dental Compliance Tracking SaaS
- **Source**: https://medium.com/@urano10/vertical-saas-micro-niches-beyond-the-obvious-01e0adf16a98

- **Type**: Validated gap with revenue model
- **Revenue Data**: Case study: $30k-$50k MRR at $300-$500/practice with 100+ dental clients; $280 CAC; 0.9% churn; $84M ARR addressable ceiling
- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5

- **Our Angle**: Compliance binder in the cloud for dental offices, marketed via dental associations and CE platforms
- **LTD Potential**: 4/5
