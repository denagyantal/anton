## AI-Native Vertical SaaS for Trades (HVAC, Plumbing, Roofing, Electrical)

- **Category**: Vertical AI
- **Signal Strength**: Strong
- **Sources**:
  - https://www.zuper.co/blog/introducing-the-ai-operating-system-for-roofing
  - https://myquoteiq.com/top-10-ai-tools-for-plumbing-businesses-in-2026/
  - https://myquoteiq.com/best-ai-estimating-software-roofing-2026/
  - https://embeddedblog.wpengine.com/blog/saaspocalypse-vertical-saas-ai/
  - https://www.servicetitan.com/blog/ai-driven-roofing-automation
- **Description**: AI is being bolted onto vertical trade software at pace — AI estimating, AI call answering, AI follow-up automation, and photo-based damage assessment are now table-stakes differentiators. Zuper is positioning itself as an "AI Operating System for Roofing." QuoteIQ bundles AI estimating, AI call answering, AI follow-up, and photo enhancement into a single plumbing-focused subscription. The general trade software platforms (ServiceTitan, Jobber, FieldEdge) are adding AI layers but remain complex and expensive — creating a window for leaner, AI-first competitors.
- **Target Industries**: HVAC contractors, plumbing shops, electrical contractors, roofing companies (all 5–50 employee shops)
- **Market Timing**: Growing — AI features are differentiating but not yet universal; first-movers in specific trades have an 18–24 month window
- **Example Products**:
  - Zuper (https://www.zuper.co) — AI OS for roofing/HVAC/electrical/plumbing
  - QuoteIQ (https://myquoteiq.com) — AI-bundled trade software starting with roofing/plumbing
  - ServiceTitan (https://www.servicetitan.com) — market leader, $9.5B valuation at IPO, but expensive for small shops
  - Jobber (https://www.getjobber.com) — simpler/cheaper, strong for SMB field service
- **White Space**: AI-first software purpose-built for a single trade vertical (e.g., electrical-only or plumbing-only), targeting 1–10 tech shops who are priced out of ServiceTitan ($200–$600/mo) and outgrowing Jobber. Specifically: AI job costing/estimating that learns from historical tickets; AI tech dispatch based on skill + GPS proximity; automated invoice-to-payment follow-up sequences tuned to trade billing patterns.
- **Speed to Market**: Yes — a 2–4 week MVP is feasible with an LLM backend + existing field service primitives (scheduling, invoicing). The moat is vertical depth and data.
- **LTD Fit**: Moderate — trades owners buy on ROI ("saves me 3 hours/week") but prefer monthly billing with no lock-in. LTD feasible at $199–$299 with AppSumo exposure; upsell to MRR for AI features.
- **Content Strategy**: "[trade] software", "HVAC scheduling software", "AI estimating for plumbers", "roofing job management software", "electrical contractor app"

## Offline-First Mobile Field Tools for Deskless Workers

- **Category**: Mobile-First Field Tools
- **Signal Strength**: Strong
- **Sources**:
  - https://www.arrivy.com/blog/best-field-service-mobile-apps-for-technicians/
  - https://www.clappia.com/blog/offline-gps-field-data-software
  - https://fieldcamp.ai/blog/best-field-service-mobile-apps/
  - https://coastapp.com/blog/field-service-apps/
- **Description**: The field service management market hit $6.26B in 2026 and projects to $23.61B by 2035. The key pain: technicians work in basements, rural areas, construction sites — bad or no internet is the norm, not an edge case. Yet most field service apps still fail when offline. The category of truly offline-first apps (forms save locally, GPS captures offline, auto-sync on reconnect) is underpenetrated. AI route optimization (35% less drive time), skill-based auto-dispatch, and mobile-native job documentation are the highest-demand features.
- **Target Industries**: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, cleaning, inspection, construction, utilities
- **Market Timing**: Growing — mobile-first platforms are displacing desktop-first FSM tools; offline capability is table-stakes but rarely done well
- **Example Products**:
  - Arrivy (https://www.arrivy.com) — field operations platform
  - Coast App (https://coastapp.com) — mobile-first field service app
  - Jobber (https://www.getjobber.com) — strong mobile UX
  - MapTrack (https://www.maptrack.com) — GPS + field service
- **White Space**: A lightweight, offline-first mobile job management app that works without internet for HVAC/plumbing/electrical techs — no 200-feature enterprise platform. Single-purpose: job checklist → photo proof → signature → invoice → sync. Sub-$50/mo per tech pricing. No desktop requirement.
- **Speed to Market**: Yes, 2–4 weeks for a focused MVP (offline-capable PWA or React Native app)
- **LTD Fit**: High — field service owners hate monthly SaaS costs; LTD at $149–$249 per tech slot is compelling. AppSumo potential is strong.
- **Content Strategy**: "offline field service app", "mobile job management for technicians", "field service app without internet", "technician mobile app"
