## FieldFlow — $5/mo Jobber Alternative for Solo Contractors

- **Source**: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47294092
- **Additional Links**: https://fieldflow-nine.vercel.app/auth
- **Platform**: HN
- **Type**: Show HN
- **Engagement**: 1 point, 0 comments (early/new, March 2026)
- **Revenue Data**: None disclosed; founder uses it daily as a carpenter
- **Boring Business Score**: 5
- **Target Industry**: Field service / independent contractors (carpenters, plumbers, electricians, landscapers)
- **Core Value Prop**: $5/month flat field service software — invoicing, scheduling, client management — built by a solo contractor who was paying $150+/month for bloated alternatives
- **Gap/Opportunity**: Jobber starts at $39/month, Tradify at $47/user. Both are wildly overpriced and feature-bloated for one-person operations. Could also offer an LTD at $99 to jumpstart AppSumo. Huge gap between prosumer spreadsheet users and expensive enterprise FSM platforms.

- **Core Value Prop**: Mid-market commercial property managers are stuck: entry-level tools (AppFolio, Buildium) are too limited for complex leases, but enterprise platforms (Yardi, MRI) are too expensive and slow to implement. The gap is a purpose-built platform for 5–50 commercial properties.

## General Market Signals (2025-2026)

### Solo Contractor Software Pricing Pain
Jobber: $39-199/month. ServiceTitan: $400-500+/month. Tradify: $47/user/month. The gap between "nothing" and "enterprise" for solo/micro contractors is enormous. Every trade — plumbing, electrical, HVAC, painting, landscaping — has this same problem. The FieldFlow Show HN proves the demand exists.

### Vertical SaaS 3× Price Premium
Multiple independent sources confirm: purpose-built vertical SaaS commands 3× the price of a generic tool. A kanban board for landscapers charges 3× more than Trello. This isn't just theory — Rentman's $15M+ ARR in AV rental proves it works at scale.

### Boring Industries Still Using Spreadsheets/Paper
Most-cited still-manual workflows: HOA maintenance tracking, pest control route management, mobile mechanic job cards, funeral home scheduling, independent dental practice billing. These are all begging for simple, purpose-built software.

### AI Making Indie Boring-Biz SaaS Faster to Build
Solo founders now ship vertical SaaS in 6 weeks using Claude Code/Cursor. This means the time-to-compete in boring niches is shrinking — but the advantage still goes to whoever builds first with real industry knowledge.
