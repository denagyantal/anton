## FieldFlow — $5/mo Jobber Alternative for Solo Contractors

- **Source**: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47294092
- **Additional Links**: https://fieldflow-nine.vercel.app/auth
- **Platform**: HN
- **Type**: Show HN (March 2026)
- **Engagement**: Low (1 pt, 0 comments) — brand new, barely discovered
- **Revenue Data**: $5/mo — builder uses it daily himself as a solo carpenter
- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5
- **Target Industry**: Solo trade contractors (plumbers, electricians, handymen, carpenters)
- **Core Value Prop**: Invoicing, scheduling, client management for one-person operations — no crew management bloat. Builder was paying $150+/mo for field service software he hated.
- **Gap/Opportunity**: Jobber starts at $39/mo, Tradify is $47/user — both designed for teams. Solo contractors (the majority of the market) have zero options below $39. The solo tier is completely unserved. 89% of tradespeople lack adequate digital tools (SBA Q3 2025).
- **Our Angle**: A more polished, marketing-invested version — mobile-first, AI-assisted estimating, offline mode. $19/mo hits the sweet spot between free spreadsheets and Jobber. LTD at $149 would convert well on AppSumo.
- **LTD Potential**: 5/5 — clear price anchor, massive underserved market, no funded competition at this tier

## SoloBid — Estimates & Invoicing for Solo Trade Contractors

- **Source**: https://www.indiehackers.com/post/help-me-pick-3-micro-saas-ideas-for-underserved-niches-which-would-you-pay-for-5b0c370d3b
- **Additional Links**: https://taxsort-tools.pages.dev/solobid
- **Platform**: Indie Hackers
- **Type**: Idea validation / landing page test (March 2026)
- **Engagement**: 8 landing page signups in first week (most of the 3 ideas tested)
- **Revenue Data**: Not yet built — validated demand via signups
- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5
- **Target Industry**: Solo electricians, plumbers, handymen
- **Core Value Prop**: Send clean estimates from phone, get paid. No crew management, no scheduling for 30 trucks. $19/mo.
- **Gap/Opportunity**: ServiceTitan costs $1K+/mo. Jobber is $50-150/mo. Solo contractors starting out (first 1-2 years) have no on-ramp. This is the pre-Jobber tool — the entry-level tier nobody has built well. Validated: SoloBid got 8x more signups than the other two ideas in the same test.
- **Our Angle**: Build the on-ramp to Jobber. Mobile-first, phone-number-based onboarding (no email required), AI photo-to-estimate (take a photo of a job site, get an estimate draft), WhatsApp/SMS payment reminders. Upsell path to team features when they hire their first employee.
- **LTD Potential**: 5/5 — price-sensitive market loves LTD; strong AppSumo community of trades businesses

## CleanRoute — Route Optimization for Solo Cleaning Businesses

- **Source**: https://www.indiehackers.com/post/help-me-pick-3-micro-saas-ideas-for-underserved-niches-which-would-you-pay-for-5b0c370d3b
- **Additional Links**: https://taxsort-tools.pages.dev/cleanroute
- **Platform**: Indie Hackers
- **Type**: Idea validation / landing page test (March 2026)
- **Engagement**: 1 landing page signup (least validated of the 3 ideas, but the problem is real)
- **Revenue Data**: Not yet built
- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5
- **Target Industry**: Solo house cleaners, 1-person cleaning businesses
- **Core Value Prop**: Optimize daily routes between client homes, handle rebooking. Solo cleaners waste 45+ min/day on bad routing. ZenMaid and Jobber are built for teams.
- **Gap/Opportunity**: Route optimization for solo cleaners is completely unaddressed. The existing tools (ZenMaid, Jobber, HouseCall Pro) are designed for multi-tech operations. A solo cleaner's needs: daily route order, client notes, rebooking when clients cancel, simple invoicing. No one is doing this for sub-$20/mo.
- **Our Angle**: Combine route optimization (Google Maps API), recurring appointment management, and simple invoicing in one mobile app. Key differentiator: WhatsApp/SMS client reminders so cleaners don't chase no-shows. $15/mo flat, LTD $99.
- **LTD Potential**: 4/5 — huge market (estimated 1.2M solo cleaning businesses in US), very low price sensitivity at $15/mo, good AppSumo audience fit

- **Boring Business Score**: 4/5
- **Target Industry**: DIY landlords, 1-10 rental units

## Key Market Signals (Cross-Cutting Themes)

### Pricing Gap: The Solo/Micro Tier Is Unserved
The dominant signal across all sources: enterprise field service tools (ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro) start at $50-$1,000+/mo — priced for teams of 5+. The solo and 1-3 employee tier (the vast majority of trades businesses) is completely underserved. Any product hitting $15-$49/mo with mobile-first UX can dominate this segment.

### AI Voice as a Wedge for Trades
Voice AI answering services are proving ROI for HVAC/plumbing at $300-800 MRR. The implementation arbitrage exists now but will commoditize in 18-24 months. The play is building it as a vertical SaaS product (pre-built flows for specific trades) before it becomes a commodity white-label service.

### "Scratch Your Own Itch" Vertical SaaS Is Working
Rentman ($15M+ ARR) from an AV rental operator validates the pattern: built by someone inside the industry, ignored by VCs, bootstrapped to $20M. The comments from experienced operators/angels confirm the same pattern applies to HVAC, dental, restoration — "vertical SaaS in niche industries is where the biggest moats are quietly being built right now."

### SBA Data: 89% of Tradespeople Lack Adequate Digital Tools
The Small Business Administration's Q3 2025 Technology Adoption Report puts numbers on the opportunity: 89% of tradespeople lack adequate digital tools. This is the macro tailwind behind every idea in this report.
