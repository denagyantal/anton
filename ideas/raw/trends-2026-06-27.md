## AI Voice Receptionists for Trades (HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical)

- **Signal Strength**: Strong
- **Sources**:

  - https://www.withallo.com/blog/ai-phone-answering-services-for-hvac

  - https://getivai.com/hvac
  - https://www.gosameday.com/post/ai-answering-services-features-comparison-for-hvac-and-plumbing-businesses
- **Description**: HVAC contractors lose an average of $45,600/year to missed calls; plumbers lose up to $125,000. The average home service business misses 30–40% of calls during peak hours. A wave of purpose-built AI voice agents are now targeting this specific pain point — answering, qualifying leads, booking appointments, and routing emergencies. Products like Rosie AI, Trillet ($49/mo), SkipCalls ($199/year), NextPhone, and AgentZap are all competing in this niche. The segment is growing fast and is distinct from generic AI answering services because they train on contractor-specific call patterns (emergency vs. estimate, dispatching, seasonal spikes).
- **Target Industries**: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, pest control, cleaning, roofing contractors
- **Market Timing**: Growing — multiple funded entrants, still fragmented, no dominant winner yet
- **Example Products**:
  - Rosie AI — https://www.rosieai.com (purpose-built for trades, launched 2024)
  - Trillet — https://www.trillet.ai (best value at $49/mo, voice + SMS + WhatsApp)
  - SkipCalls — https://skipcalls.com ($199/year flat rate)
  - NextPhone — https://www.getnextphone.com
  - AgentZap — https://agentzap.ai
  - Allo — https://www.withallo.com
  - IVA — https://getivai.com
- **White Space**: Multi-location dispatching + AI voice combined; integration layer connecting voice AI to existing FSM tools (Jobber, Housecall Pro); Spanish-language voice AI for the large Hispanic trades workforce; AI that handles follow-up texts/estimates after the call, not just booking.
- **Speed to Market**: 2–4 weeks for an MVP (VAPI/Retell AI + Twilio + scheduling API)
- **LTD Fit**: Strong — trades owners are price-sensitive, $199–499 LTD is plausible, and ROI story ("recover one missed call and pay for itself") is compelling
- **Content Strategy**: "HVAC answering service", "plumbing missed calls", "AI receptionist for contractors", "best HVAC virtual receptionist 2026"

Over 72% of service orgs now use AI tools, and 93% have at least partially implemented AI. The winning platforms in 2026 are defined not by feature lists but by how much back-office work they fully automate — predictive maintenance via IoT sensors, computer vision for quality inspection, autonomous route optimization, and AI-driven scheduling without human intervention.

- **Market Timing**: Growing — early leaders emerging, still no dominant AI-native FSM for SMBs
- **Example Products**:
  - Breezy AI — https://getbreezyapp.com (first AI-native platform for residential HVAC pros)
  - Fieldproxy — https://fieldproxy.com (positioned as AI-first ServiceTitan alternative)
  - FieldCamp — https://fieldcamp.medium.com
- **White Space**: AI-native FSM built specifically for solo operators or 1–5 person shops (ServiceTitan and Jobber both under-serve this segment); AI that automatically generates quotes from job photos; preventive maintenance scheduling driven by equipment age data.
- **Speed to Market**: 4–6 weeks for MVP with scheduling + AI dispatch core
- **LTD Fit**: Moderate — FSM is sticky SaaS (monthly), but a capped LTD for a solo operator tier ($299–499) would convert well
- **Content Strategy**: "AI field service management", "autonomous dispatch software", "AI HVAC software 2026", "field service AI agents"
