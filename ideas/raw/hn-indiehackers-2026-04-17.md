- **Target**: 1-person plumbers, locksmiths, mobile mechanics, handymen

- **Target**: State-licensed pest control businesses (required to keep detailed chemical logs)
- **LTD Score**: 4/5

- **Evidence**: ZenMaid serves maid services (B2C residential) at $200k MRR; commercial janitorial is B2B with fundamentally different workflows (contract management, safety compliance, shift scheduling for large crews) — no dominant bootstrapped player
- **Target**: Commercial cleaning companies (office buildings, hospitals, schools)
- **LTD Score**: 4/5

- **Evidence**: TrashLab ($3M seed) validates the waste hauler space; they target large operators; 1-5 truck dumpster rental companies need a simple online booking widget + dispatch + invoicing at $49/mo
- **Target**: Local dumpster rental businesses
- **LTD Score**: 4/5
