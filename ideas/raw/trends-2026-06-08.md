## AI Voice Agents for Trades (Missed Call Problem)

- **Category**: Vertical AI
- **Signal Strength**: Strong
- **Sources**:
  - https://fortune.com/2026/04/27/avoca-ai-agents-missed-calls-hvac-plumbing-roofing-kleiner-perkins-chen-shrivastava-braswell/
  - https://contractortoolstack.com/software/avoca-ai/
  - https://www.trillet.ai/blogs/best-ai-answering-service-for-trades-2026
  - https://www.leadtruffle.co/blog/best-ai-answering-services-contractors-2026/
  - https://augmentedtrades.com/ai-receptionist-software-plumbers-2026/
  - https://hvacknowitall.com/blog/ai-private-equity-and-the-independent-hvac-contractor-in-2026
- **Description**: Tradespeople (HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical) miss 27–62% of inbound calls because technicians are on job sites. AI voice agents now answer, qualify, schedule, and dispatch automatically. Avoca raised $125M and reached $1B valuation in April 2026 — backed by Kleiner Perkins, Meritech, General Catalyst. Multiple smaller players filling the market (Trillet at $49/mo, Marlie.ai at $0.19/min, Rosie at $49/mo unlimited, LeadTruffle at $229+/mo). Only ~12% of contractors have adopted AI in any workflow — massive adoption gap.
- **Target Industries**: HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical, pest control, landscaping
- **Market Timing**: Growing — Avoca signals this is proven, but 88% of contractors still unaddressed
- **Example Products**:
  - Avoca — https://getbreezyapp.com/ (Breezy) and https://fortune.com/2026/04/27/avoca-ai-agents-missed-calls-hvac-plumbing-roofing-kleiner-perkins-chen-shrivastava-braswell/
  - Trillet — https://www.trillet.ai/
  - LeadTruffle — https://www.leadtruffle.co/
  - Marlie.ai (integrates Housecall Pro + Jobber)
- **White Space**: Affordable ($29–49/mo) AI answering built specifically for 1–5 person operations — current sub-$100 solutions lack deep integration with dispatch/scheduling. Also: electrical-specific and pest-control-specific variants barely exist. Roofing-specific (storm season surge handling) is untapped.
- **Speed to Market**: Yes — voice AI APIs (ElevenLabs, Twilio, Vapi) make this buildable in 2–4 weeks. Core differentiator is trade-specific scripts + dispatch integration.
- **LTD Fit**: Moderate — trades prefer monthly SaaS but an LTD at $199–299 could appeal to solo operators who distrust recurring fees
- **Content Strategy**: "AI answering service for plumbers", "HVAC missed call software", "contractor after hours answering", "[trade] AI receptionist"

- **Speed to Market**: Yes — 2–4 weeks for core loop (job creation, scheduling, invoicing, Stripe payments)
- **LTD Fit**: High — solo operators love a one-time purchase; AppSumo sweet spot is $59–99 for tools like this

- **Market Timing**: Growing — regulatory pressure is forcing the final paper holdouts to digitize in 2025–2026

Current tools do 2–3 of these well but require integrations for the rest. Specialty niche: refrigerated transport or hazmat compliance layer on top of base TMS.
- **Speed to Market**: Moderate — compliance logic (HOS rules, IFTA calculations) takes time to get right; 6–8 weeks for MVP
- **LTD Fit**: Moderate — owner-operators are value-conscious and would buy LTD; fleet managers less so

- **Description**: Dentrix (1980s) and Eaglesoft still dominate dental PMS with outdated on-premise installs, server dependencies, and expensive annual support contracts. Cloud competitors (CareStack, Curve Dental, Denticon, Dentrix Ascend) are taking share from mid-size and DSO practices, but pricing targets multi-location practices. 2,500+ practices use CareStack. Solo practices (1 dentist, 1–2 hygienists) are the underserved tier — they want cloud-based but can't afford $400–800/month enterprise platforms. The Adit platform (AI-powered, all-in-one) is gaining attention for consolidating fragmented tool stacks.
- **Target Industries**: Solo dental practices, small group practices (1–3 dentists), specialty dental (orthodontics, pediatric)
- **Market Timing**: Growing — cloud migration accelerating, but legacy vendor lock-in slows adoption
- **Example Products**:
  - CareStack — https://carestack.com/
  - Curve Dental — https://www.curvedental.com/
  - Adit — AI-powered all-in-one
  - Practice-Web — budget cloud option for solo practices
- **White Space**: Lightweight cloud dental PMS targeting solo practices at $99–149/mo — scheduling, charting, billing, insurance verification, patient reminders. Current options either oversell (CareStack/Denticon at enterprise pricing) or undersell (legacy on-prem that's just been web-wrapped). AI-native insurance pre-authorization automation is nearly completely unaddressed.
- **Speed to Market**: Slow — HIPAA compliance, HL7/FHIR integration, and clinical charting make this a 3–6 month build
- **LTD Fit**: Low — dentists don't buy software on AppSumo; direct sales + 30-day free trial is the model
- **Content Strategy**: "cloud dental software for solo practice", "replace Dentrix affordable", "dental scheduling software small office", "dental billing software cloud"
