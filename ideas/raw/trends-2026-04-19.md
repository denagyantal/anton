## AI-Native Field Service for Micro-Teams (HVAC / Plumbing / Electrical)

- **Category**: Trades Going Digital + Vertical AI
- **Signal Strength**: Strong
- **Sources**:
  - https://www.repair-crm.com/2026/04/04/hvac-field-service-management-software-for-small-teams-the-2026-operational-guide
  - https://www.servicetitan.com/industries/hvac-software
  - https://www.housecallpro.com/industries/hvac-software/
  - https://sera.tech/
  - https://getfieldy.com/blogs/best-hvac-estimating-dispatch-scheduling-software
  - https://www.fieldpie.com/blog/best-plumbing-software/
- **Description**: Small HVAC/plumbing/electrical shops (2–5 techs) are being underserved by both enterprise tools (ServiceTitan at $400+/mo, designed for 20+ trucks) and basic tools that lack real AI. Manual "double-entry trap" costs shops ~$4,800/employee/year in lost productivity. AI scheduling, equipment-level service history, and mobile-first offline tools remain missing from budget-tier options.
- **Target Industries**: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, appliance repair — shops with 1–5 technicians
- **Market Timing**: Growing
- **Example Products**:
  - ServiceTitan (https://www.servicetitan.com/) — enterprise, overpriced for small teams
  - Housecall Pro (https://www.housecallpro.com/) — popular but limited AI
  - Sera (https://sera.tech/) — newer, $399/mo, AI-assisted dispatching
  - Fieldy (https://getfieldy.com/) — all-in-one, growing fast
  - Repair-CRM (https://www.repair-crm.com/) — explicitly targeting small teams
- **White Space**: Sub-$100/mo AI-native FSM built from day one for solo operators and 2–5 tech shops. Features missing at this tier: predictive maintenance alerts by equipment serial number, offline mobile with photo attach, QuickBooks sync without manual re-entry, flat-rate pricing library.
- **Speed to Market**: Yes — MVP in 3–4 weeks (scheduling + dispatch + invoicing core)
- **LTD Fit**: Strong — boring business owners hate subscriptions; $299–499 LTD would convert
- **Content Strategy**: "hvac software for small business", "field service software under $100", "jobber alternative for HVAC", "plumbing dispatch software"

Existing TMS solutions target mid-large fleets; small operators pay for features they don't need or can't afford enterprise pricing.
- **Target Industries**: Owner-operators, small fleets (1–10 trucks), freight brokers
- **Market Timing**: Growing (regulation forcing adoption NOW)
- **Example Products**:
  - DispatchMVP (https://dispatchmvp.ai/) — AI dispatch for owner-operators, newer entrant
  - Alvys (https://alvys.com/) — AI-integrated TMS, growing
  - FleetRabbit (https://fleetrabbit.com/) — ELD + GPS at $3/vehicle/month
  - Toro TMS (https://www.torotms.com/) — small fleet focused
- **White Space**: All-in-one $49/mo TMS for 1–5 truck owner-operators with built-in FMCSA digital DVIR compliance, simple load management, and fuel expense tracking — no enterprise upsell, no per-seat pricing.
- **Speed to Market**: 3–4 weeks for compliance + load tracking MVP
- **LTD Fit**: Moderate — owner-operators are frugal; one-time LTD for compliance tools at $199–299 is compelling
- **Content Strategy**: "small fleet TMS", "owner operator dispatch software", "FMCSA digital DVIR compliance tool", "trucking software under $50"

## Home Health & Care Scheduling Software

- **Category**: Paper-to-Digital + Compliance Tech
- **Signal Strength**: Moderate
- **Sources**:
  - https://painonsocial.com/blog/vertical-saas-ideas
  - https://www.aimagicx.com/blog/vertical-ai-micro-saas-business-model-2026
  - https://wearepresta.com/ai-saas-startup-ideas-2026-10-high-growth-opportunities-for-founders/
  - https://techaisle.com/blog/668-2026-smb-top-10-business-issues-tech-priorities-agentic-ai-shift
- **Description**: Home health agencies, adult daycare centers, and small care homes are still scheduling caregivers on spreadsheets or whiteboards. Caregiver matching (client preference + skills + availability + compliance certification), EVV (Electronic Visit Verification — now mandated in all 50 states for Medicaid-funded care), and mileage/billing for state reimbursement are uniquely complex requirements. Large platforms (ClearCare, HHAeXchange) target 50+ caregiver agencies; the 5–25 caregiver market is neglected.
- **Target Industries**: Home health agencies, adult day services, private duty care, companion care agencies
- **Market Timing**: Growing (EVV mandate driving urgency)
- **Example Products**:
  - HHAeXchange — enterprise EVV + scheduling
  - ClearCare / WellSky — mid-market
  - Rosemark — smaller agencies
  - AxisCare — growing, modern UI
- **White Space**: Simple EVV-compliant scheduling + caregiver app for 5–25 caregiver agencies at $99/mo flat. Key differentiator: mobile clock-in/out with GPS (EVV compliance), automatic state billing file export, caregiver certification expiration alerts. Current affordable options are clunky or non-compliant.
- **Speed to Market**: 4 weeks MVP (EVV GPS + scheduling core)
- **LTD Fit**: Low-Moderate — regulated industry, agencies need ongoing compliance updates; subscription preferred. Could do LTD for non-Medicaid agencies.
- **Content Strategy**: "home health scheduling software", "EVV software small agency", "caregiver scheduling app", "hhaxchange alternative small agency"
