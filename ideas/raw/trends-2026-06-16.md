- **Speed to Market**: Yes — mobile app + AI quote generation MVP achievable in 3-4 weeks
- **LTD Fit**: Strong — trades owners are price-sensitive and love one-time deals; $97 LTD for quote + scheduling AI is credible

## Meta-Trend: Embedded Finance is the TAM Multiplier

- **Category**: SMB Platform Consolidation
- **Signal Strength**: Strong
- **Sources**:
  - https://www.saasmag.com/vertical-saas-niche-beats-horizontal-2026/
  - https://www.fieldboss.com/blog/best-hvac-field-service-software/ (mentions Wisetack, GreenSky integrations)
  - https://www.arrivy.com/blog/the-best-field-service-management-software-for-small-businesses/ (in-field payments as table stakes)
- **Description**: The SaaS Mag 2026 analysis is explicit: "The median TAM for a vertical SaaS business jumps from $250M to $513M once fintech products are included." Any vertical SaaS built in 2026 that doesn't include a payments/financing wedge is leaving 2x of TAM on the table.
- **Target Industries**: All boring business verticals — trades, home services, auto repair, property management
- **Market Timing**: Growing (becoming standard expectation)
- **Example Products**:
  - Wisetack (consumer financing for trades) — wisetack.com
  - GreenSky (home improvement financing) — greenskyonline.com
  - ServiceTitan Financing integration — servicetitan.com
  - Housecall Pro Payments — housecallpro.com
- **White Space**: Embedded business banking + revenue-based financing for trade shop owners — a "boring business neobank" that uses job history as underwriting data. No current player owns this.
- **Speed to Market**: 8-12 weeks (requires banking/lending partnerships; complex but defensible)
- **LTD Fit**: No — financial products don't fit LTD model; this is an MRR/revenue-share play
- **Content Strategy**: "business banking for contractors", "financing for HVAC business", "same day pay for trade techs"
