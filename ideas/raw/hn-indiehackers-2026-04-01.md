## Kickserv — Field Service / Plumbing Invoicing & Dispatch

- **Source**: https://www.flowjam.com/blog/27-micro-saas-examples-that-actually-print-money-in-2025
- **Additional Links**: https://www.kickserv.com/
- **Platform**: Indie Hackers (referenced)
- **Type**: Revenue milestone / Validated product
- **Engagement**: Widely cited as case study
- **Revenue Data**: $120k MRR — notably "refuses to grow beyond 10 employees"
- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5
- **Target Industry**: Plumbing, HVAC, field service trades
- **Core Value Prop**: Scheduling, dispatch, and invoicing for field service technicians
- **Gap/Opportunity**: Kickserv serves a massive but fragmented market. ServiceTitan charges $250-350/technician/month with forced enterprise contracts — massive pricing gap for small operators (1-10 techs). Most small trades shops are still on paper or cobbled-together tools.
- **Our Angle**: A leaner, simpler, LTD-eligible alternative targeting 1-5 tech shops who are priced out of ServiceTitan and find Jobber/Housecall Pro too generic. Focus on one trade vertical (e.g., pest control or pool service) for tighter product-market fit.
- **LTD Potential**: 4/5

- **Type**: Market gap analysis / Discussion

- **Revenue Data**: Existing players: Buildium (~$50k MRR), Rentec Direct — but all target 10+ unit landlords
- **Boring Business Score**: 4/5
- **Target Industry**: Accidental landlords, small residential property investors (2-10 units)
- **Core Value Prop**: Simple rent collection, maintenance request tracking, and tax-time expense categorization for small landlords who don't want enterprise software
- **Gap/Opportunity**: 71% of US rental units are owned by "mom and pop" landlords with fewer than 10 units. They use spreadsheets, Venmo, and text messages. Existing tools (AppFolio, Buildium) are overkill and priced for professionals. This group desperately needs a one-page dashboard.
- **Our Angle**: Position as "landlord tax prep made easy" — the pain point isn't day-to-day but at tax season. Auto-categorize income/expenses, generate Schedule E report, export to CPA. $49-99/year one-time or low monthly. Strong LTD potential.
- **LTD Potential**: 5/5

- **Boring Business Score**: 3/5

- **Core Value Prop**: Venue-aware booking system that handles simultaneous room allocation, group sizing, waivers, and upsells

- **Type**: Ask HN / Community discussion

- **Boring Business Score**: 4/5
- **Target Industry**: Independent restaurants, multi-location food operators
- **Core Value Prop**: Keep menu items, prices, and availability synchronized across DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub, restaurant website, and in-house POS
- **Gap/Opportunity**: Identified explicitly in HN discussion as a pain causing "lost sales and operational disruptions." ## PostalAgent — Direct Mail CRM for Local Service Businesses

- **Source**: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46482268 (Ask HN: What are you working on? Jan 2026)
- **Additional Links**: https://postalAgent.com
- **Platform**: Hacker News
- **Type**: Show HN / Early product
- **Engagement**: Mentioned in "What are you working on?" thread
- **Revenue Data**: Early stage / not disclosed
- **Boring Business Score**: 4/5
- **Target Industry**: Local service businesses (integrates with Jobber, Pipedrive, Zoho, Follow Up Boss, Zapier)
- **Core Value Prop**: Personalized postcard campaigns targeting consumers and businesses by address, integrated with existing CRM/FSM platforms
