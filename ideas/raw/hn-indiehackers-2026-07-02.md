Differentiate by including industry-specific pricing databases (local material costs, regional labor rates) and integrating with Google Maps for job-site travel time estimation. Price: $49/mo or $299 LTD.
- **LTD Potential**: 5/5 — pure software, quote + invoice + PDF generation is a classic LTD product. Contractors pay once for a tool that saves them 2-3 hours per week forever.

## Market Intelligence Summary

**Key macro signals from today's scan:**

1. **Trades businesses are wealth-generating at scale** — Private equity is acquiring HVAC/plumbing businesses at 7-figure multiples. Independent operators need software to compete without selling out.

2. **89% of tradespeople lack adequate digital tools** (SBA Q3 2025) — The adoption gap is still enormous. Most blue collar businesses run on spreadsheets, text, and paper invoices.

3. **AI adoption in trades is accelerating** — CNN Business (Oct 2025): plumbers are fastest-growing AI user segment, using ChatGPT for quoting and customer communication. This creates a wedge for AI-native trade tools.

4. **Field service management market: $5.49B (2025) → $23.61B by 2035** (16% CAGR) — The rising tide lifts all boats. Building a vertical SaaS in this space today means riding 10 years of tailwinds.

5. **Regulatory disruption creates switching moments** — FMCSA ELD deadline (April 2026) forces trucking operators to switch. FTC action on property management software (Dec 2025) creates trust vacuum. Both are distribution moments.

6. **The "ZenMaid formula" still works** — ZenMaid went from 0 to $3M/yr in 11 years targeting maid services. The exact same playbook (become the user → build niche scheduling/invoicing → charge $50-150/mo → let word of mouth compound) is replicable for pest control, pool service, tree service, junk removal, and dozens of other single-trade verticals that ZenMaid doesn't serve.

**Highest-conviction opportunities:**
- AI-powered quote-to-invoice tool for solo contractors (ToolsAI validated the demand, execution gap is wide)
- Niche scheduling SaaS for a single underserved trade (pest control, pool service, tree care)
- AI voice receptionist productized for a single trade vertical (not a generic agency product)
