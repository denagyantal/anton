- Global Vertical SaaS market: $94B-$143B (2026), projected $369B by 2033 at 16.3% CAGR

- Vertical SaaS companies achieve 2-3x higher valuations than horizontal SaaS at comparable revenues

Key macro thesis for 2026:
1. AI-native architecture replacing bolted-on AI — ground-up AI platforms taking share from 2010s-era legacy tools

3. Embedded finance within vertical SaaS increasing engagement by 88%
4. The 2010s vertical SaaS replacement cycle is underway — older platforms will lose market share by 2027
