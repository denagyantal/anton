# Trend Spotter Report — 2026-07-04

## Summary
Today's scan focused on boring business vertical SaaS, field service digitization, and vertical AI disruption. Strong signals across voice AI for trades, legacy software disruption in veterinary/dental, and emerging white space in compliance tech for small contractors.

## Trend 1: Voice AI Receptionists for Trades — The $1B Signal

- **Category**: Vertical AI
- **Signal Strength**: Strong
- **Sources**:
  - https://aiautomationglobal.com/blog/avoca-ai-voice-agent-trades-unicorn-2026
  - https://www.avoca.ai/
  - https://www.idlen.io/news/avoca-ai-1-billion-valuation-kleiner-perkins-services-economy-voice-agents-april-2026/
  - https://contractortoolstack.com/software/avoca-ai/
  - https://solvea.cx/blog/best-ai-receptionist-for-hvac
  - https://leapingai.com/blog/voice-ai-platforms-compared-leaping-ai-vs-avoca
- **Description**: Avoca AI raised $125M+ at a $1B valuation (April 2026) selling an AI voice receptionist to HVAC, plumbing, roofing, and electrical contractors. It answers missed calls, books jobs into ServiceTitan, and is on track to book $1B in jobs for customers in 2026. Customers include 1-800-GOT-JUNK?, Goettl, and Turnpoint Services. Avoca's moat is its ServiceTitan Gold Partner status. The broader category is emerging: Cira by Crafted Labs, Suzee AI, Rosie, and Smith.ai are all competing for the same calls-to-bookings workflow.
- **Target Industries**: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, pest control — any trades business running on inbound calls
- **Market Timing**: Growing (Avoca proved the model; competitors are entering now)
- **Example Products**:
  - Avoca AI — https://www.avoca.ai/ ($1B valuation, April 2026)
  - Cira by Crafted Labs — AI receptionist for home service SMBs
  - Suzee AI — always-on voice AI for small businesses
  - Rosie — pure-AI receptionist at $49–$149/mo (budget tier)
  - Smith.ai — hybrid AI+human at $95–$685/mo
- **White Space**: Voice AI for non-ServiceTitan shops (Jobber, Housecall Pro users are underserved — Avoca's moat is ST-specific). Also: AI follow-up and re-booking for cancelled service calls, multi-language voice AI for Spanish-speaking trade crews. Outbound AI calling for seasonal reminders (HVAC tune-ups, winterization) is barely touched.
- **Speed to Market**: 4–6 weeks for an MVP focused on a single CRM integration (e.g., Jobber webhook + Bland.ai/Retell for voice)
- **LTD Fit**: Moderate — trades owners prefer monthly billing tied to volume; LTD could work at $299–$499 for a call-limit tier
- **Content Strategy**: "AI receptionist for plumbers", "never miss a service call", "HVAC after-hours answering service", "Avoca alternative for Jobber"

- **Speed to Market**: 6–8 weeks for a niche add-on (e.g., AI client comms layer on top of existing practice management APIs)
- **LTD Fit**: Strong — solo practice owners are price-sensitive; a $299–$499 LTD for a single focused tool (comms, reminders, intake) would convert well

## Trend 3: Plumbing & Trades Scheduling Software — Mid-Market Gap

- **Signal Strength**: Strong
- **Sources**:
  - https://www.intelmarketresearch.com/plumbing-service-software-market-41867
  - https://www.bdrco.com/blog/hvac-business-software-guide/
  - https://serviceagent.ai/blogs/hvac-scheduling-software/
  - https://fieldworkhq.com/
  - https://gorilladesk.com/

- **Description**: The plumbing service software market is projected to grow from $624.9M in 2026 to $1.46B by 2034 (11.2% CAGR). The market is bifurcated: ServiceTitan dominates larger multi-truck operators ($685M ARR, 25% YoY growth), but small to mid-market shops (1–5 trucks) are underserved. Legacy integration is the primary adoption barrier — many small plumbing businesses still run on spreadsheets and phone calls. The top alternatives all cluster at $39–$99/mo: Fieldwork, Housecall Pro, Jobber, Workiz, GorillaDesk. None dominate the 1–3 truck segment with a truly simple, mobile-first UX.
- **Target Industries**: Independent plumbing, HVAC, electrical, and general trades contractors (1–10 employees)
- **Market Timing**: Growing (strong TAM growth; no clear winner in the sub-ServiceTitan segment)
- **Example Products**:
  - ServiceTitan — https://www.servicetitan.com/ (enterprise, $500+/mo)
  - Jobber — https://getjobber.com/ (SMB, $49–$149/mo)
  - Housecall Pro — SMB-friendly, strong mobile
  - GorillaDesk — https://gorilladesk.com/ (pest/lawn/cleaning focus)
  - Fieldwork — https://fieldworkhq.com/ ($39/mo solo plan)
- **White Space**: Simple, opinionated scheduling + invoicing for solo/micro-trades (1–2 trucks) who find Jobber "too complex". QuickBooks integration that actually works without an accountant. SMS-first communication tools for technicians who don't use email. Instant-estimate tools for common jobs (drain unclogging, faucet replacement) priced by zip code.
- **Speed to Market**: 3–4 weeks for a focused MVP (scheduling + invoicing + SMS for one trade vertical)
- **LTD Fit**: Strong — trades owners are LTD buyers; $199–$299 for a single-operator tier would convert on AppSumo
- **Content Strategy**: "plumbing scheduling software", "HVAC invoicing app", "ServiceTitan alternative small business", "best app for plumbers"

- **Market Timing**: Early-to-Growing (healthcare/legal mature; SMB trades still very early)
- **Example Products**:
  - Harvey — $300M ARR, legal AI
  - Avoca — $1B valuation, trades voice AI
  - Sierra — $150M ARR, customer support AI
  - Abridge / Hippocratic AI — healthcare clinical documentation
  - EvenUp — personal injury case automation

- **Speed to Market**: 3–5 weeks for a single-workflow AI agent (e.g., AI quote follow-up for auto shops using Claude + Twilio SMS)
- **LTD Fit**: Moderate to Strong depending on ROI clarity; best framed as "pays for itself in 1 recovered job"
- **Content Strategy**: "[industry] AI assistant", "automate [workflow] for [boring business]", "AI for plumbers / HVAC / dentists"

## Trend 5: Construction ESG & Carbon Compliance Tech for Small Contractors

- **Category**: Compliance & Regulation Tech
- **Signal Strength**: Moderate
- **Sources**:
  - https://getgreenbadger.com/
  - https://builtworlds.com/companies/green-badger/
  - https://neuroject.com/esg-software-tool/
  - https://saaslatestnews.com/vertical-saas-startups-2026/ (mentions $15B market, green mandate tailwinds)
  - https://www.tekmon.com/esg-software-for-construction-companies
- **Description**: Commercial construction increasingly requires ESG/LEED compliance documentation as a condition of contracts with large GCs and government projects. Green Badger is the leading purpose-built tool (claims 80% reduction in LEED documentation time). However, it targets large projects. Small contractors ($1M–$10M revenue) who need to qualify for ESG-compliant projects face two problems: (1) no simple tool for tracking materials and emissions on small projects, and (2) no certification preparation pathway. The $15B construction ESG market is being driven by green building mandates in NY, CA, and EU-import rules.
- **Target Industries**: Small general contractors, specialty subcontractors (MEP, insulation, roofing), construction material suppliers
- **Market Timing**: Early (regulatory deadline pressure is building; most small contractors have no solution)
- **Example Products**:
  - Green Badger — https://getgreenbadger.com/ (LEED + ESG, targets large projects)
  - Clue — https://www.getclue.com/ (equipment emissions tracking)
  - SustainIQ — 300+ ESG metric reporting, enterprise-focused
  - Locus Technologies — clean construction compliance
- **White Space**: Lightweight ESG scorecard for small contractors to qualify for green project bids (vs. Green Badger's enterprise complexity). Material emissions calculator that works from a standard Bill of Materials CSV. "Compliance passport" so small subs can prove ESG compliance to multiple GCs without re-entering data.
- **Speed to Market**: 4–6 weeks for a simple materials tracker + compliance checklist MVP
- **LTD Fit**: Moderate — small contractors will buy once if it helps them win a contract; $199–$399 LTD viable
- **Content Strategy**: "LEED compliance software small contractor", "construction ESG reporting", "green building certification small business"

Franchisor-to-franchisee comms tool separate from Slack/email chaos. Training and compliance checklist tool for new franchisee onboarding.

## Trend 7: IoT & Smart Home Integration Layer for Field Service Techs

- **Category**: Mobile-First Field Tools / Vertical AI
- **Signal Strength**: Moderate
- **Sources**:
  - https://www.bdrco.com/blog/home-service-industry-trends/ (30 trends for 2026)
  - https://serviceagent.ai/blogs/hvac-scheduling-software/
  - https://myquoteiq.com/best-invoicing-software-hvac-businesses-2026/
- **Description**: HVAC, plumbing, and electrical systems are increasingly connecting to smart home ecosystems (Nest, ecobee, Ring, Leviton). Technicians now encounter connected systems on service calls with no tools to interface with them. This creates two new product opportunities: (1) a technician-facing app that can pull real-time diagnostic data from a customer's smart thermostat before arriving on-site, and (2) proactive service triggers — when a Nest reports unusual runtime patterns, auto-create a service ticket and notify the customer's regular HVAC contractor.
- **Target Industries**: HVAC contractors, plumbers (leak detection IoT), electricians (smart panel techs), home warranty companies
- **Market Timing**: Early (IoT in homes is mainstream; the service workflow integration layer barely exists)
- **Example Products**:
  - ServiceTitan — basic IoT integrations in roadmap
  - No clear winner in pre-arrival diagnostics or proactive IoT-triggered service
- **White Space**: "Smart home diagnostic pre-flight" app for HVAC techs (pull thermostat history before arrival). Proactive maintenance trigger: IoT anomaly → automated reminder to homeowner → one-click booking with their regular contractor. Smart water sensor → instant plumber dispatch.
- **Speed to Market**: 6–8 weeks for integration with Nest/ecobee APIs + Jobber/Housecall Pro scheduling webhook
- **LTD Fit**: Moderate — HVAC company owners would pay; better as recurring $49/mo per tech
- **Content Strategy**: "smart thermostat HVAC service integration", "IoT field service management", "predictive HVAC maintenance software"
