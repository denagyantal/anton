## Top White Space Opportunities (Not Yet Built Well)

1. **IFTA fuel tax automation for owner-operators** — Trucking's most painful recurring task. No clean cheap solution.
2. **AI estimate follow-up for contractors** — Auto-text/call leads who got a quote but didn't book. Massive revenue recovery.
3. **Commercial janitorial software** — Cleaning companies are digitizing fast but commercial janitorial has almost no focused tools.
4. **Trades-specific job costing inside QuickBooks** — Contractors need margin-by-job visibility that QuickBooks doesn't natively provide.
5. **Mental health solo practitioner all-in-one** — Notes + billing + scheduling + insurance verification, HIPAA-compliant, under $100/mo.
6. **Pool service vertical FSM** — GorillaDesk comes closest but it's generic; pool has unique chemical tracking, equipment history needs.
7. **AI lease drafting for DIY landlords (state-law-aware)** — High-value document generation, easy to LTD price.
8. **Veterinary clinic modern replacement** — AVImark/Impromed are deeply legacy; modern cloud alternative with AI clinical notes is wide open.
