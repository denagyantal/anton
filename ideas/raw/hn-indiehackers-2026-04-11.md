## Solo Contractor Field Service Management ($5/mo Jobber Alternative)

- **Source**: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47294092
- **Additional Links**: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47668698 (FieldLedgr, another similar entry)
- **Platform**: HN
- **Type**: Show HN
- **Engagement**: 1 point, 0 comments
- **Revenue Data**: Not disclosed (early stage, $5/mo flat rate pricing)
- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5
- **Target Industry**: Solo contractors / tradespeople (carpenters, plumbers, electricians, HVAC, general contractors)
- **Core Value Prop**: A $5/month field service management tool covering invoicing, scheduling, and client management — built by a carpenter who was paying $150+/month for bloated tools. No per-user fees, no ticket queue — single founder responds to feedback directly.
- **Gap/Opportunity**: Jobber starts at $39/mo, Tradify at $47/user. A massive pricing gap exists between free/ad-hoc tools and professional-tier software. Solo operators are massively underserved — they don't need 80% of what Jobber offers. Multiple HN entrants attacking this gap signals validated demand with weak execution. FieldLedgr (free tier) and FieldFlow ($5/mo) are both 0-engagement entries — neither has found distribution yet.
- **Our Angle**: A polished, niche-focused version targeting one specific trade (e.g., plumbers only, or HVAC technicians only) with trade-specific templates, compliance checklists, and language — charged at $19-29/mo. Niche messaging commands 3× premium vs generic tools. LTD play at $99-149 on AppSumo.
- **LTD Potential**: 4/5

- **Type**: Show HN
- **Engagement**: 3 points, 3 comments

- **Core Value Prop**: AI-powered quote generation for trade businesses — eliminates the time tradespeople spend manually creating estimates and proposals. Trades still create quotes via spreadsheets, phone calls, or pen-and-paper; this automates the process.

Could be sold standalone or as a bolt-on to existing field service apps. AppSumo LTD potential is high — trades are notorious LTD buyers.

- **Type**: Discussion / Founder Story

- **Core Value Prop**: ValetVault was built specifically for barbers after the founder spoke with hundreds of barbers across Australia and discovered that most "barbershop software" was actually salon software rebranded — missing barber-specific workflows like walk-in queue management, barber-to-barber tip splitting, chair rental billing, and beard/cut history cards.

## Pest Control Route + Compliance Software

- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5
- **Target Industry**: Independent pest control companies (1-10 technicians)

Strong distribution through PCA (Pest Control Associations) and state-level licensing boards.

1. **Solo contractor tool gap** — multiple Show HN entries with zero traction but clear demand signal. Jobber ($39+) vs free = wide open $9-29/mo slot.
2. **Trade-specific AI automation** — AI quoting, AI phone answering for trades/vets. Multiple early entrants with no distribution. First mover with AppSumo launch wins.
