- **Speed to Market**: Fast — an MVP that generates compliance documents based on state + industry could ship in 2-3 weeks
- **LTD Fit**: Strong — small business owners hate recurring compliance costs. A one-time purchase for "stay compliant with AI regulations" is an easy sell, especially with fear-based marketing around fines

  - [Vertical SaaS Is Cooked — Hacker News](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46891634)
  - [Has AI removed the appeal of vertical SaaS? — Hacker News](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46662857)
- **Description**: A massive market rotation is underway. Nearly $300B in market value evaporated from application software companies in early February 2026 after the launch of agentic AI platforms. The market thesis: if 10 AI agents can do the work of 100 employees, you don't need 100 SaaS seats anymore. This is a 90% reduction in seat revenue for the same output. However — for boring businesses, this creates a massive *opportunity*: their current "software" is spreadsheets, paper, and phone calls. They're not losing SaaS seats to AI; they're gaining their *first* software. The SaaSpocalypse hurts horizontal SaaS for knowledge workers but actually accelerates adoption of vertical tools for trades/services.
- **Target Industries**: All boring businesses — the disruption of horizontal SaaS creates buyer confusion and opens the door for focused vertical tools
- **Market Timing**: Early/Growing — the narrative is fresh (Feb 2026), and the implications for boring businesses are underappreciated
- **Example Products**: The disruption is sector-wide. Opportunity is for *new* AI-native vertical tools that are outcome-priced (not seat-priced)
- **White Space**: AI-native tools for boring businesses priced by outcome (per job completed, per tenant managed, per invoice processed) rather than per seat. First-mover advantage for whoever builds the "AI-first CRM for plumbers" or "AI-first scheduling for cleaners."
- **Speed to Market**: Fast — an AI-native tool with outcome-based pricing for a specific boring vertical could MVP in 2-4 weeks
- **LTD Fit**: High — boring business owners are especially nervous about recurring costs during the SaaSpocalypse. "Pay once, own forever" messaging resonates even more now
- **Content Strategy**: "ServiceTitan alternative," "cheap HVAC software," "[industry] software no monthly fee," "[industry] AI tool," "software for [trade] business"
