### Market Context
- Vertical SaaS market CAGR ~23.9%, projected $157.4B by end of 2026
- AEC & Trades, Manufacturing, Legal are top 3 emergent verticals by VC deal momentum (Euclid Ventures)

- Global FSM software market: $4.43B (2022) → $7.3B projected by 2028
- Compliance software: $35.4B → $40.8B in 2026 alone

### What's Actually Getting Funded
- Mobile-first, AI-native tools for industries with paper/spreadsheet workflows
- Platforms that bundle scheduling + invoicing + payments + CRM (reduce app count)
- Tools built by founders with direct industry experience (roofer → CompanyCam, cardiologist → Abridge)
- All-in-one plays that become embedded infrastructure (switching costs = clinical/operational risk)

### Themes NOT Yet Saturated (White Space Summary)
1. Voice AI for trades dispatch and scope-of-work generation
2. HIPAA compliance tools for sub-50 employee healthcare practices at $29–49/mo
3. Affordable all-in-one for 1–5 tech HVAC/plumbing shops (below ServiceTitan's floor)
4. AI supplier quote agents for contractors (procurement automation)
5. Small landlord AI tools (lease review, rent control compliance, maintenance routing)
6. Mobile mechanic / 1-bay shop management (underserved below AutoLeap's target market)
7. Owner-operator trucking AI dispatch at $49–99/mo
