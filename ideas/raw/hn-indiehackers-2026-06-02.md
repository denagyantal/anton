## AI Voice Receptionist for Home Service Trades (Rosie AI / NeverMissHQ)

- **Source**: https://www.indiehackers.com/post/best-ai-receptionist-for-small-business-in-2026-top-5-compared-13ba10fa45
- **Additional Links**:
  - https://www.indiehackers.com/post/building-a-profitable-ai-voice-saas-agency-300-800-mrr-per-client-frAbgO1yQMfHOFFtY3gE
  - https://rosieai.com
- **Platform**: Indie Hackers
- **Type**: Product comparison / Revenue discussion
- **Engagement**: Featured as April 2026 article; AI voice SaaS post published Jan 2026
- **Revenue Data**: $300–800 MRR per client; ~80% margin. Rosie AI at $49–$149/mo per business. NeverMissHQ selling to HVAC/plumbing/roofing contractors. AI voice agency model: $800–2K setup + $300–$800/mo retainer vs ~$50–100/mo in costs.
- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5 — contractors, plumbers, HVAC technicians, electricians
- **Target Industry**: Home services trades (plumbing, HVAC, roofing, electrical, cleaning)
- **Core Value Prop**: Businesses miss 60–80% of inbound calls; every missed call loses a $500–2K job to a competitor. AI answering at $25–200/mo vs $2,800–3,500/mo for a human receptionist.
- **Gap/Opportunity**: Rosie AI specifically trained on home services call patterns (understands "water heater leaking" = urgent for plumber). Integrates with Jobber, ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro. But most AI receptionist tools are horizontal — the deep trade-specific tuning + native FSM integrations is the gap. NeverMissHQ markets as "virtual receptionist," never mentions AI.
- **Our Angle**: White-label AI receptionist with pre-built FSM integrations (Jobber, Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan) targeting a single trade vertical (e.g., HVAC only or pest control only) rather than all trades. Trade-specific emergency routing logic baked in. LTD would work as a setup-fee model.
- **LTD Potential**: 3/5 — recurring model is native, but LTD possible for lifetime access to base minutes

## Key Market Patterns Observed (2026)

1. **AI voice + trades** is validating fast — Rosie AI, NeverMissHQ, Smith.ai all targeting the same HVAC/plumbing/roofing market. Entry window still open for single-vertical specialists.

2. **Equipment inspection** is the next wave — multiple IH articles, growing demand in construction, industrial, medical. MaintainX is the known player but priced out of the solo-contractor/small-crew segment.

3. **PG/hostel management in emerging markets** — India specifically, but Southeast Asia too. WhatsApp-native workflows matter here.

4. **Micro-SaaS acquisition** is proving viable at $200–600K ARR — Noosa Labs confirms this. Several boring-vertical tools could be acquired rather than built.

5. **Auto repair** and **tattoo studio** management are the two most frequently mentioned "no good software" niches in HN underserved threads — both buildable with small teams.

6. **AV/event equipment rental** proved $15M+ ARR is achievable in a seemingly tiny niche — the Rentman pattern should be applied to adjacent rental markets (medical, party, industrial).
