## ZenMaid — Cleaning Business Scheduling SaaS

- **Source**: https://www.indiehackers.com/post/tech/from-a-cleaning-side-hustle-to-a-3m-yr-saas-for-cleaning-services-suhsqkDZB1zIwRmXxrFm
- **Additional Links**: https://get.zenmaid.com/
- **Platform**: Indie Hackers
- **Type**: Revenue milestone / Founder interview
- **Engagement**: 28 upvotes, 10+ comments (Feb 2025 IH feature)
- **Revenue Data**: $3M/yr ($250K/mo) ARR, bootstrapped for 11 years. Founded 2013.
- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5
- **Target Industry**: Residential cleaning / maid services
- **Core Value Prop**: Scheduling, operations, and client management software built exclusively for maid services — replaces spreadsheets and paper, not a generic CRM
- **Gap/Opportunity**: ZenMaid is 11 years old and still growing — the market hasn't been saturated. Competitors (Jobber, Housecall Pro) try to serve all home services and feel generic to cleaners. ZenMaid grew via word-of-mouth in a tight-knit industry community. Room for a modern AI-native competitor, or a hyper-niche version (e.g. "commercial cleaning only" or "move-out cleaning companies")
- **Our Angle**: AI-powered route optimization + instant customer rebooking + automated cancellation recovery. ZenMaid still uses a "boring" tech stack and has zero AI features — an AI-first modern competitor that handles the phone, reschedules missed appointments, and optimizes cleaner routes could steal customers
- **LTD Potential**: 4/5 — cleaning businesses pay $49-$150/mo and have very low churn once embedded

## Breezy — AI Workforce for HVAC / Plumbing / Electrical Contractors

- **Source**: https://getbreezyapp.com/
- **Additional Links**: https://getbreezyapp.com/blog/how-breezy-helped-popa-heating-and-cooling-streamline-operations-and-improve-customer-satisfaction

- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5
- **Target Industry**: Residential HVAC, plumbing, electrical (home services trades)
- **Core Value Prop**: AI agents that answer after-hours calls, collect homeowner info, auto-create jobs in the system for follow-up and scheduling — replacing the CSR role. Full scheduling + invoicing + dispatching for residential trade businesses.
- **Gap/Opportunity**: ServiceTitan costs $1K+/mo and is over-engineered for smaller shops (1-10 techs). Housecall Pro is mid-market but generic. ## Solo Contractor Estimate & Invoice Tool (Gap between ServiceTitan and Nothing)

- **Source**: https://superframeworks.com/articles/untapped-underserved-micro-saas-niches
- **Additional Links**: https://entrepreneurloop.com/bootstrapped-saas-niches-solo-founders/, https://www.nxcode.io/resources/news/micro-saas-ideas-2026
- **Platform**: Indie Hackers / Analysis
- **Type**: Validated market opportunity / multiple discussions
- **Engagement**: Referenced across multiple IH and analysis posts in 2025-2026
- **Revenue Data**: SBA Q3 2025 report: 89% of tradespeople lack adequate digital tools. Market pricing: ServiceTitan $1K+/mo, Jobber $50-150/mo. SoloBid concept: $19/mo targets solo operators below Jobber's floor.
- **Boring Business Score**: 5/5
- **Target Industry**: Solo electricians, plumbers, handymen, landscapers (1-3 person operations)
- **Core Value Prop**: Send a clean estimate from your phone in 60 seconds, collect a deposit, auto-follow-up, generate an invoice on job completion — nothing else. The solo tradesperson doesn't want dispatching or fleet management, just the money flow.
- **Gap/Opportunity**: The entire market is designed for 5+ tech businesses. ServiceTitan is $1K+/mo. Jobber at $50-150/mo still has features a solo plumber will never touch. There are ~2M solo trade operators in the US who use WhatsApp, text messages, and paper invoices because nothing simple exists at their price point ($19-29/mo). Indie hackers regularly note this gap but nobody has captured it at scale yet.
- **Our Angle**: Mobile-first "estimate in 30 seconds" flow. AI auto-fills job details based on a photo of the problem. One-tap deposit collection. No setup, no onboarding. Distribution: Facebook Groups for solo plumbers/electricians, r/Plumbing, r/electricians.
- **LTD Potential**: 5/5 — solo tradespeople have no subscription budget tolerance; a $59-$99 LTD would convert massively on AppSumo

- **Boring Business Score**: 3/5 — live events production is boring relative to tech, but not a "trade"
- **Target Industry**: Live events, conference production, AV technicians, broadcast studios
- **Core Value Prop**: Synchronized remote countdown timers that multiple crew members can see from their devices — replaces manually-synced stopwatches. One person controls the timer, everyone else sees it in sync.

Factory countdown timer (Andon board replacement) and construction site daily countdown (shift timing) are adjacent opportunities.
