## Meta-Signals & Cross-Cutting Themes

1. **AI phone answering for boring businesses** is the #1 trend of H1 2026, validated by Avoca's unicorn status. Every trade vertical needs a version of this.
2. **Vertical AI is eating horizontal SaaS** — specialist tools consistently achieve 3x retention vs. generic tools (tech-insider.org data).
3. **The $29–$79/mo gap** — enterprise tools are priced out of solo operators; free/cheap tools lack depth. The wedge is always: "we do ONE thing the expensive tool does, but only for [niche], for $49/mo."
4. **Paper-to-digital still has huge headroom** — 74% of residential roofers, 58% of mid-size PM firms still on paper or legacy. Execution risk is low because the customer is ready.
5. **Compliance and licensing automation** is an underserved niche with strong recurring revenue and low churn (license deadlines never go away).
6. **LTD opportunity** is strongest for: (a) tools with a clear one-time data import (roofing inspection app, dental pay calculator), (b) tools solving an annual/recurring pain (license tracker, IFTA), (c) tools with a "set up once, works forever" model.
