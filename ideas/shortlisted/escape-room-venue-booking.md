## One-Line Pitch
Purpose-built booking platform for escape rooms (and adjacent: dog groomers, music studios) — handles multi-room simultaneous bookings, team group sizing, difficulty matching, waiver collection, and automated reminders that generic tools (Acuity, Calendly) can't do cleanly.

## Problem
Escape room operators currently cobble together Bookeo, Fareharbor, or Acuity with manual processes. Generic calendar apps cannot handle:
- Multiple simultaneous room bookings (no room conflict detection)
- Team/group assignment by experience level or size
- Difficulty matching to group composition
- Built-in digital waiver collection before entry
- Automated "your adventure starts in 2 hours" reminder sequences

The result: overbookings, groups showing up without waivers, wrong rooms assigned to wrong groups. The same structural mismatch applies to dog groomers (breed-specific time slots, grooming history) and music studios (teacher availability + instrument/room coordination).

## Market Evidence
- $1.2B+ escape room industry globally; 5,000-20,000 facilities worldwide
- Generic booking system workarounds documented across Escape Room Operators Facebook groups
- Dog grooming: 150K+ salons in US; breed-specific appointment duration not supported by Acuity
- Music studios: teacher + room + instrument availability scheduling requires 3-tool workaround
- Existing tools (Checkfront, Bookeo, Fareharbor) are generic and over-featured for micro-venue operators

## MVP Scope (2-3 weeks)

1. **Multi-room booking grid** — visual availability calendar, room conflict detection, max group size per room
2. **Online booking flow** — group size selection → room recommendation → date/time → payment capture
3. **Digital waiver collection** — pre-built waiver templates, e-sign before arrival, store signed copies
4. **Automated reminders** — SMS/email 24h and 2h before booking; "add to Google Calendar" link
5. **Host dashboard** — today's bookings, upcoming groups, waiver status check-in
6. **Basic reporting** — occupancy by room, revenue by day/week/month
