# Pet Grooming Business Software — Score: 86/105

**Tier**: 1 (Strong Opportunity)
**Verdict**: BUILD
**Evaluation Date**: 2026-03-23

## One-Line Pitch
Grooming-specific booking and client management for solo and small salons — breed-aware scheduling, pet parent photo sharing, and "your pet is ready" notifications at $25/mo flat.

## Problem
Independent pet groomers and small grooming salons (1-3 groomers) are squeezed between generic booking tools that don't understand pets (Vagaro, Square Appointments — no breed notes, temperament tracking, grooming history) and enterprise multi-service pet care platforms (MoeGo $59-129/mo, Gingr custom pricing) that include boarding, daycare, and training features a grooming-only business doesn't need. No tool offers breed-specific scheduling intelligence — a Goldendoodle full groom takes 2-3 hours while a Chihuahua bath takes 30 minutes, but generic tools don't adjust time slots. Groomers use a chaotic mix of Square for booking, paper cards for pet notes, personal text messages for "your pet is ready" notifications, and Instagram for before/after photos.

## Market Evidence
- $10B+ US pet grooming industry; 63M+ dog-owning households
- MoeGo ($59-129/mo), DaySmart Pet ($29-129/mo), Gingr (custom) validate demand
- Vagaro ($25-85/mo) has pet grooming support but as bolted-on feature — no breed intelligence
- Massive FB communities: Dog Groomers United ~80K, Dog Grooming Network ~50K, Mobile Dog Grooming ~15K
- Before/after photo sharing creates natural viral marketing on Instagram
- Mobile groomers (van-based) are a growing segment needing phone-friendly solutions

## Must-Have Filters
- [x] Problem is real (groomers using 3-5 separate tools; no breed-aware scheduling exists)
- [x] Can build without deep domain expertise (booking + pet profiles + photos)
- [x] Market is not dominated by a single unbeatable player (MoeGo growing but targets multi-service)
- [x] Revenue potential > $10K MRR within 12 months (tens of thousands of independent groomers)

## Key Differentiators
1. $25/mo flat — designed only for grooming, no boarding/daycare/training bloat
2. Breed-aware smart scheduling (auto-suggests time slots based on breed/size/service type)
3. Pet parent communication built in (before/after photos, pickup alerts, grooming notes)
4. Smart waitlist with auto-fill from cancellations
5. Mobile groomer mode with route planning for van-based operations
6. 15-minute setup with grooming-specific templates

3. Pet grooming may be seasonal in some regions
4. Groomers may eventually need boarding/daycare features as business grows
