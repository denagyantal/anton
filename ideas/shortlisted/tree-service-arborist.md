## Product Concept: "ArborDesk"

**Pricing**: $79/mo flat (up to 5 users) — month-to-month, no annual required
**LTD**: $199 (up to 3 users, lifetime)

**Core features**:
1. Pre-built arborist job templates (pruning, removal, stump grinding, cabling) — ready out of the box, no configuration week
2. Per-tree history cards: species, DBH, work history, treatment log, hazard rating, photos
3. ANSI A300 / ISA standard inspection checklists on mobile
4. Offline-capable mobile app (works in areas without cell coverage)
5. QuickBooks Online two-way sync that actually works
6. Route planning for multi-stop residential/commercial jobs

**Key differentiators**:
- First job dispatched in under 30 minutes (vs ArboStar/SingleOps 6+ hour setup)
- Tree-specific enough to beat Jobber (per-tree records, ISA checklists)
- Under $100/mo to beat Arborgold on price
- Reliable QBO sync (vs SingleOps's documented sync failures)
- Offline-capable mobile (vs SingleOps mobile that loses data)
