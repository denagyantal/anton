# Chiropractic Practice Management

## Score: 75/105

## Verdict: EXPLORE FURTHER

## Overview
Affordable all-in-one practice management for solo chiropractors caught between overpriced chiropractic-specific software ($159-349/mo) and underpowered generic healthcare tools ($19-65/mo). Cloud-native with SOAP note templates, spinal diagrams, insurance billing, and clean modern UI.

## Pain Point
ChiroTouch (market leader with 36K+ providers) "nickle-dimes to death" — text reminders, intake forms, backup are all extra fees on top of $159-299/mo base. $1,700/yr prepaid support still has 1+ hour wait times. Rigid contracts with difficult cancellation. Solo chiropractors (~55-67% of practitioners) are caught between overpriced, complex chiropractic software and underpowered generic tools that lack SOAP templates, spinal diagrams, and insurance billing workflows.

## Target Market
- Solo chiropractors (~35-44K practitioners)
- Small group practices (2-3 providers)
- 65K+ chiropractic businesses in US
- $21.9B industry (IBISWorld 2026)
