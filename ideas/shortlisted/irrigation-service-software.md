## One-Line Pitch
Purpose-built route, scheduling, and customer management software for irrigation service contractors — the same model Skimmer ($5.7M ARR) used to dominate pool service, applied to irrigation.

## Problem
Skimmer (getskimmer.com) proved that pool service — a deeply boring, route-based maintenance business — supports $5.7M ARR with 7,000+ customers at ~$57/month average. Pool service and irrigation service are nearly identical workflows: technician drives a route, visits customer properties, performs maintenance service, takes notes on system status, and generates invoices.

Irrigation service companies (~50,000 in the US) have zero dedicated software. They manage:
- **Spring startup visits** — activate all irrigation zones, check heads, adjust pressure, repair winter damage; 2–4 hour visits for each customer
- **Summer check-in visits** — quick zone check, adjust timing for weather
- **Fall winterization** — blow out all zones with compressed air; highly time-compressed (everyone needs it done before first freeze)
- **Backflow preventer testing** — annual certification required by most municipalities; tracked per customer with certification records
- **Repair dispatching** — broken head, controller failure, zone not activating

Currently: paper route sheets, QuickBooks for invoices, phone calls for scheduling. No Skimmer for irrigation exists.

## Market Evidence
- Skimmer: $5.7M ARR, 7,000+ customers — proves the exact model works in adjacent service category
- ~50,000 irrigation service businesses in the US
- Pool service (Skimmer's market) has ~40,000 pool service companies — irrigation is comparable scale
- National Irrigation Association (IA) has 4,000+ member companies — organized professional community
- Seasonal compression (spring startup + fall winterization = 30-day rush) = urgent software need for route optimization

## Key Differentiators
1. **Irrigation-specific customer profiles** — property address, zone count, controller brand/model, head types, backflow location, winterization notes
2. **Seasonal work order templates** — spring startup / summer check / fall winterization / repair; each has its own checklist and typical invoice
3. **Backflow preventer certification tracking** — annual cert required by municipality; track per customer, alert 30 days before renewal, attach PDF cert
4. **Route optimizer for spring/fall rush** — cluster appointments by neighborhood during the compressed startup/winterization season
5. **Before/after photos per zone** — document head condition on spring startup; customer sees exact repair scope

## MVP Features (3–4 Week Build)

3. Route view (map of today's appointments)
