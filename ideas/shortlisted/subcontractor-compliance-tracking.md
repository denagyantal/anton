## Core Problem
General contractors managing 40-80 active subcontractors must track:
- **COIs (Certificates of Insurance)** — verify coverage, expiry dates, endorsements
- **Contractor licenses** — state licenses with expiry dates (vary by trade and state)
- **Lien waivers** — conditional/unconditional progress and final waivers
- **W-9s and tax documentation** — updated annually, required for 1099s
- **Safety certifications** — OSHA 10/30 cards, site-specific certs

This is **entirely manual today** — spreadsheets, email follow-ups, and administrative coordinators spending 20-30 hours/week chasing documents. One expired COI on a job site can invalidate the GC's insurance coverage — the stakes are real.

## Key Differentiators
1. **AI OCR document reading** — upload a COI PDF, AI extracts policy numbers, coverage amounts, and expiry dates automatically; no manual data entry
2. **Automated expiry alerts** — 90/60/30-day reminders sent to both GC admin and the subcontractor; tracks whether subcontractor responded
3. **Procore/Buildertrend integration** — plug-in to existing workflow, not a replacement; positions as a point solution, not a full PM platform
4. **Lien waiver tracking** — progress waivers tied to payment milestones; auto-chases missing waivers before funding
5. **Sub portal** — subcontractors self-upload documents; GC sees status dashboard in real time
