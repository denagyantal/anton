## One-Line Pitch
The missing piece for home inspectors: a referral CRM that tracks which real estate agents send business, auto-sends thank-yous, and shows inspectors exactly which agents to cultivate — growing revenue without growing ad spend.

## Problem
Home inspectors get 80%+ of their business from real estate agent referrals. Yet no inspection software has a built-in agent relationship manager. Spectora ($100+/mo) dominates report writing but has **zero CRM or referral-tracking features**. Inspectors track agent relationships in spreadsheets or their memory, missing systematic follow-up opportunities constantly.

The business is entirely referral-driven — an inspector who grows from 1 to 3 trusted agent relationships doubles their revenue. A tool that helps inspectors systematically grow their agent network is directly tied to revenue growth, making WTP high and ROI demonstrable.

## Market Evidence
- 30,000+ licensed home inspectors in the US
- Spectora growing rapidly ($100+/mo) — validates software spend in this niche
- Inspector Toolbelt ($69/mo) — scheduling + admin, no referral tracking
- HomeGauge (older interface, report-writing focus)
- Generic CRMs (HubSpot free, Pipedrive) — not inspection-aware, require manual setup
- No dedicated "home inspector referral CRM" exists at any price point
- NACHI (National Association of Certified Home Inspectors) forums show active discussion about finding/retaining real estate agent clients

## Key Differentiators
1. **After-inspection auto-trigger** — after each completed inspection, automatically sends a thank-you to the referring agent + logs the referral in the CRM
2. **Agent performance dashboard** — "Agent Smith sent 8 inspections this month, last referral: May 15" — shows inspectors who to cultivate vs. who is fading
3. **Monthly agent drip** — pre-built email templates to stay top-of-mind with agent's office (market update, seasonal tips, new service announcements)
4. **Top agent report** — monthly "your top 10 referral sources" email to inspector = motivates continued use
5. **Spectora integration** — import completed inspections directly to auto-populate agent activity

## MVP Scope (3–4 weeks)
1. Agent contact database with referral count and last-referral-date tracking
2. Post-inspection trigger: inspector marks job complete → system auto-emails thank-you to referring agent
3. Monthly dashboard email to inspector: top agents, referral trends, agents who haven't sent business in 90+ days
4. Drip campaign: pre-written monthly email templates to agent list with one-click send
5. Spectora webhook/CSV import (phase 2: real-time sync)
6. Price: $29/mo standalone CRM / $69/mo if bundled with basic inspection management (scheduling + reminders)
