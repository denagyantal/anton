# OSHA / Construction Safety Compliance Checklist for Small Subcontractors

**Score: 86/105** | **Verdict: EXPLORE FURTHER → BUILD** | **Tier**: 1 | **Evaluation Date**: 2026-05-20

## One-Line Pitch
Dead-simple OSHA inspection checklist app for subcontractors — mobile checklist in under 5 minutes, auto-PDF report, and certification expiry tracker at $29/month.

## Problem
OSHA penalties increased in 2025: max serious violation now $16,550/case; repeat violations $165,514/case. Small subcontractors (electricians, plumbers, roofers, concrete crews, 1–20 employees) face real enforcement pressure but have no affordable, simple tool. Enterprise options (Procore, SafetyCulture/iAuditor, SmartQHSE) are built for large GCs and priced accordingly. The key compliance tasks for a 5-person electrical sub are:
- Daily site safety inspection (OSHA requires documentation)
- OSHA-10/30 certification tracking per worker (with expiry dates)
- Equipment certification tracking (aerial lifts, forklifts, scaffolding)
- Incident reporting with required OSHA 300 log entries

AGC research confirms three adoption success factors: inspection takes <15 minutes on mobile, management reviews daily, one champion superintendent. Every existing tool fails at least one of these for small subs.

## Market Evidence
- OSHA penalties increasing annually (2025 update: $16,550 serious, $165,514 repeat)
- Construction has highest OSHA violation rates of any industry
- ~700,000 specialty subcontractors in US (electrical, plumbing, roofing, concrete, demolition)
- ExpiryEdge targets cert tracking specifically but lacks daily inspection workflow
- SafetyCulture has paying customers but priced out of small subs (enterprise focus)
- Raken has daily reports + safety but is broader and more expensive

## MVP Scope (2–3 weeks)

**Core features:**
1. Pre-built OSHA inspection checklists by trade (electrical, plumbing, roofing, concrete)
2. Mobile-first: complete inspection in under 5 minutes at job site
3. Auto-generates branded PDF report after inspection (company logo, date, inspector, site)
4. OSHA-10/30 certification tracker per worker with expiry alerts (email/SMS 30/60 days out)
5. Equipment certification tracker (aerial lifts, forklifts, scaffolding by serial number)
6. Basic incident log (required for OSHA 300 log entries)
7. Manager dashboard showing daily inspection status across job sites

**Phase 2:**
- Custom checklist builder (add trade-specific items)
- OSHA 300/300A/301 auto-population from incident log
- Toolbox talk templates (library of pre-written safety meetings)
- Subcontractor certificate request portal (GCs can request certs from subs)
