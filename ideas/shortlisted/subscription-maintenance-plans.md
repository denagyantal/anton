# Subscription Maintenance Plans for Trades

## Score: 85/105

## Verdict: BUILD — Quick Win

## Overview
Purpose-built tool for trades businesses (HVAC, plumbing, pest control, pool service) to create, sell, and manage subscription maintenance plans. "The Memberful for trades businesses." Helps contractors build recurring revenue through bronze/silver/gold tier maintenance plans with automated scheduling and billing.

## Pain Point
Home services businesses are shifting to subscription-based maintenance models for predictable revenue. HVAC companies sell annual maintenance plans, plumbers offer subscription drain cleaning, pest control sells quarterly treatments. But most manage these plans on spreadsheets or basic CRM tools not designed for maintenance schedules. ServiceTitan has membership features but at $200+/mo. No affordable, standalone tool exists for small trades businesses.

## Target Market
- HVAC companies (annual maintenance plans)
- Plumbing businesses (drain cleaning subscriptions)
- Pest control operators (quarterly treatment plans)
- Pool maintenance services
- Appliance repair companies
- Garage door companies
- Any trades business wanting predictable MRR
