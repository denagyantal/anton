## One-Line Pitch

Mobile inspection app with pre-built checklists for service businesses — photo + GPS + timestamp each checklist item → instant branded PDF report emailed to the customer at job completion. SafetyCulture at $24/user/mo vs. our $149 LTD for the whole crew.

## Problem

Field inspection software is a mature but fragmented category built for enterprise safety teams (SafetyCulture, Fulcrum, Formstack = 50+ users, per-seat pricing). Meanwhile, service crews of 2–10 people — pool cleaners, property inspectors, pest control techs, cleaning company supervisors, HVAC maintenance techs — are still using:
- Paper clipboards (no photo documentation)
- WhatsApp photos to the owner (no organized storage or client delivery)
- Google Forms (no GPS, no PDF output, no branding, no offline)

The specific gap: no vertical-specific inspection app owns "pool service condition report" or "janitorial inspection for building managers" with pre-built templates, flat pricing, and instant branded PDF delivery to the client.

Service businesses that provide inspection reports to their clients (pool owners, facility managers, building owners) win more contracts and retain clients longer. A pool company that sends a timestamped water chemistry + equipment condition report after every visit is much harder to switch away from than one that just texts "done."

## Product Concept

**"InspectRoute"** — Mobile inspection app with vertical-specific templates for service businesses.

**Entry niche: Pool service (start here)**

**Core MVP features (2–3 week build):**
- **Pre-built pool service template**: water chemistry (pH, chlorine, alkalinity, stabilizer), filter condition, pump/motor status, visual cleanliness, equipment leaks, safety/code items
- Photo capture per checklist item — automatic GPS + timestamp embedded
- **Offline mode** (work without cell service in remote areas)
- One-tap completion → **instant branded PDF report** emailed to pool owner
- Technician assignment per route/stop
- Client management: pool address, equipment list, contract type

**Expansion templates (Phase 2):**
- Commercial janitorial inspection (for facility managers)
- Pest control service visit log
- HVAC maintenance visit checklist
- Property management move-in/move-out condition report

**Pricing:**
- $29/mo — 1 technician, unlimited inspections, 1 template
- $49/mo — up to 5 technicians, all templates, client portal
- LTD: $99 — up to 3 technicians, all templates
- LTD: $149 — up to 10 technicians, unlimited templates

## Key Differentiators

1. **Vertical-specific pre-built templates** — pool service operators don't want to build a form from scratch; they want to tap "water chemistry" and have the fields already there
2. **Instant branded PDF to client email** — the report delivery is the product; no login required for client
3. **Offline first** — service crews often work in locations with poor connectivity
4. **"Proof of service" positioning** — not "inspection software" but "evidence that the job was done right" for contract protection

## Target Channels (Pool Service Entry)

- r/pools, r/swimmingpools (service professionals)
- "Pool Service Business Owners" Facebook groups (30K+ combined)
- PHTA (Pool & Hot Tub Alliance) professional community
- "Skimmer" competitor channel — pool service operators already buying software
- Search: "pool service inspection app", "pool technician checklist app", "pool service report software"

## Risks

1. **Google Forms is free and usable** — must demonstrate clear value over free (PDF branding + GPS + offline + client delivery)
2. **Horizontal threat**: SafetyCulture adds vertical templates; FastField expands pricing — moat depends on vertical depth not breadth
3. **Template customization demand**: every service business has slightly different needs; over-customization request volume could overwhelm support
4. **Low urgency**: unlike compliance (legal requirement), inspection reports are best-practice but not always mandatory
