## One-Line Pitch

The missing operating system for commercial cleaning companies (1–10 crews) — multi-crew scheduling, client portal with QA sign-off, OSHA SDS tracking, and flat-rate invoicing at $29–49/mo where ZenMaid left off.

## Problem

ZenMaid dominates residential cleaning at $200K MRR, but commercial cleaning is a fundamentally different operation: multi-crew scheduling across client locations, QA checklists per client site, OSHA Safety Data Sheet (SDS) management for cleaning chemicals, client portals for contract renewal and job sign-off, and recurring flat-rate invoicing on contracts.

No affordable, purpose-built tool exists for commercial cleaning companies with 1–10 crews. GorillaDesk, Jobber, and ServiceTitan are designed for field service trades (HVAC, pest control) with no commercial cleaning workflow context. ZenMaid is residential-only. Generic scheduling tools (Square, Housecall Pro) have no multi-crew shift logic or SDS management.

Commercial cleaning operators use spreadsheets for crew assignments, paper QA checklists that clients never see, and QuickBooks for invoicing — three disconnected systems for a business that should run on one.

## Market Evidence

- ZenMaid: $200K MRR (residential cleaning only) = proof of category; commercial is adjacent and underserved
- GorillaDesk/Jobber have no commercial cleaning context (no QA checklists, no SDS management, no client sign-off portal)
- ISSA (International Sanitary Supply Association): 9,000+ commercial cleaning member companies
- Commercial cleaning industry: $90B US market (2024), CAGR 6.3%
- 1M+ commercial cleaning businesses in the US; majority are 1–25 employee operations
- Capterra search for "commercial cleaning software" returns only generic FSM tools with no cleaning-specific features

## Boring Business Fit Check

- Industry: Commercial cleaning / janitorial services
- Recurring revenue model: Monthly contracts with flat-rate billing
- Non-discretionary need: Scheduling, quality control, and compliance are core operations
- Sweaty startup angle: Physical cleaning crews, real-world contracts, not digital work
- Franchise potential: Multi-location commercial cleaning is a franchised model (Jan-Pro, Coverall)

## Key Differentiators

1. **Multi-crew shift scheduling** with zone/location assignment and live crew check-in
2. **Client portal** with QA sign-off, before/after photos, and contract renewal
3. **OSHA SDS management** — chemical safety data sheets per product, accessible in field
4. **Recurring contract invoicing** with automatic billing and payment collection
5. **Quality assurance checklists** per client site, customizable per contract
