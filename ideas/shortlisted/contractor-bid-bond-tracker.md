## One-Line Pitch
Purpose-built dashboard for GCs managing 10–30 active bid securities — automated expiry alerts, renewal initiation, and white-label licensing for surety brokers.

## Problem
Construction contractors track bid bonds, performance bonds, and payment bonds manually — spreadsheets or email folders. Mid-size GCs managing 10–30 active bids at a time have no centralized visibility into which bonds expire when.

When a bid security expires before a project starts: the job is delayed, the contractor must re-issue the bond ($500–$5,000+ cost), and the client relationship is damaged. For GCs bidding on public/commercial work, this is an ongoing operational risk with zero purpose-built software addressing it.

Existing project management tools (Procore, Buildertrend) track project timelines but have no concept of bond lifecycle management.

## Market Evidence
- $2T US construction industry; all companies bidding public/commercial work must post bid securities
- Mid-size GCs (50–300 employees) typically have 10–30 active bid securities outstanding at any time
- No purpose-built bond tracking tool identified — all use spreadsheets or generic PM
- Surety brokers (who issue bonds) would benefit from white-label access to remind their contractor clients

## Core MVP Features
1. Bond list: project name, bond type, amount, surety, expiry date
2. Automated email reminders at 30/14/7 days before expiry
3. One-click renewal initiation (email template to surety broker)
4. Dashboard showing all active bonds with status (active / expiring soon / expired)
5. Simple document storage (PDF copies of bonds)

## Pricing
