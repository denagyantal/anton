## One-Line Pitch
Simple "brand standard" checklist + photo verification for micro-franchisors (5-20 locations) — franchisee completes daily/weekly checklist on phone, franchisor sees real-time compliance status dashboard; $99/mo unlimited locations.

## Problem
Micro-franchisors (5-20 locations in cleaning services, food trucks, tutoring centers, fitness studios) cannot afford FranConnect or Operandio ($500-2,000+/mo), but still need to enforce brand standards across locations. Current workaround: WhatsApp photos, shared Google Sheets, periodic in-person visits. Result: brand drift, inconsistent customer experience, compliance liability.

- Large franchise management platforms (FranConnect, Naranga) start at $500+/mo — built for 50-500+ unit franchisors
- Operandio (operations checklists) targets enterprise multi-location businesses
- Homebase, 7shifts (staff scheduling) don't cover brand compliance
- No tool purpose-built for the 5-20 location micro-franchisor segment

## MVP Scope (2-3 weeks)

1. **Checklist builder** — franchisor creates brand standard checklists (opening procedures, cleanliness standards, customer greeting scripts)
2. **Mobile franchisee app** — franchisee completes checklist daily, uploads photo evidence per item
3. **Franchisor dashboard** — real-time view: which locations completed today, which items failed/passed, photo evidence gallery
4. **Alert system** — notify franchisor when location misses checklist or fails critical items
5. **Compliance reporting** — weekly/monthly compliance score per location, trend view
6. **Template library** — pre-built checklists for cleaning services, food trucks, tutoring centers, fitness studios

## V2 Features

## Key Differentiators
1. Built for 5-20 locations (not 500+ like FranConnect/Naranga)
2. Photo evidence per checklist item — visual proof of compliance, not just checkboxes
3. Zero setup complexity — no training required for franchisee mobile app
4. Template library for common micro-franchise verticals (cleaning, tutoring, fitness, food truck)
5. Price: $99/mo vs $500+/mo for enterprise alternatives

## Top 3 Risks
1. **Narrow segment**: Micro-franchisors (5-20 locations) is a small total addressable market — needs adjacent expansion to multi-location independent businesses (restaurants, gyms)
2. **Discovery challenge**: Micro-franchisors don't congregate on Product Hunt or AppSumo — franchise association channels required
3. **Enterprise creep**: As operators grow to 20+ locations, they will look at FranConnect — must retain or upsell before they outgrow
