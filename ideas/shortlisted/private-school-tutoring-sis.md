# Private School / Tutoring Center SIS

**Score**: 77/105
**Verdict**: EXPLORE FURTHER
**Tier**: 1 (Strong Opportunity)
**Evaluation Date**: 2026-04-20

## One-Line Pitch
The first affordable SIS for small private schools and tutoring centers — enrollment management, attendance, grades, parent portal, and Stripe billing for $75-150/mo (vs. PowerSchool's $20K+/yr).

## Problem
Small private schools, charter schools, and tutoring centers are running student data in Excel, tracking incidents in notebooks, and managing tuition payments through a mix of Venmo, paper checks, and QuickBooks. Enterprise SIS platforms (PowerSchool, Infinite Campus) are priced for public school districts — completely inaccessible for a 50-student tutoring center. The gap between free (Google Classroom) and enterprise ($20K+) is entirely uncontested.

Pain score: 90/100 from Reddit community validation.

## Market Evidence
- 33,000+ private schools in the US
- 50,000+ tutoring centers
- PowerSchool priced at $20K+/yr — completely inaccessible for small schools
- Infinite Campus — district-only, not available to private schools
- $50-200/mo per institution is a completely uncontested price point
- Jackrabbit serves dance/gymnastics but not general education
- Direct quote: "Mess of student data in Excel, incidents tracked in notebooks" — validated Reddit complaint

## Must-Have Filters
- [x] Problem is real (Excel + notebooks + Venmo is the current state)
- [x] Can build without deep domain expertise (standard SIS features are well-understood)
- [x] No dominant player in $50-200/mo range
- [x] Revenue potential > $10K MRR ($100/mo × 100 schools = $10K MRR — conservative)

## Key Differentiators
1. $75-150/mo vs. $20K+/yr for PowerSchool — no-brainer for small institutions
2. Stripe-native tuition billing (not Venmo/checks)
3. Parent portal for attendance, grades, and payment
4. Incident tracking (replaces notebooks)
5. FERPA-compliant data handling

## Target Customers
- Primary: Tutoring centers (10-500 students)
- Secondary: Small private schools (K-12, under 500 students)
- Also: Language schools, music academies, coding bootcamps, enrichment programs

## Competitor Landscape
| Competitor | Price | Weakness |
|-----------|-------|----------|
| PowerSchool | $20K+/yr | Enterprise only; district-focused |
| Infinite Campus | District only | Not available to private schools |
| Jackrabbit | $45-259/mo | Dance/gymnastics only; not general education |
| Generic spreadsheets | Free | No parent portal, no billing, no reporting |

## Pricing
- **Starter**: $75/mo for up to 100 students
- **Growth**: $150/mo for 100-500 students
- **LTD**: $199 per institution (AppSumo)

## Next Steps
1. Validate willingness to pay vs. Google Classroom free tier (5 customer interviews)
2. Build enrollment + parent portal + tuition billing (Stripe) first — 3-4 week MVP
3. Add attendance tracking and grade/progress reports
4. Add incident logging and basic reporting
5. Launch on EdTech-focused communities + tutoring center FB groups

## Risks
1. Google Classroom free tier anchors price expectations for education buyers
2. Education community is slow to adopt new software
3. FERPA compliance requirement adds overhead (data residency, access controls)
4. Diverse institutional needs may require significant customization
