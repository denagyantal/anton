# Catering & Event Operations Management

## One-Line Pitch
The first sub-$100/mo BEO and operations platform for independent caterers — proposal-to-invoice workflow, recipe scaling, event staffing, and food cost tracking without enterprise bloat.

## Problem
Dedicated catering companies (not restaurants with a catering menu — standalone caterers doing weddings, corporate events, and social gatherings) are universally underserved. Every tool they're pushed toward is either:
- **Restaurant-focused POS software** (Square, Toast) that ignores their event-based workflow
- **Enterprise catering platforms** (Caterease $200–500/mo, Total Party Planner $149–299/mo) designed for hotel banquet operations with 50+ events/month

The independent caterer doing 3–8 events/week with $200K–$1.5M annual revenue runs everything in Word + Excel + QuickBooks — a fragile, error-prone stack.

**Core unmet needs:**
1. **BEO (Banquet Event Order)** — the master document for each event covering menu, quantities, staffing, setup/breakdown timeline, venue contact, client preferences, serving as both internal work order and client-facing confirmation. Currently done in Word templates.
2. **Recipe and food costing** — scaling a recipe from 20 to 175 guests with correct quantities and accurate food cost per head. Currently done in Excel.
3. **Event-specific staffing** — which servers/chefs are assigned to which event, with hours and pay calculated per event rather than weekly schedule.
4. **Deposit and balance collection** — 50% deposit at booking, final balance 7 days before event. Currently tracked in QuickBooks + calendar reminders.

## Product Concept: CaterFlow

**Core workflow ($49/mo up to 20 events/month / $89/mo unlimited):**
1. **Inquiry** → client fills out event details form (date, guest count, event type, venue, budget)
2. **Proposal** → menu + pricing per head, presented as a professional PDF
3. **Signed BEO** → auto-generated from the proposal; client signs digitally; includes full event details, staffing plan, timeline, delivery address
4. **Deposit invoice** → 50% automatically calculated; Stripe payment link
5. **Event prep** → recipe scaling auto-calculated from guest count; shopping list generated; staff assignment with hours logged
6. **Day-of execution checklist** → setup items, service milestones, breakdown tasks
7. **Final invoice + balance collection** → outstanding balance collected via Stripe
8. **Post-event review** → actual food cost vs. estimated; staff hours vs. budgeted; event profit margin

**Key differentiator**: BEO auto-generation from proposal (no re-typing) + recipe scaling integrated with food cost tracking = the two manual steps that cost caterers hours per event. No sub-$100/mo tool connects these workflows.

## MVP Scope (4–5 weeks)
1. Event creation with client details + guest count
2. Menu builder with per-item food cost tracking
3. Proposal generation (PDF output with pricing per head)
4. BEO auto-generation from proposal (client digital signature via link)
5. Deposit + final balance invoicing (Stripe)
6. Recipe scaling calculator (quantities auto-adjust to guest count)
7. Staff assignment per event with pay calculation
