## Product Concept

**"WireOps"** — electrical contractor management for 1–5 tech residential service companies.

**Core MVP (6–8 weeks):**
1. **Job scheduling & dispatch**: Customer intake → assign to tech → route to address → update status
2. **Electrical flat-rate pricebook**: Pre-loaded common residential calls (breaker replacement, panel upgrade, GFCI, outlets, switches) — each with flat customer price + labor time estimate; fully customizable
3. **Permit milestone tracker**: Permit applied → inspection scheduled → inspection passed → project closed; automated reminder when permit milestone is overdue
4. **Photo-documented work orders**: Before/after photos per job with checklist by work type; sent to customer automatically on job completion
5. **Customer texting & invoicing**: SMS quote link → customer approves → invoice on completion → Stripe payment

**Phase 2 (MRR upsells):**
- Material list auto-generation from job type (saves trip to supply house)
- QuickBooks sync for accountant handoff
- Multi-tech scheduling view and GPS tracking
- Service agreement / recurring PM contracts

**Pricing:**
- $79/mo — 1 tech, unlimited jobs
- $99/mo — up to 3 techs, flat team pricing
- $149/mo — up to 5 techs
- LTD: $79 (1 tech, lifetime) / $149 (up to 5 techs) — strong AppSumo play

## Key Differentiators

1. **Built-in electrical flat-rate pricebook** — pre-loaded with common residential service calls (customizable); no other affordable FSM has this for electricians
2. **Permit milestone tracker** — alerts when permit is due for inspection or overdue; no other tool below $245/tech/month tracks permit stages
3. **Transparent flat pricing** — $79/mo starts; no "request pricing" gate that erodes trust (the #1 complaint about FieldPulse in 2026)
4. **Solo electrician accessible** — no 5-tech minimum; no $5,000 setup fee; set up in 2 hours
