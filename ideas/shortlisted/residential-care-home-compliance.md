## One-Line Pitch
CQC-ready digital compliance tracker for small residential care homes (5-30 beds) — medication logs, incident reports, staff records, and inspection-ready PDF reports — replacing paper/spreadsheets at $79-149/mo.

## Problem
Small residential care homes (5-30 beds) face the same regulatory compliance requirements as large facilities (CQC inspection in UK, state licensing in US) but cannot afford enterprise solutions. The CQC (Care Quality Commission) introduced stricter digital record-keeping requirements in 2025, creating an urgent forcing function for digitization. Current reality:
- **Paper-based**: Most small care homes track medication administration records (MAR), incident reports, staff training records, and inspection checklists on paper or in spreadsheets
- **Enterprise gap**: PointClickCare and MatrixCare start at $500-2,000/mo and are designed for 100+ bed facilities
- **No affordable middle ground**: Nothing purpose-built exists for 5-30 bed facilities below $500/mo
- **Regulatory risk**: CQC digital requirements (2025) means paper-based tracking is now a compliance liability
- **UK-first opportunity**: 17,000+ care homes in the UK; CQC's 2025 digital mandate creates urgent demand; US market (65,000+ assisted living facilities) is the follow-on

## Market Evidence

## Core Feature Set (MVP)
1. **Medication Administration Records (MAR)** — daily logging, missed dose alerts
2. **Incident Report Logger** — record and track incidents with required fields for CQC
3. **Staff Training Records** — track qualifications, expiry dates, reminder alerts
4. **Inspection Checklist Generator** — CQC inspection-ready checklist with completion tracking
5. **Monthly PDF Report** — auto-generate inspection-ready summary report
6. **Regulatory Alerts** — push notifications for expiring training, overdue records
