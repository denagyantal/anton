## One-Line Pitch
Mobile-first pest control operations platform for 1–5 tech owner-operators — hands-free voice logging, AI route scheduling, license tracking, and AI training modules — replacing pen/paper and enterprise-only alternatives at $49/mo flat.

## Problem

"Pen and paper still dominates the industry." Existing platforms (ServiceTitan, FieldRoutes/ServiceTitan-owned, PestPac) are expensive and built for enterprise. Solo/small pest control operators (1–5 techs) have no modern, affordable tool. Key pain points:

1. **Hands-free CRM data entry**: Pest control techs drive between jobs and input records simultaneously — a safety and UX problem that no current tool addresses. Voice-first input is the obvious solution.
2. **License/regulatory complexity**: EPA certifications, state pesticide application licenses, and CE credits all require annual renewal. Missing renewal = cannot operate legally. No affordable tool tracks this per-employee.
3. **AI-assisted scheduling**: Pest control routes have seasonal spikes (ants/mosquitoes in spring, rodents in winter). No affordable tool does route optimization for recurring treatment schedules.
4. **In-app upsell prompts**: Techs are on-site and miss upsell opportunities (exclusion work, termite prevention, recurring plans). AI prompting based on property history is unaddressed at the small-operator level.
5. **AI training modules**: Founder cut licensing exam time to 13 days (company record) using an internal GPT. This is an unaddressed product feature for operators trying to scale.

## Product Concept: "RouteShield"

**Pricing**: $49/mo flat for up to 5 techs | Month-to-month
**LTD**: $79 (solo), $149 (up to 5 techs)

**Core features**:
1. **Voice-first job logging** — tech says "completed, saw German cockroaches in kitchen, applied gel bait" → AI structures into service record
2. **Recurring route management** — set up quarterly/monthly/bi-monthly schedules; auto-generates upcoming jobs for each property
3. **Mobile-first field app** — service history per property, previous treatment notes, customer access codes, product usage log
4. **License & cert tracking** — upload EPA cert, state pesticide license, employee OSHA cards → automated 90/60/30-day expiry reminders
5. **AI upsell prompts** — tech on-site gets prompted: "This property hasn't had a termite inspection in 18 months — suggest?"
6. **AI training module** — built-in flashcard quiz system for licensing exams (state-specific pesticide law, pest ID, application rates)

**Key differentiators**:
- Voice logging = only pest control tool designed for hands-free input while driving
- Flat per-company pricing (not per-tech) = grows with them
- License vault built-in (not an add-on)
- AI training modules = reduces new tech onboarding from weeks to days
