# Gun Shop POS with ATF Bound Book Compliance

**Score**: 80/105 | **Tier**: 1 | **Verdict**: BUILD | **Evaluation Date**: 2026-05-03

## One-Line Pitch
The first modern cloud POS for FFL dealers — ATF-compliant A&D record keeping, 4473 storage, and serialized inventory at $49/mo vs. $200+/mo legacy tools.

## Problem
60,000+ licensed FFL (Federal Firearms License) dealers in the US are legally required by the ATF to maintain an A&D (acquisitions & dispositions) bound book — a serialized record of every firearm that enters and leaves their shop. The only affordable cloud option today (FastBound, $29–99/mo) has dated UI and limited POS integration. Every other compliant system (Celerant, Lightspeed, Rapid Gun Systems, Orchid POS) is enterprise-priced ($200–500+/mo). Most small dealers use outdated desktop software or paper-and-Excel, creating compliance risk.

## Product Concept: FFLDesk

**Core features ($49/mo or $149 LTD + annual compliance update):**
1. ATF-compliant A&D bound book — digital serialized record of all acquisitions and dispositions
2. ATF Form 4473 electronic storage and retrieval
3. Serialized inventory tracking (every firearm with make, model, caliber, serial)
4. Basic POS — sales, layaways, consignments, transfers (Form 4473 trigger)
5. NICS check logging and status tracking
6. Compliance alerts (bound book discrepancies, 4473 retention requirements)

**Key differentiator**: First mobile-friendly, modern-UI cloud FFL compliance tool at an independent dealer price point. Regulatory compliance requirement = built-in customer qualification.

## MVP Scope (6–8 weeks)
1. A&D bound book (digital, searchable, printable for ATF inspection)
2. Serialized inventory with status tracking (in stock / transferred / lost)
3. 4473 PDF generation and storage
4. Basic POS: cash sales, layaways, background check status log
5. ATF audit report generation
