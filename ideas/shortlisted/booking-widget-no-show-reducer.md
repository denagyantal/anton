# Booking Widget + No-Show Reducer for Local Services

## Score: 83/105

## Verdict: BUILD — Quick Win

## Overview
Lightweight, niche-specific booking widget that embeds on any business website with SMS reminders and deposit collection to reduce no-shows. One line of code to embed, customers book and pay deposit, automated SMS reminders at 24hr and 2hr. Customizable per industry (vehicle type for detailers, service area for plumbers, chair selection for barbers).

## Pain Point
Local service businesses lose 10-30% of revenue to no-shows. Plumbers, dentists, salons, auto detailers, and cleaning services all suffer. SMS reminders reduce no-shows by 26-38%. Most use generic calendar tools (Google Calendar, Calendly) that lack SMS confirmations, deposits, and industry-specific booking flows. Full FSM platforms include booking but at $100+/mo. No standalone, embeddable booking widget with SMS + deposits at sub-$50/mo.

## Target Market
- Any local service business — plumbers, dentists, salons, auto detailers, cleaning services, HVAC, landscapers
- Sweet spot: solo operators and 2-5 person teams who have a website but no booking system
- Businesses losing $500-5,000/mo to no-shows
