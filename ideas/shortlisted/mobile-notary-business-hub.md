## One-Line Pitch

All-in-one business management for mobile notary signing agents — order intake from title companies, calendar sync, per-assignment fee tracking, GPS mileage log, and quarterly tax estimate in one $29/mo app instead of 5 disconnected tools at $140+/month.

## Problem

Mobile notaries and loan signing agents travel to borrowers' homes to witness mortgage closings. They are independent contractors managing their own business but currently pay $140+/month across 5 disconnected apps that don't integrate:

- **Scheduling**: Google Calendar (manual entry from signing platform emails)
- **Mileage tracking**: MileIQ ($5–10/mo)
- **Invoicing/payment**: Square or Wave ($0–20/mo)
- **Document storage**: Dropbox ($10–20/mo)
- **Order management**: Manual spreadsheet (from multiple title companies)
- **Tax tracking**: Separate spreadsheet for 1099-NEC income

The work requires:
- Managing assignments from multiple title companies (often 5–10 different signing platforms)
- Tracking per-assignment fees ($75–200 per signing)
- Calculating per-mile reimbursement (IRS standard rate)
- Generating W-9-compliant income reports for quarterly taxes
- Chasing non-paying title companies for overdue fees

**CloseWise** is the emerging platform ($49–99/mo) but still young with limited integrations. Most notaries piece together generic apps because no product covers the full workflow.

## Market Evidence

- ~5M commissioned notaries in the US; ~200K+ active loan signing agents (subgroup who witness mortgage closings for income)
- Growing with real estate volume; notary income tracking is a consistent pain mentioned in every notary Facebook group
- CloseWise at $49–99/mo validates willingness to pay for dedicated software
- Notary Cafe (largest notary forum) and Signing Agent forums are very active and word-of-mouth driven
- "Must-have apps for mobile notary" blog posts exist = people are searching for solutions
- Real estate volume dipped 2022–2024 but is rebounding; new agents entering the market as rates ease

## Key Differentiators

1. **Order intake unification** — link with major signing platforms (SnapDocs, SigningOrder, ServiceLink) via webhook; orders auto-appear in calendar
2. **Per-assignment fee tracking** — track paid vs. unpaid by title company; flag overdue invoices; one-click payment chase email
3. **GPS mileage auto-track** — opens automatically when you start driving to a signing; calculates reimbursable miles at IRS standard rate
4. **Quarterly tax estimate** — real-time 1099-NEC income tracker with estimated federal + state quarterly payment due
5. **Annual income report** — professional PDF for CPA / self-preparation; W-9 compliant
