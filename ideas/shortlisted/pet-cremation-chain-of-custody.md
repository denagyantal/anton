## One-Line Pitch
QR-code chain-of-custody tracking from pet pickup to urn delivery — with real-time SMS status updates to grieving families — for the underserved pet cremation market.

## Problem
Pet cremation is a fast-growing sub-market driven by pet humanization trends. Families increasingly demand **proof of individual cremation** — they want to know their pet wasn't mixed with others. This creates a critical need for chain-of-custody tracking software. The current options:

- **Beloved Pet Software**: The only dedicated pet cremation tracker, but limited in features and aging. Covers flame-based and aquamation but hasn't innovated in years.
- **Funeral home software** (Gather, Passare): Designed for human remains, not pet workflows.
- **Spreadsheets and paper logs**: Still dominant across small pet cremation facilities.

The gap: No modern, mobile-first, affordable chain-of-custody SaaS exists for the pet cremation market. The broader funeral software market is consolidating (PlotBox acquired Batesville's HMIS Oct 2025; Guidant launched digital cemetery commerce Oct 2025), creating urgency for specialty niches to build standalone tools before being absorbed.

## Market Evidence
- Pet cremation industry growing rapidly — pet humanization trend driving premium pet aftercare
- Beloved Pet Software exists but is aging and limited — confirms demand but leaves room for modern replacement
- Market consolidation (PlotBox, Guidant acquisitions) signals the window is open for 2-3 years before specialty niches get absorbed
- Funeral home software market consolidating around 2-3 players (Gather, Passare dominate human remains)
- High willingness to pay: pet cremation facilities typically charge $150-500+ per cremation, so $99/mo SaaS is trivial cost

## Product Concept
**PetRest** or **PawTrack** — mobile-first chain-of-custody SaaS:

1. **Intake**: Scan QR code on intake bag → attach pet info (name, breed, owner contact, service type)
2. **Workflow tracking**: Staff scans QR at each step — pickup, intake, freezer, retort, processing, packaging, delivery
3. **Family portal**: Real-time SMS status updates to grieving family ("Your beloved Max is now in our care" → "Max's cremation is complete" → "Max is ready for pickup")
4. **Certificate generation**: Auto-generate Certificate of Individual Cremation with timestamps
5. **Audit trail**: Full chain-of-custody log for liability protection + family peace of mind
6. **Pricing**: $99/mo per facility or $799 LTD
