## One-Line Pitch
Dispatch-first platform for small local temp staffing agencies — worker availability calendar, shift dispatch board, SMS shift confirmation, timesheet aggregation, and weekly client invoicing at $149/month for up to 100 active workers.

## Problem
Small local temp staffing agencies (light industrial, warehouse, hospitality, general labor) are caught between enterprise staffing ATS/VMS platforms and generic hiring tools. Bullhorn is the industry standard but targets agencies with 10+ recruiters and costs $6,000–$15,000+/year. The result: small agencies (1–5 dispatchers, 50–300 active workers) run on spreadsheets, WhatsApp, and phone calls.

The specific workflow pain for a temp/labor agency (distinct from professional recruiting):
1. **Shift dispatch** — workers are placed by shift, not by job. Maria works Site A Monday, Site B Tuesday, Site C Wednesday–Friday. Most ATS tools track "placements" not "shifts."
2. **Worker availability management** — temps indicate available days/times each week; matching open shift requests against available workers requires a dedicated dispatch view
3. **Timesheet aggregation** — each client site has workers clocking in/out; the agency must aggregate all timesheets by client for weekly invoicing
4. **DNR flags** — clients can request a worker not be sent back; agencies track this in spreadsheets; no cheap tool has this built in

Existing affordable tools: Zelos ($0–$49/mo) covers basic shift dispatch but lacks invoicing and CRM. AkkenCloud is mid-market with opaque pricing. No tool at $149/month covers the full dispatch-to-invoice workflow for temp/labor agencies.

## Market Evidence

## Key Differentiators
1. **Worker availability calendar** — workers self-update via SMS/app each week
2. **Shift dispatch board** — drag-drop workers onto open client shifts by date/location
3. **SMS shift confirmation** — worker replies Y/N; no-shows flagged automatically
4. **Timesheet aggregation** — all timesheets by client, auto-rolled up for weekly invoicing
5. **DNR flag** — per client-worker pair (unique feature that signals domain expertise)
6. **Auto-generated client invoice** — from confirmed timesheets every week

## Pricing
- $149/month: Up to 100 active workers, unlimited shifts
- $249/month: Up to 300 active workers
- $149 LTD: Core dispatch + invoicing (no SMS confirmation infrastructure)
