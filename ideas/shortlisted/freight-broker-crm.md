## One-Line Pitch
Freight broker operations hub for 1–10 broker shops: load cards, carrier insurance cert auto-alerts, load-level P&L, and one-click rate confirmation PDFs — at $49–99/mo between Excel and McLeod.

## Problem
Independent freight brokers (1–10 brokers at small/startup brokerages) match shippers with carriers and earn a margin. Their work is relationship-heavy and margin-intensive — tracking commissions, carrier compliance, and load-level P&L is done manually in Excel for most small brokerages.

**Specific pain points:**
1. **Insurance cert tracking**: Carriers must have active cargo + liability insurance; expired certs = brokerage liability. Tracking 50–300 carrier certs in a spreadsheet with manual expiry reminders is error-prone. One expired cert = potential six-figure lawsuit.
2. **Load-level P&L**: Knowing exact margin on load #5432 in real time (including fuel surcharges, accessorials, payment timing)
3. **Customer credit exposure**: Small brokerages get burned by customers who order loads but don't pay
4. **Rate confirmation PDFs**: Generating carrier confirmation + customer rate confirmation PDFs for every load takes 15–20 minutes of paperwork

Enterprise TMS tools (McLeod $500–5K+/mo, MercuryGate, Turvo) are built for 50+ broker operations. The gap is specifically at 1–10 brokers, pre-scale.

## Product Concept (MVP: 4–5 weeks)

**Core features:**
1. **Load cards**: Shipper, carrier, origin/destination, equipment type, customer/carrier rates, load status
2. **Carrier database**: Insurance cert upload + auto-alert 30/60 days before expiry; active/inactive status
3. **Load-level margin**: Real-time P&L per load (customer rate minus carrier rate minus accessorials)
4. **Rate confirmation PDFs**: One-click generate carrier confirmation + customer rate confirmation with load details
5. **Customer credit tracking**: Credit limit per customer, current exposure, overdue invoices
6. **Commission report**: Weekly/monthly commission by broker

**Phase 2:**
- Load board integration (DAT, Truckstop) for carrier search
- Stripe invoicing for customer billing
- Factoring company integration (OTR Capital, RTS Financial)
