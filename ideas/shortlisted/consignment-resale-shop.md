# Consignment & Resale Shop Inventory with Auto-Decay Pricing — Score: 82/105

**Verdict**: BUILD

## One-Line Pitch
Consignment-specific POS + inventory with auto-decay pricing engine, consignor portal, and automated payout calculations — replacing parallel spreadsheets for $39-59/mo.

## Problem
Consignment shops operate on contracts that require automatic price reductions at set intervals (20% off after 30 days, 50% off after 60 days, return to consignor after 90 days). No POS or inventory system handles auto-decay pricing natively. Shop owners run parallel spreadsheets tracking consignor agreements, current markdown status, and payout calculations. Month-end consignor payout calculations across hundreds of items take hours. The resale market is $350B+ globally (growing 25% YoY) but the software is stuck in the spreadsheet era.

## Market Evidence
- 25,000+ consignment/resale shops in US
- Secondhand market growing 25% YoY; ThredUp reports 70% of consumers shopped secondhand in 2025
- SimpleConsign ($79-199/mo, dated interface), ConsignPro (desktop-only, legacy), Ricochet (expensive, enterprise)
- Consignment Facebook groups (50K+ members) constantly ask for better software
- Generic POS systems (Shopify, Square) require manual workarounds for consignment workflows
