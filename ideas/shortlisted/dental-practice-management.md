- [x] Problem is real

- [x] Revenue potential > $10K MRR

## Key Consideration
This idea has the highest ceiling but also the highest complexity. A communication-layer add-on (appointment reminders, patient texting, recall campaigns) may be a better MVP entry point than a full PMS.

## Next Steps
1. Assess HIPAA compliance cost/effort for MVP
2. Determine if communication-layer add-on is a better entry point
3. Research AI insurance verification as standalone wedge product
4. Talk to dental practice managers about their top 3 pain points

## Risks
1. HIPAA compliance adds significant build time and ongoing costs
2. Archy ($47M funded) and Isaac PracticeOS are well-funded competitors
3. Extremely high switching costs — dentists won't switch PMS easily
4. Domain expertise required for insurance billing, charting, treatment planning
