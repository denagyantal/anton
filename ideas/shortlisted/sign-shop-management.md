## One-Line Pitch
Sign-shop-specific job management with a substrate pricing calculator — the feature ShopVOX users actually need, at half the price after ShopVOX's 70% price hike.

## Problem
Sign shops (vinyl signs, vehicle wraps, banners, trade show displays, architectural signage) run complex per-order production workflows. Every job has unique dimensions, materials, substrates, and multi-step production (design → proof → print → cut/weld → install). ShopVOX — the category leader — raised prices from $215/month to $366+/month, a 70%+ increase with no meaningful new functionality.

Specific user complaints:
- "Too many clicks to process jobs"
- "Export function doesn't include line items and notes for quotes"
- "Steep learning curve for new team members"

Printavo is the main alternative but focuses on screen printing/apparel, not signage. The core unmet need is a job-flow system that understands signage-specific production: substrate cost calculation (square footage × material cost per sq ft + waste factor), cut-time estimation, install scheduling, and permit tracking for exterior sign jobs.

Small sign shops (2–10 employees, $300K–$2M revenue) do all of this manually in spreadsheets after outgrowing free tools but can't justify $350+/month.

## Key Differentiators
1. **Substrate pricing calculator** — select material type (vinyl, coroplast, aluminum, ACM) → input dimensions → auto-calculate cost with bleed and waste factor; eliminates 30 min of manual math per quote
2. **Sign-specific job stages** — design → proof approval → approved → print → cut → install (not generic "in progress / done")
3. **Permit flag for exterior sign jobs** — one checkbox flags jobs that require a city permit; separate tracking of permit status
4. **Install crew mobile app** — crew arrives on-site, checks off job, photographs completed install, syncs to customer record
5. **ShopVOX CSV migration tool** — one-click import of existing customer and job data

## MVP Features (4–5 Week Build)

4. Customer portal for proof approval (client approves digital proof before production)
5. Invoice on completion with QuickBooks sync
