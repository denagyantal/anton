# Tax Professional Client Portal & Workflow

**Score**: 82/105 | **Tier**: 1 | **Verdict**: BUILD | **Evaluation Date**: 2026-03-07

## One-Line Pitch
Modern client document collection, automated reminders, and e-signatures for tax preparers at $99-199 LTD vs $3K+/season incumbents.

## Problem
61.5% of tax practitioners say they overpay for software (AICPA 2025). Drake Tax support collapsed — busy signals, AI routing to dead phone numbers since April 2025. Lacerte charges $3K+/season with per-return add-on fees. Client portals are clunky, causing abandoned workflows. Tax pros spend hours on copy-paste follow-up emails chasing documents. Two-way SMS shows 8x response rates vs email.

## Market Evidence
- AICPA 2025 survey: 61.5% say they overpay
- Thomson Reuters survey: only 37% "very satisfied" with tech stack
- Drake Tax: 10+ year users "frustrated more than usual" since April 2025
- Tax prep is seasonal but professionals buy tools year-round
- Two-way SMS for document collection: 8x response rate vs email

## MVP Scope (4-5 weeks)
1. Secure document upload portal (per-client)
2. Automated SMS + email reminder campaigns ("We still need your W-2")
3. Two-way SMS client communication
4. E-signature integration (DocuSign or BoldSign)
5. Client checklist tracking (what's received, what's missing)
6. Basic dashboard for tax preparer

## Go-to-Market
1. r/taxpros community engagement
2. Accounting FB groups and forums
3. Launch timing: Q4 2026 (before tax season 2027)
4. Content: "Drake Tax alternative" and "modern tax client portal" SEO
5. $99-199 LTD (or $29-49/mo recurring)

## Risks
1. Highly seasonal — 70%+ usage in Jan-Apr
2. Tax pros are change-averse during busy season
3. Must complement (not replace) existing tax prep software
