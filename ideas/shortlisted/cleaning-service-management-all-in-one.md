# Cleaning Service Management All-in-One

**Score**: 86/105 | **Tier**: 1 | **Verdict**: BUILD | **Evaluation Date**: 2026-03-07

## One-Line Pitch
Per-job (not per-seat) cleaning business OS: scheduling, checklists, completion photos, invoicing at $69-99 LTD.

## Problem
Cleaning businesses (residential, commercial, Airbnb turnover) use fragmented tools. ZenMaid charges per-seat ($4-24/seat), punishing businesses with part-time cleaners. BookingKoala had a trust-destroying March 2025 outage. 40%+ of cleaning businesses still use paper/WhatsApp. No single tool handles scheduling + per-room checklists + completion photos + invoicing + tip tracking.

## Market Evidence
- $90B cleaning industry
- ZenMaid: validated market at $19-49/mo + per-seat fees
- Onedesk: $100K+ MRR, bootstrapped cleaning SaaS (Indie Hackers)
- 40%+ still paper-based — massive greenfield
- "WhatsApp chaos" documented as dominant communication method in cleaning

## MVP Scope (4-6 weeks)
1. Recurring schedule management (weekly/biweekly/monthly)
2. Per-room checklists with completion photos
3. Client portal for booking and communication
4. Automated invoicing (per-job pricing)
5. Tip tracking and crew pay calculation
6. Airbnb turnover integration (iCal sync)

## Go-to-Market
1. AppSumo launch at $69 LTD (solo) / $99 LTD (teams)
2. Cleaning business FB groups (50K+ members across several groups)
3. Content: "How to start a cleaning business" guides with software recommendations
4. Key differentiator: per-job pricing, NOT per-seat

## Risks
1. Crowded market — need sharp positioning on per-job pricing
2. Part-time/seasonal workforce creates complex scheduling
3. Airbnb cleaning is high-churn segment
