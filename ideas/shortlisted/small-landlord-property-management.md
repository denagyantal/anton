## Must-Have Filters
- [x] Problem is real (evidence of people paying for solutions)
- [x] We can build an MVP without deep domain expertise
- [x] Market is not dominated by a single unbeatable player

## Boring Business Fit
- [x] VCs starting to notice (Cambio $18M) but small landlord segment still ignored
- [x] Many customers are non-technical side-hustle landlords
- [x] Existing software is either too expensive or too limited
- [x] Real budgets (rental income funds software easily)
- [x] Low churn — landlords don't switch frequently

## Team Fit (4 co-founders)
- **Front-end**: Tenant portal, maintenance request flow, dashboard with financials
- **Back-end**: Payment processing (ACH rent collection), accounting engine, maintenance ticketing
- **Designer**: Dual UX — landlord dashboard + tenant-facing portal, mobile-responsive
- **Generalist**: BiggerPockets community engagement, comparison content, landlord podcast outreach

## Key Differentiators
1. **Transparent flat pricing** — no hidden fees (DoorLoop complaints), no minimum unit counts (AppFolio 50-unit floor)
2. **Complete at low price** — the "Goldilocks" solution between free-incomplete and expensive-bloated
3. **Mobile app** — Avail and Hemlane lack proper mobile apps
4. **Simple setup** — Buildium has steep learning curve; import from spreadsheet in minutes
5. **No post-acquisition degradation** — Avail (Realtor.com acquisition) suffered 300%+ price increase

## MVP Feature Set
1. Tenant portal (maintenance requests, document sharing, communication)
2. Online rent collection (ACH via Stripe)
3. Maintenance request ticketing with photo upload
4. Basic accounting (income/expense tracking, P&L by property)
5. Lease management (digital signing, expiration alerts)
6. Tenant screening integration
7. Owner dashboard with key metrics
8. QuickBooks export

## LTD Pricing Strategy
- **Tier 1**: $59 LTD — up to 10 units
- **Tier 2**: $99 LTD — up to 50 units
- **Tier 3**: $149 LTD — unlimited units
- **Post-LTD**: $15/mo (up to 10 units) / $29/mo (up to 50) / $49/mo (unlimited)
