# Small Landlord Property Management (1-50 Units)

**Score: 95/105** | **Verdict: BUILD** | **Evaluation Date: 2026-03-05**

## Concept
All-in-one landlord tool for self-managing landlords with 1-50 units. Online rent collection (ACH + card), digital lease signing, tenant screening, maintenance request portal, expense tracking, and year-end Schedule E tax report. Designed for the person who manages their own properties, not a PM company. Flat pricing, no per-unit fees.

## Target Customer
Self-managing landlords with 1-20 units, house hackers, small real estate investors, accidental landlords. The BiggerPockets community. Currently using Venmo/Zelle for rent, Google Docs for leases, spreadsheets for tracking.

## Key Differentiators
1. **Flat pricing per portfolio** — not per unit
2. **Built for landlords** — not property management companies
3. **Tax-ready** — generates Schedule E data, exports to TurboTax/CPA
4. **AI maintenance triage** — tenant photos → AI diagnoses → contractor dispatch with cost estimate
5. **Mobile-first** — manage properties on the go
