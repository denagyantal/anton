# Auto Detailing Business Management — Score: 93/105

**Verdict**: BUILD
**Tier**: 1 (Strong Opportunity)

## One-Line Pitch
Mobile detailing booking + vehicle CRM with before/after photos and recurring client management at $49/mo — built for the detailer, not the repair shop.

## Problem
Mobile and shop auto detailing businesses (500K+ in the US) use a patchwork of Square for payments, Google Calendar for scheduling, and text messages for client communication. Existing FSM tools (Jobber, HCP) are built for multi-tech trade businesses, not solo or 2-person detailing operations that need: route-aware scheduling clustered by geography, vehicle-specific service history with before/after photo documentation, recurring client reminders (90-day redetail nudges), product inventory tracking (coatings, chemicals), and simple mobile invoicing with tip support. No purpose-built tool exists below $100/mo.

## Market Evidence
- 500K+ auto detailing businesses in US; majority solo or 2-person operations
- Average detailer charges $150–400/detail job; mobile detailing growing 8%+ annually
- Jobber/HCP: $49–249/mo, built for multi-tech, overkill for solo detailer
- Vagaro/Booksy: salon-focused, no vehicle photo documentation
- DetailPRO and similar tools: outdated, desktop-heavy, no mobile-first design
- Auto detailing white space explicitly identified in competitor analysis (June 2026)

## Key Differentiators
1. Vehicle CRM with make/model/VIN history and service log per vehicle
2. Before/after photo capture with side-by-side comparison (marketing content generation)
3. Geography-clustered route scheduling (reduce drive time between mobile jobs)
4. Recurring redetail reminders (90-day, 6-month, annual outreach automation)
5. Product inventory tracking (ceramic coating stock, chemicals, consumables)
6. Tip-enabled mobile invoicing via Stripe

## Risks
1. Low switching cost — Square/Google Calendar is "free enough" for minimal ops
2. Mobile detailing is weather-dependent (seasonal revenue risk)
3. Larger FSM tools (Jobber/HCP) could add vehicle photo feature
