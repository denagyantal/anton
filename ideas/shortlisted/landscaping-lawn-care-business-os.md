# Landscaping / Lawn Care Business OS

**Score**: 85/105 | **Tier**: 1 | **Verdict**: BUILD | **Evaluation Date**: 2026-03-07

## One-Line Pitch
Route-based scheduling, seasonal pricing, and chemical tracking for lawn care pros at $69-99 LTD.

## Problem
Landscapers and lawn care operators need route-based scheduling (not generic appointment booking), recurring job templates, seasonal pricing adjustments, and crew management. Existing tools are either generic (Jobber — not lawn-specific), dated (Yardbook — free but limited), or complex (Service Autopilot). $130B+ industry with operators grossing $5K-50K/month who won't pay $300/mo for SaaS but will pay $29-79/mo.

- r/lawncare 400K+ members, LawnSite forums extremely active
- Yardbook (free) validates demand but dated interface limits growth

## MVP Scope (4-6 weeks)
1. Route-based scheduling with recurring job templates
2. Crew assignment and daily route sheets
3. Seasonal pricing adjustments (spring/summer rates)
4. Before/after photo documentation
5. Invoicing with auto-billing for recurring clients
6. Chemical/fertilizer application logs (compliance)
