## Concept
Cloud-based shop management for independent mechanics and 1-3 bay shops. Estimates and work orders in 60 seconds. Built-in DVI (photo/video inspections sent via text). Customer texting for approvals. Parts search integration. Profitability dashboard. Native QuickBooks sync. Two-way SMS/text-to-pay. No contracts, 5-minute setup.
