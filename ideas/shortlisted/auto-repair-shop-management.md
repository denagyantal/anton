# Auto Repair Shop Management

**Score: 85/105** | **Verdict: EXPLORE FURTHER** | **Evaluation Date: 2026-03-05**

## Concept
Modern, affordable shop management for 1-3 bay independent shops. Cloud-based, mobile/tablet-first. DVI included (not add-on). Customer texting. Integrated parts search. Profitability dashboard. $50-150/mo — filling the dead zone between ARI ($20) and Tekmetric ($179).

## Target Customer
Independent mechanics, 1-3 bay shops, mobile mechanics. Currently using paper work orders and whiteboards.
