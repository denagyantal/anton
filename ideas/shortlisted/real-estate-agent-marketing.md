# Real Estate Agent Marketing Automation

**Score: 75/105** | **Verdict: EXPLORE FURTHER** | **Evaluation Date: 2026-03-05**

## Concept
AI marketing kit generator for real estate agents. Input listing address → auto-pull MLS data and photos → generate branded flyer, social media carousel, email template, open house sign, and just-sold announcement in one click. Monthly content calendar with AI-suggested posts.

## Target Customer
New and independent real estate agents, small brokerages. 2M+ licensed agents; 100K+ new agents/year; 87% fail within 5 years creating perpetual new-customer demand.
