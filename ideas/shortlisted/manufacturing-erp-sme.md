## One-Line Pitch
Managed cloud ERP for 10-100 employee manufacturers — production orders, BOM, purchasing, inventory at $200-800/mo instead of Odoo's complexity or NetSuite's $30K+/yr.

## Problem
SME manufacturers (5-200 employees) have no modern, affordable, cloud-native ERP. SAP/Oracle require enterprise budgets. NetSuite starts at $30K+/yr. Odoo is bloated and painful to deploy. Legacy tools (IQMS, E2 Shop) are desktop-first and require on-site IT. The Carbon open-source ERP (Show HN: 322 points, 158 comments — top 5% engagement) demonstrates massive community validation that this gap is real and urgent. Comments specifically cited deployment complexity as the #1 challenge for self-hosted options — meaning there's a clear market for a managed SaaS version.

## Approach Options

**Option A (Fastest to market):** Managed SaaS on Carbon open-source core
- Fork/contribute to Carbon (https://github.com/crbnos/carbon)
- Add hosted/managed deployment, polished onboarding, and support
- Charge $200-500/mo vs. self-hosting complexity
- Target: 10-50 employee job shops and make-to-order manufacturers

**Option B (Niche-first):** Pick one manufacturing sub-vertical
- **Job shop / make-to-order** — most fragmented, spreadsheet-heavy
- **MRO / maintenance management** — adjacent to existing FSM knowledge
- **Food manufacturing** — traceability + compliance angle (FDA)
- Build deep vertical features (lot tracking, compliance reports) before going broad

**Recommended:** Option A (managed Carbon) + Option B (job shop niche-first) combined.

## Key Differentiators
1. **Managed SaaS** — zero deployment complexity (vs. Odoo self-hosted)

4. **White-glove onboarding** — "live in 5 days" positioning
