## One-Line Pitch
Replace spreadsheets in construction finance: track what's owed to subs, what's retained, when liens are due — for GCs and specialty subcontractors at $79–149/mo.

## Problem
Main contractors and subcontractors track subcontracts, payment applications, and retentions in spreadsheets. Construction payment cycles are notoriously slow (30–90 day terms with 5–10% retention held until project completion). Lien waiver deadlines are missed. Cash flow is damaged for subcontractors who can't see their total retention exposure across all active projects. Pay-when-paid clauses create cascading delays.

## Key Differentiators
- Visual dashboard: total outstanding retention across all active projects
- Automated lien waiver deadline alerts (state-specific)
- Payment application tracking (% complete, payment received vs expected)
- Import from QuickBooks/Excel (no forced migration)
- Subcontractor portal: subs can submit payment apps and view their retention balance

## Target Customer
- **Primary**: Electrical and mechanical subcontractors ($1M–$20M revenue, 5–50 active projects)
- **Secondary**: Small GCs (5–50 subcontractors) who need to track what they owe to subs

## MVP Feature Set
1. Payment application tracker (project → billing period → invoice amount → payment received → balance due)
2. Retention ledger (% retention withheld → cumulative balance → release date)
3. Lien waiver management (preliminary notices, conditional/unconditional waivers, state-specific deadlines)
4. QuickBooks import/sync
5. Simple subcontractor portal (view their own payment status)
