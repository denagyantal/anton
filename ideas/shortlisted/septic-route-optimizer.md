## One-Line Pitch
Route-optimized scheduling with permit-zone compliance, tank-size variability, and dump station tracking for septic pumping companies — the tool that already generates $51K/month.

## Problem
Septic tank service companies (pumping, inspection, installation) manually schedule routes using spreadsheets, Google Maps, and phone calls. No software accounts for the unique constraints of septic route planning: permit zone restrictions vary by county, tank sizes vary (250–5,000+ gallons) affecting pump times, dump station locations limit route radius, and state/county compliance records must be maintained for every pumped tank. One solo developer built a simple tool solving these exact problems and reached $51,000/month MRR with 340 paying customers at $150/month each — with zero marketing spend.

## Market Evidence
- **Proven revenue**: $51,000/month MRR ($612K ARR) — solo developer, zero marketing, built in <3 months
- **Paying customers**: 340 septic service companies at $150/month
- **Zero competition**: No purpose-built septic software exists at any price point; operators use spreadsheets + Google Maps
- **Market size**: ~28,000 septic pumping companies in the US; millions of septic systems serviced annually
- **Compliance mandate**: Every pumped septic tank requires a record filed with the county — operators must track this regardless; software that automates it is essential
- **Adjacent markets**: Junk removal, portable toilet rental, micro dumpster operators — same route optimization + compliance problem
