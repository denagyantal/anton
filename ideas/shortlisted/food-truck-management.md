## The Gap
**None of the 15 food truck tools reviewed combine**: (a) daily location/route scheduling with a public-facing "where are we today" map page, (b) event/catering booking with contracts and deposits, (c) permit expiration tracking with renewal reminders, and (d) event CRM for repeat organizers.

**Current workaround**: Google Calendar for routes + email/DMs for event bookings + spreadsheets for permit deadlines + Square/Toast for payments = 4–6 disconnected tools with no integration.

**Key insight**: The tool does NOT try to replace Square or Toast (POS). It is the ops layer on top. This dramatically reduces scope and build time.

## Product Concept: "TruckStack"

**Pricing**: $49/mo (single truck) — month-to-month
**LTD**: $149 (lifetime, single truck)

**Core features**:
1. Public location schedule page with map embed (customers check where the truck is today)
2. Catering/event booking portal with deposit collection (contracts + e-signature)
3. Permit expiration tracker with 30/60/90-day renewal reminders (health dept, parking, city vendor licenses)
4. Event CRM for repeat organizers (contact history, past event details, preferred menu)
5. Basic route planning for multi-location days (lunch spots + dinner location)

**Key differentiator**: Permit tracker alone is a compelling hook — no competitor has it. Operators face fines and permit revocations from missing renewal deadlines. The public location page is daily-use for customer communication.
