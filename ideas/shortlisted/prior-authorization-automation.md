## One-Line Pitch
AI-powered prior authorization automation for specialty medical practices — starting with mental health (160K+ therapists) and expanding to chiropractic, OT, and other specialties where PA is a weekly burden with no affordable solution.

## Market
- Prior authorization costs the US healthcare system $35B+ annually in administrative waste
- Physical therapy vertical already proven: one solo founder hit $41K MRR with a PT-specific PA automation tool — and no notable competitor has emerged
- Mental health: 160K+ therapists in the US; billing is notoriously fragmented across payers; prior auth for behavioral health is complex and specialty-specific
- This opportunity repeats across every specialty: chiropractic, occupational therapy, dermatology, orthopedics — each has payer-specific requirements creating a moat for specialty-specific tools
- Availity (enterprise incumbent) is inaccessible to solo practices ($500K+ implementation)

## Core Problem
Solo and small specialty practices (under 10 clinicians) spend 10-20 hours/week on prior authorization:
- Each payer has different forms, portals, and requirements
- Status tracking is manual — someone has to call the insurance company
- Denials require appeals documentation — more manual work
- Staff spend hours chasing auth numbers that should arrive in minutes

The economics are brutal: one FTE medical admin coordinator costs $45-55K/year just to handle PA. A $99-199/month tool that automates 60-70% of the work pays for itself in weeks.

## Key Differentiators
1. **Specialty-specific forms** — payer-specific PA forms for each specialty (mental health, chiropractic, etc.) pre-loaded and auto-populated from clinical notes
2. **Status tracking dashboard** — see all pending PAs in one place, color-coded by urgency/expiry
3. **Appeal letter generation** — AI generates evidence-based appeal letters when PA is denied
4. **Payer portal automation** — automates form submission to major payer portals (UnitedHealthcare, Aetna, Cigna) where API access is available
5. **HIPAA-compliant** — built on HIPAA-compliant infrastructure from day 1

## Target Market: Mental Health First

- SimplePractice's 2025 price hike created a migration moment — therapists actively seeking alternatives
