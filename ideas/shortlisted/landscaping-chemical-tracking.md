## Summary
Compliance-first mobile app for landscaping chemical tracking. Auto-capture weather data at time of application, GPS-stamp location, photo documentation, generate client notification emails, produce compliance reports for inspections. $49-79/mo or $79-99 LTD.

## Market Data
- **Target**: 600K+ landscaping businesses. EPA/state regulations mandate chemical tracking
- **Key**: Paper logs don't meet regulatory compliance. Digital logs with auto-weather/GPS clearly superior
- **Note**: Better as module within Landscaping OS than standalone product

## Risks
- Very narrow niche — may be too small standalone
- Better as feature of broader landscaping tool
- Regulatory requirements vary by state
