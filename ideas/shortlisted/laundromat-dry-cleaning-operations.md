# Laundromat & Dry Cleaning Operations Platform

**Score: 78/105** | **Verdict: EXPLORE FURTHER** | **Evaluation Date: 2026-03-05**

## Concept
Affordable all-in-one for laundromat and dry cleaning operators. POS with weight-based pricing, customer loyalty tracking, pickup/delivery scheduling with driver app, machine maintenance alerts, multi-location dashboard, basic accounting. Priced at $49-79/store/month — half the market leaders.

## Target Customer
Independent laundromat owners, dry cleaners, wash-and-fold services. 35K+ laundromats in US. Average revenue $150-300K/year. Industry early in digital adoption.
