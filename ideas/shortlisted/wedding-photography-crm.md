## One-Line Pitch
Photography-native CRM built specifically for the wedding photography workflow — multi-session timelines, questionnaire-to-shot-list automation, and vendor coordination — at $99 LTD for solo photographers burned by HoneyBook's price hike and Táve's VSCO acquisition.

## Problem
Two market disruptions in 2025–2026 created acute demand simultaneously:
1. **HoneyBook raised prices** in early 2025, triggering mass churn from long-time users. HoneyBook is also US/Canada only, locking out international photographers.
2. **Táve was acquired by VSCO** in May 2025 and relaunched as VSCO Workspace in August 2025 — the dedicated Táve community (known for workflow depth) is uncertain about product direction and actively shopping alternatives.

Wedding photography has very specific unmet CRM workflows that generic tools (Dubsado, HoneyBook) only partially serve:
- **Multi-session booking flows** — engagement shoot → wedding day → album delivery → gallery reveal are all one client/project; most CRMs track single-session clients
- **Gallery delivery integration** — Pic-Time, ShootProof, Pixieset links need to be triggered and tracked within the CRM
- **Second-shooter + vendor coordination** — timeline documents shared with vendors without giving them CRM access
- **Questionnaire-to-timeline builder** — client answers auto-populate shot list and day-of timeline

## Market Evidence
- 90,000+ wedding photographers in the US
- Average wedding photographer books 20–40 weddings/year at $2K–$8K each
- HoneyBook price increase 2025 = documented community-wide switching event
- Táve/VSCO Workspace = second simultaneous displacement event (rare dual trigger)
- Studio Ninja ($12–$17/mo) captures simple end; Dubsado ($20–$40/mo) and Sprout Studio ($24–$59/mo) serve mid-market but without photography-native automation
- Active communities: r/WeddingPhotography, photographer FB groups, The Photo Managers, SLR Lounge forums

3. **Vendor coordination PDF** — shareable without requiring vendors to create accounts
4. **Gallery delivery tracker** — integrated with Pic-Time, ShootProof, Pixieset links
5. **$99 LTD** — positioned directly against HoneyBook's $39–$79/mo (3–6 month payback)
