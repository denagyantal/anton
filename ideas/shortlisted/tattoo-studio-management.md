## One-Line Pitch
Purpose-built tattoo studio software: multi-step consultation → deposit → design → appointment booking with artist portfolio matching — replacing generic salon tools that butcher the tattoo workflow.

## Problem
Tattoo studios have a unique multi-step booking workflow (consultation → deposit collection → design approval → appointment scheduling) that generic salon tools (Booksy, Vagaro, Square) handle poorly. Current pain points:
- **Deposit handling**: Booksy and Vagaro have "horrible reviews from tattoo artists specifically around deposit handling" — no-show protection is broken, deposit refund disputes are common, deposit tracking across artists is manual
- **Artist portfolio matching**: No tool lets clients filter by artist style (geometric vs. traditional vs. watercolor) to self-select the right artist
- **Multi-step booking**: Single-appointment booking tools miss the consultation + design approval phase; studios manage this via text, DM, and phone
- **Consent forms**: Waiver/consent form collection happens on paper at the door; no digital pre-session collection exists in affordable tools
- **Design file management**: Custom design files are shared via email/Instagram DMs, with no organized per-client record

## Market Evidence
- 21,000+ tattoo studios in the US (average 3-8 artists per studio)
- Current tools: Booksy, Vagaro, Square — all generic salon tools with poor reviews from tattoo artists
- Ink Book (last major update 2021) is the only tattoo-specific tool; universally seen as outdated
- High-spending clientele: $200-$2,000 per session; studios motivated to reduce no-shows (deposits solve this)
- Multiple Indie Hackers sources (wearefounders.uk, entrepreneurloop.com) explicitly name this as a prime 2026 boring-business SaaS opportunity
- Cannabis dispensary software analogy: similar niche that found product-market fit with vertical-specific tooling

## Key Differentiators
1. **Auto deposit collection at booking** — eliminate no-show revenue loss
2. **Portfolio-matched booking** — clients filter by style (geometric, neo-traditional, watercolor)
3. **Design file management** — per-client design folders, no more Instagram DMs
4. **Digital consent + waiver collection** — before the appointment, not at the door
5. **Multi-step workflow** — consultation → design approval → appointment (not single-step)

## Potential Approach
Build for the tattoo artist pain, not the studio owner. Tattoo artists are the power users who will champion the tool — if they love it, the studio owner follows. Start with deposit automation (clearest ROI) and artist portfolio matching (clearest differentiator). Distribution: post in tattoo artist Facebook groups and Instagram communities; offer free 3-month trial to 10 artists in exchange for testimonials and feedback.
