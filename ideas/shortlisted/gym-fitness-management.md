# Gym / Fitness Studio Management - Score: 80/105

**Verdict**: EXPLORE FURTHER
**Tier**: 1 (Strong Opportunity)
**Evaluation Date**: 2026-03-19

## One-Line Pitch
Dead-simple management app for independent gyms and studios under 200 members — scheduling, billing, member management at $39/mo flat with no marketplace commissions.

## Problem
Mindbody dominates but real cost is $500-700+/mo with 20% marketplace commissions, 2.99%+ processing fees, and premium add-ons. 8-week onboarding is impossible for a gym owner who's also coaching classes. PushPress charges $97/mo extra for a branded app. Wodify costs escalate with add-ons. Independent gyms with <300 members allocate <10% of budget to IT. Many use Google Sheets + Venmo + WhatsApp because dedicated software is too expensive or complex.

## Market Evidence
- $2.2B gym management software market (10-14% CAGR)
- ~40,000 fitness studios + ~30,000 health clubs in US, majority independent
- 49% of independent fitness centers upgraded software 2021-2024
- 39% of small gyms spend <10% of operational budget on IT
- Mindbody: $139-$700+/mo per location + 20% marketplace commission
- PushPress: free basic but $159/mo Pro + $97/mo branded app
