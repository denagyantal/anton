# Landlord Property Management + Tax-Ready Accounting — Score: 90/105

**Verdict**: EXPLORE FURTHER

## One-Line Pitch
The only property management app that makes tax season a non-event — rent collection + maintenance + receipt scanning + auto-populated Schedule E in one flat $8/unit/mo tool for small landlords.

## Problem
17 million individual landlords file Schedule E rental income on their US tax returns (IRS data). The vast majority own 1–4 units and cobble together:
- A property management tool (TurboTenant, Innago, RentRedi) for rent collection and maintenance
- A spreadsheet or shoebox of receipts for expense tracking
- Their CPA manually translating everything into Schedule E each tax season

No tool combines both workflows affordably. Every property management platform either stops at rent collection OR does accounting, but none generate a print-ready Schedule E report. The result: small landlords either overpay their CPA for bookkeeping work or make errors on their tax returns.

**Competitor failures creating switching opportunity:**
- RentRedi: unauthorized Stripe access issues, unresponsive support
- Buildium: removed Vendoroo integration without notice; "adds vendors without user permission"
- TurboTenant: "feels simple and basic," no multi-platform listing

## Market Evidence
- 17M individual landlord filers in the US (IRS data)
- Majority own 1–4 units and use paper or spreadsheets for tax prep
- Multiple competing PM tools (Innago, Landlord Studio, Shuk Rentals) validate active digitalization of this market
- Landlord Studio's primary positioning around Schedule E reporting confirms this is a monetizable feature
- New entrants in 2025–2026 (Shuk Rentals, Innago updates) = growing demand signal

## Product Concept

**"LandlordLedger"** — Property management + tax-ready accounting for small landlords.

**Core features:**
- Rent collection via ACH (no fees — pass through Stripe verbatim)
- Maintenance request portal (tenant submits → landlord approves → tracks to resolution)
- Tenant communication log
- Receipt capture with ML expense categorization (snap photo → auto-categorized by IRS expense type)
- **Auto-populated Schedule E** — the killer feature: every income and expense entry already tagged to the right Schedule E line; generates a print-ready PDF + CPA export
- Lease storage and e-signing

**Pricing:**
- $8/unit/mo — all features, no add-ons
- LTD: $79 (up to 5 units) / $149 (up to 20 units)

## Key Differentiators
1. **First PM app to generate a print-ready Schedule E** — "your accountant will thank you"
2. **No hidden fees** — ACH rent collection at standard Stripe rates, no markup, no per-transaction fees
3. **Complete workflow**: rent collection → expense tracking → Schedule E — eliminates the shoebox + spreadsheet + CPA dual-cost problem
