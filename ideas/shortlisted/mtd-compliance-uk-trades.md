# MTD Compliance Software for UK Trades

## Score: 76/105

## Verdict: EXPLORE FURTHER — TIME-SENSITIVE

## Overview
Making Tax Digital (MTD) compliance tool designed specifically for UK tradespeople — plumbers, electricians, builders, cleaners. Translates job/work language into HMRC-compliant quarterly submissions. Speaks "trades" language (jobs, materials, callouts) instead of "accounting" language (debits, credits, journals).

## Pain Point
The UK's Making Tax Digital for Income Tax (MTD ITSA) goes live April 6, 2026 — weeks away. ~780,000 sole traders earning £50K+ must now maintain digital records and submit quarterly income/expense summaries via HMRC-approved software. Another 970,000 join April 2027 (£30K threshold). All existing MTD-compatible tools (Xero, Sage, FreeAgent) are accounting software first — tradespeople don't think in "debits and credits," they think in "jobs completed and parts used." No one has built "MTD compliance for plumbers."

## Target Market
- UK sole traders earning £50K+ (780K in April 2026)
- UK sole traders earning £30K+ (additional 970K in April 2027)
- Plumbers, electricians, builders, cleaners, landscapers
- Any self-employed UK tradesperson
