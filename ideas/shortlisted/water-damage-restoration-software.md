## Product Concept

**"DryOps"** — restoration job management for small teams.

**Core MVP (8–10 weeks):**
1. **Job intake**: Create job → customer/property info → damage type (water/fire/mold/smoke) → assign technician → insurance adjuster contact
2. **Moisture log**: Digital moisture readings log per room per day → auto-formats to IICRC S500 standard → daily drying report PDF for insurance
3. **Photo documentation**: Tagged before/during/after photos per room → organized by job → exportable for adjuster packet
4. **Equipment tracking**: Log equipment placed (dehumidifiers, air movers, thermal cameras) by room → log pickup → calculate rental period
5. **Work authorization + customer sign-off**: Digital work authorization form → customer signs on tablet → stored with job file
6. **Xactimate-ready documentation**: Structured damage documentation formatted for Xactimate import (measurements, materials, scope items) — reduces estimator time
7. **Invoicing**: Job completion → invoice with documented scope → QuickBooks sync

**Phase 2 (MRR upsells):**
- XactAnalysis integration (preferred vendor portal)
- Mold remediation clearance documentation
- Certificate of satisfaction workflow
- Multi-technician job assignment and status updates

**Pricing:**
- $99/month — 1 user/technician, unlimited jobs
- $179/month — up to 5 users (team)
- LTD: $99 (solo) / $179 (team, up to 5 users) — viable AppSumo campaign
- Month-to-month only; no setup fee; no annual contract required

## Key Differentiators

1. **No setup fee, no annual contract** — Albi charges $3,500/year; PSA and Xcelerate charge $1,500 just to start; DryOps starts on the first day for free
2. **IICRC S500-standard moisture logs** — digital drying logs formatted to the industry standard; adjuster accepts them without conversion
3. **Built for the small operator** — not insurance companies, not enterprise restorers; features match what a 2-person company actually uses
4. **Xactimate-ready documentation** — structured scope export reduces time spent re-entering job details into Xactimate Online
