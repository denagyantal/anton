# Landscaping & Lawn Care All-in-One

**Score: 91/105** | **Verdict: BUILD** | **Evaluation Date: 2026-03-05**

## Concept
All-in-one platform for lawn care and landscaping businesses. Seasonal scheduling (spring ramp-up, winter wind-down), route optimization for multiple daily stops, property measurement for quoting (satellite imagery), chemical application tracking for compliance, weather-aware scheduling, and invoicing. Mobile-first for operators in the field.

## Target Customer
Lawn care operators, landscaping companies, and related outdoor services (irrigation, snow removal, tree service) with 1-10 crew members. Currently using Google Calendar, paper estimates, and separate invoicing.
