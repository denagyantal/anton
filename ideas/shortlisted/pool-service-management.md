**Tier**: 1 (Strong Opportunity)

## One-Line Pitch
Flat-rate pool service management with integrated water chemistry tracking and route optimization — replacing Skimmer's per-customer pricing that punishes growth.

## Problem
Small pool service operators (1-5 trucks) serving 50-300 residential customers are trapped between Skimmer's per-customer pricing ($0.50/customer/mo — 200 customers = $100/mo, 500 = $250/mo) and generic field service platforms (ServiceTitan is a "bloated enterprise app overloaded with menus" that's a "confusing, buggy, nightmare to use" for pool companies). Generic FSM tools (Jobber, Housecall Pro) handle scheduling but lack pool-specific features: water chemistry testing logs (pH, chlorine, alkalinity, CYA), chemical dosage records, equipment lifecycle tracking, and pool-specific compliance. Pool pros are forced to use separate apps or paper logs for these critical needs.

## Market Evidence
- $6B+ US pool service industry, tens of thousands of small operators
- Skimmer: 35K+ pool pros, dominant but per-customer pricing criticized
- ServiceTitan: 50% pricing satisfaction — 50%+ of users unhappy; "confusing, buggy" for pool companies
- Pool Office Manager: ~$30-75/mo — smaller alternative exists, proving demand for simpler tools
- Many operators still use paper route sheets, separate water testing apps (Pool Shark H2O), and QuickBooks for invoicing

## Key Differentiators
1. $49/mo flat — unlimited customers, no per-customer fees, all features included
2. Integrated water chemistry tracking (pH, chlorine, alkalinity, CYA) with trend analysis
3. Equipment lifecycle management (pump age, filter condition, heater maintenance) — drives upselling
4. Mobile-first — techs complete routes, log chemistry, and process payments from phone
5. No contracts — month-to-month
