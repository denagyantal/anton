## One-Line Pitch
Simple digital safety training log and crew certification tracker for landscaping, lawn care, and construction crews — built to satisfy OSHA audit requirements at $29/month or $149 LTD for companies with 1–20 crew members.

## Problem
Multiple new compliance requirements are forcing boring businesses to adopt software they'd otherwise ignore:
- **OSHA is proposing digitized crew safety training logs** for landscaping and construction — currently paper-based at most operators
- **FMCSA removed 26 non-compliant ELD devices** in Q1–Q2 2026, forcing trucking migration — similar mandate pattern
- **New contractor licensing rules** in several states require digital proof of insurance and safety certifications on-demand

Each compliance event is a customer acquisition forcing function — operators who ignored software previously are now forced to buy something. The current workarounds (paper training logs, PDF certificates in email, binders in the truck) create audit exposure.

No affordable trade-specific compliance logging tool exists below $49/month. SafetyCulture/iAuditor is generic ($19–$99/user/month). Procore serves large construction ($500+/month). A simple digital training log at $29/month or $149 LTD would be the easiest buy decision in this portfolio — operators buy it once to avoid OSHA fines.

## Core Features (MVP)
1. **Crew safety training log** — who attended which training, when, e-signature
2. **Digital certificate storage** — upload/track expiry of safety certifications (OSHA 10, First Aid, etc.)
3. **Safety attestation** — crew member signs off on daily safety briefing via phone
4. **Incident report** — photo + description + timestamp
5. **OSHA-ready export** — print or export compliance report for audit
