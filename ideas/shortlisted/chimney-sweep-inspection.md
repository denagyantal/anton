## One-Line Pitch
Purpose-built scheduling + CRM + photo documentation for chimney sweep companies — the niche that already generates $52.5K/month with zero competitors.

## Problem
Chimney sweep service companies (cleaning, inspection, repair) have zero purpose-built software. They manage appointments in paper calendars, record inspection findings on hand-written forms, and track customers in Excel. When a customer calls asking when they last had their chimney cleaned, the owner digs through filing cabinets. There is no digital record of chimney conditions, no before/after photo documentation tied to the customer record, and no automated follow-up reminder for annual cleaning. One founder (whose father was a chimney sweep) built a simple tool solving these problems and reached $52,500/month MRR with 420 paying customers at $125/month each.

## Market Evidence
- **Proven revenue**: $52,500/month MRR ($630K ARR) — insider founder, presumably built within 6-12 months
- **Paying customers**: 420 chimney sweep companies at $125/month
- **Zero competition**: No purpose-built chimney sweep software exists; operators use paper + Excel
- **Market size**: ~20,000 chimney sweep companies in the US (Chimney Safety Institute of America estimate); market growing with fireplace/wood stove adoption
- **NFPA 211 compliance**: Every level 2 inspection requires documented findings — software that auto-generates compliant inspection reports is mandatory for insurance + liability
- **Adjacent expansion**: Dryer vent cleaning, radon testing, mold inspection, air duct cleaning — all same scheduling + photo doc + compliance pattern; combined market 3-5x the chimney segment

5. Camera inspection video upload and storage (linked to customer record)
6. Expansion: same platform for dryer vent, radon, mold inspection trades
