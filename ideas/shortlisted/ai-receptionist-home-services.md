- [x] Problem is real (60-80% of calls unanswered)
- [⚠️] Build without deep domain expertise — voice AI is complex
- [x] Not dominated by single player
- [x] Revenue potential > $10K MRR (strong — $99-199/mo per business)

## Key Differentiators
1. Trade-specific call handling (HVAC emergency routing, plumbing dispatching)
2. Affordable ($99-199/mo vs $230-500/mo for Smith.ai/Ruby)
3. Bilingual (English/Spanish) — critical for trades
4. Integration with popular FSM tools

## Next Steps
1. Test voice AI quality with Twilio/Vapi for basic call handling
2. Validate per-call pricing model ($1-2/call vs $99-199/mo flat)
3. Determine minimum viable call handling scope
4. Consider as premium add-on module for FSM Lite product

## Risks
1. Voice AI quality not yet reliable enough — one bad call loses a customer
2. Per-call costs make LTD model nearly impossible
3. Well-funded competitors iterating quickly
4. Better as an add-on to FSM Lite than standalone product
