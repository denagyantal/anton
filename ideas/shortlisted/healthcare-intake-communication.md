# Healthcare Practice Digital Intake & Communication

**Score: 79/105** | **Verdict: EXPLORE FURTHER** | **Priority: #21**

## Concept
Affordable ($49-99/mo) patient communication for solo/small practices. Automated text reminders, digital intake forms with EHR integration, 2-way texting, review requests after visits, and recall campaigns. The "Mailchimp for healthcare practices."
