## One-Line Pitch
Dumpster availability calendar + booking page + dispatch + automated invoicing for micro waste haulers (1-5 roll-offs) who are too small for every existing tool.

## Problem
Small waste hauling and dumpster rental operators (1-5 roll-off containers) manage their business entirely through phone calls, spreadsheets, and hand-written route sheets. When a customer calls asking if a 20-yard dumpster is available next Tuesday, the owner looks at a paper calendar. Existing software (Docket, Hauler Hero, AMCS) targets mid-size haulers (5+ trucks) and prices accordingly — $300-500+/month. A micro-hauler running 3 roll-offs and doing $250K/year in revenue cannot justify this. The gap: a simple dumpster availability calendar with customer booking page, driver dispatch, and automated invoicing at $49/month or $299 one-time.

## Market Evidence
- **Docket**: 4.9 stars / 84 Google reviews — proves small haulers pay for specialized software
- **Multiple competitors** (Hauler Hero, AMCS, WasteExpert, Soft-Pak) all target 5+ trucks
- **Market size**: ~12,000 dumpster rental companies in the US; majority are small operators
- **Adjacent market**: Junk removal companies (1-800-GOT-JUNK franchisees, independent ops) have identical dispatch problem
- **Similar validated niche**: Septic route optimizer = $51K/mo; comparable pattern for dumpster rental

## Key Differentiators
1. Dumpster availability calendar (not just generic schedule) — shows which containers are deployed, when they return, and when they're available for rebooking
2. Customer self-service booking page (customers book dumpster without calling)
3. Automatic swap reminders (SMS to driver + customer when rental period ends)
4. Per-weight invoicing option (scale receipt upload → auto-calculate surcharge)
5. Price at $49/month or $299 LTD — all-in vs. $300-500+/month for Docket/Hauler Hero

## Target Customer
- Dumpster rental companies: 1-5 roll-off containers
