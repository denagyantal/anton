# AI Local SEO & Lead Generation for Trades

## Score: 75/105

## Verdict: EXPLORE FURTHER

## Overview
Tool that helps trades businesses optimize for AI-powered search (Google AI Mode, Siri, Alexa) and generate leads through automated content. Audits how AI assistants describe/recommend their business, generates structured data markup, manages reviews across platforms, and creates AI-optimized content.

## Pain Point
Google's AI Mode is fundamentally changing how homeowners find plumbers, electricians, and HVAC companies. Instead of browsing 10 blue links, AI generates a summary and recommends specific contractors by name. Siri, Alexa, and other AI assistants each recommend different contractors based on different signals. Traditional SEO is being disrupted for local trades businesses. Most haven't adapted their marketing to AI search. Meanwhile, "boring businesses" are outperforming flashy brands on Instagram with authentic behind-the-scenes content.

## Target Market
- All local service trades — plumbers, HVAC, electricians, roofers, landscapers, cleaners
- Businesses with 1-20 employees wanting more leads
- Trades businesses with existing websites but poor AI/voice search visibility
