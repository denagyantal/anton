# Solo Therapist Practice Billing & Notes

**Score: 81/105** | **Verdict: EXPLORE FURTHER** | **Priority: #15**

## Concept
Ultra-simple HIPAA-compliant platform for solo therapists: session note templates (SOAP, DAP, BIRP), one-click insurance claim submission, automated appointment reminders, integrated telehealth, client self-scheduling. Designed to take <5 minutes of admin per session.

## Key Differentiators
1. HIPAA compliant from day 1 (moat)
2. Under 5 minutes of admin per session
3. Note templates specific to therapy (SOAP, DAP, BIRP)
4. Solo-focused — not bloated enterprise features
