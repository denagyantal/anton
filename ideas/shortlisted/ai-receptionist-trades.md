## One-Line Pitch
Hyper-narrow AI receptionist for solo mechanics and trade shops — answers after-hours and overflow calls, captures name/issue/contact, books to a calendar slot, and texts the owner a summary — at $49/month flat with zero quoting or CRM complexity.

## Problem
Solo mechanics and trades owners miss 30–40% of incoming calls during peak hours. Every missed call is a lost $200–$800 job. The HN thread (320 points, 319 comments) reveals that current AI receptionist implementations (Avoca AI, AgentZap, Allo) are over-built: they can't accurately quote repairs, don't know parts availability, and fail on non-standard jobs — making them unreliable for the complex use cases they promise.

What AI CAN reliably do: (a) notify customers their car is ready, (b) give standard progress updates, (c) capture lead info when humans are unavailable, (d) book appointment slots.

The gap: no one owns the narrow "missed-call capture + appointment scheduling ONLY, no quoting" niche for solo/2-person shops at $39–49/month. Funded players (Avoca $1B, AgentZap, Allo) are building complex all-in-one platforms at $59–$199+/month — leaving the simple "just answer my phone and text me who called" use case unserved at the price point solo operators can afford.

## Market Evidence
- HN thread 320 points, 319 comments (March 2026) = largest engaged discussion on AI for auto repair
- Avoca AI: raised $125M Series B at $1B valuation — serves 800+ contractors booking $1B in jobs/year (enterprise tier)
- AgentZap: ~$59/mo entry; Goodcall $59/month for basic voice; Allo similar

- 200,000+ auto repair shops in US; 750,000+ HVAC/plumbing/electrical contractors — massive TAM

## Key Differentiators
1. **Narrow scope = reliability** — don't try to quote repairs; just capture lead and book appointment slot
2. **Zero setup friction** — forward your number, set your calendar, done in 10 minutes
3. **$49/month flat** vs $59–199+/month complex competitors
4. **Owner gets SMS summary in 30 seconds** — name, problem, contact, appointment booked
5. **No CRM, no quoting, no parts lookup** — just the one thing AI does reliably

## Pricing
- $49/month: Basic (call capture + SMS summary)
- $79/month: Pro (call capture + calendar booking + follow-up SMS to caller)
- $349/year: Annual (same as Pro, 25% discount)

## Next Steps
1. Build on Twilio/Bland AI: call capture → LLM transcript → appointment booking → SMS to owner
2. Integrate with Google Calendar for available slot detection
3. Target solo mechanics first (tightest community, most acute problem)
4. Pricing: start at $49/month flat — no annual contract
5. Key message: "Answer every call, even when you can't — never lose a job to voicemail"

## Risks
