## One-Line Pitch

"ZenMaid, but for pool cleaning" — purpose-built route scheduling, chemical treatment log, and recurring invoicing for the ~20,000 pool cleaning companies that have no dedicated software.

## Problem

ZenMaid ($3M/yr bootstrapped, proving the single-vertical SaaS playbook) built a $250K/month business specifically for maid/cleaning services. The exact same operational problems exist in adjacent verticals — and pool cleaning has the same profile with zero ZenMaid equivalent:

- Weekly recurring routes (same pools, same schedule, every week)
- Chemical dosage tracking per pool visit (pH, chlorine, alkalinity) — legally required in many states for water safety compliance
- Customer invoicing after each visit with service notes
- Route optimization (pools clustered by neighborhood for efficiency)
- Customer portal to view service history and water chemistry readings

Existing tools (Jobber, Housecall Pro, GorillaDesk) are too generic and too expensive for pool cleaning operators. Skimmer exists at $149+/mo targeting mid-market pools. The $29-79/mo slot for 1-5 truck pool operators is wide open.

## Product Concept

**Core MVP (3-4 week build):**

1. **Route Builder** — add pools to a weekly recurring route; drag-drop stop ordering; map view
2. **Chemical Treatment Log** — pH, chlorine, alkalinity, alkalinity, phosphates logged per visit; compliance PDF export per pool per month
3. **Auto-Invoicing** — auto-generate invoice on visit completion; send via email/SMS; card on file for auto-charge
4. **Client Portal** — customers view their pool's service history, water chemistry readings, and past invoices (no login required — magic link)
5. **Mobile App** — technician route view; tap to log chemicals + notes + before/after photo; GPS clock-in/out per stop

**Phase 2 (MRR upsells):**
- Route optimization (auto-sequence stops by map clustering)
- Chemical inventory tracking + auto-reorder alerts
- Multi-tech route assignment for growing companies
- Service proposal templates (one-time cleans, equipment repairs)

**Pricing:**
- $29/mo — 1 tech, up to 75 pools
- $49/mo — unlimited pools, 1 tech
- $79/mo — unlimited pools, up to 3 techs
- LTD: $79 (1 tech) / $129 (up to 3 techs)

## Key Differentiators

1. **Chemical compliance log** — per-pool visit log with PDF export for state inspectors (critical differentiator vs. generic FSM tools that ignore this)
2. **Recurring-route-first** — designed for weekly recurring schedules, not generic dispatch (same differentiation ZenMaid made vs. generic scheduling tools)
3. **Client chemistry portal** — pool owners can see their water chemistry history (builds trust + reduces "did you actually come?" calls)
4. **Zero setup time** — pre-configured for pool cleaning from day 1 (vs. Jobber needing custom configuration)
