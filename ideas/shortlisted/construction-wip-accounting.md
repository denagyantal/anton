# Construction WIP Accounting Calculator

**Score**: 89/105
**Verdict**: BUILD
**Tier**: 1 (Strong Opportunity)
**Evaluation Date**: 2026-04-20

## One-Line Pitch
QBO plugin that auto-generates the construction WIP schedule from job cost data — saving construction accountants 8-12 hours per client per month.

## Problem
Construction accountants using QuickBooks Online spend 8-12 hours per client per month manually calculating work-in-progress (WIP) schedules — a legally required revenue recognition method for contractors using percentage-of-completion accounting. No major accounting software automates this. Accountants pull data from multiple job costing sources, apply overbilling/underbilling formulas manually, and rebuild the schedule from scratch each month. Contractors over $1M revenue need WIP for bonding/banking compliance.

Pain score: 90/100 from Reddit-validated opportunity analysis.

## Market Evidence
- 700,000+ construction firms in the US
- Every contractor over $1M revenue needs WIP for bonding/banking
- Saving 8-12 hours/client/month = concrete, quantifiable ROI justification
- No affordable QBO-native WIP automation exists — only Sage 300 CRE (expensive, complex) and Foundation Software (enterprise)
- Construction-specialized CPAs are a tight-knit community who share tool recommendations heavily
