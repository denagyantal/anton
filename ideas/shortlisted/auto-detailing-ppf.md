# Auto Detailing / PPF / Tint Shop All-in-One

**Score**: 86/105
**Verdict**: BUILD

## One-Line Pitch
Vehicle-first CRM for auto detailing, PPF, and ceramic coating shops — VIN + warranty tracking + before/after photos at $39/mo.

## Market
- 50K+ dedicated auto detailing, PPF, and tint shops in the US
- Ceramic coating global market $12B; PPF market growing rapidly
- r/AutoDetailing 2M+ members = massive engaged community
- Urable at $75–200+/mo serves the segment but complex/expensive

## Key Differentiators
- Vehicle-first data model: VIN + service history + active warranty per vehicle
- Automated warranty reminder emails to customers (ceramic coating, PPF)
- Before/after photo gallery per vehicle (shareable, marketing-ready)
- Online booking with deposit collection
- No per-user fees; flat $39/mo for 1–3 bay shops

## Target Channels
- r/AutoDetailing (2M+ members)
- YouTube detailing community (massive influencer ecosystem)
- Facebook "Auto Detailing Business Owners" group
- AppSumo (detailers are tech-savvy AppSumo buyers)

## Top 3 Risks
1. Urable is well-regarded and actively improving its feature set
2. Niche market ceiling vs. broader FSM opportunity
3. Chemical/PPF inventory complexity may increase build time
