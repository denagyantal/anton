## One-Line Pitch
Daycare billing + government subsidy (CCAP/voucher) tracking for under-50-child centers at $39/mo — the affordable Brightwheel alternative that actually handles subsidy billing.

## Problem
500K+ licensed childcare providers in the US use Brightwheel ($95–201/mo) or Procare ($150–300/mo) for billing and parent communication. For small centers (under 50 children), these tools are overbuilt and expensive. More critically, 40%+ of daycare revenue comes from government subsidies (CCAP — Child Care Assistance Program, state voucher programs) — and neither Brightwheel nor Procare handles subsidy billing workflow cleanly. Operators must manually track voucher amounts, reconcile against actual attendance, and submit paper or portal-based reimbursement claims. No affordable tool exists below $50/mo that handles both parent billing and subsidy reconciliation.

## Market Evidence

## Key Differentiators
1. CCAP/voucher billing workflow: track subsidy authorizations per child, generate attendance-based reimbursement reports
2. Flat $39/mo for up to 50 children — no per-child fees (Brightwheel charges per-child)
3. Parent-facing daily reports (meals, naps, activities) via simple mobile share link
4. Waitlist management with auto-notification when a spot opens
5. Compliance calendar: state licensing renewal reminders, staff training hour tracking
