# Idea Decision Tracker

Track the status of shortlisted ideas. Move ideas through the pipeline as you validate and build.

## Statuses
- **NEW** — Just shortlisted, not yet reviewed by team
- **VALIDATING** — Actively researching (customer interviews, competitor deep-dives)
- **BUILDING** — MVP in progress
- **PARKED** — Good idea, not pursuing now (with reason)
- **REJECTED** — Reviewed and passed (with reason)
